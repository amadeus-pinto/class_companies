Reed&#x27;s, Inc. engages in the development, marketing, and sale of natural non-alcoholic carbonated sodas and natural foods. Its product lines include Reed&#x27;s Ginger Brews, Virgil&#x27;s Root Beer, Cream Sodas, Dr. Better and Real Cola, Culture Club Kombucha, China Colas, Reed&#x27;s Ginger candy and ice creams, and Sonoma Sparkler and other juice based products. The company was founded by Christopher J. Reed in June 1987 and is headquartered in Los Angeles, CA.
