WidePoint Corp. provides information technology based products, services and solutions. It provides secure, cloud-based, enterprise-wide IT based solutions that enable commercial markets and federal and state government organizations to deploy fully compliant IT services in accordance with government-mandated regulations and advanced system requirements. Its Managed Mobility Solutions offer a portfolio of IT based services and products with a set of streamlined mobile communications management, identity management and consulting solutions that provide customers with the ability to manage and protect their valuable communications assets and deploy compliant identity management solutions that provide secured virtual and physical access to restricted environments. WidePoint was founded on May 30, 1997 and is headquartered in Oakbrook McLean, VA.
