Transcat, Inc. provides calibration, repair, inspection, and compliance services. The company also distributes grade handheld test, measurement, and control instrumentation. It operates its business through two segments: Service and Distribution. The Service segment offers calibration, repair, inspection, analytical qualifications, preventative maintenance and other related services, which are processed through its proprietary asset management system, CalTrak. The Distribution segment markets, sells and rents national and proprietary brand instruments through direct catalog marketing, website and other web-based advertising, outbound sales and an inbound call center for customer&#x27;s test and measurement instrumentation needs. Transcat was founded in 1964 and is headquartered in Rochester, NY.
