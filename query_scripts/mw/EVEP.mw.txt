EV Energy Partners LP operates as a master limited partnership. Through its subsidiary, EnerVest, Ltd., it engages in the acquisition, development and production of oil and natural gas properties and all of the company operations are located in the United States. Its current properties are located in the Barnett Shale, the Appalachian Basin, the San Juan Basin, Michigan, Central Texas, the Mid-Continent areas in Oklahoma, Texas, Arkansas, Kansas and Louisiana, the Monroe Field in Northern Louisiana and the Permian Basin. The company was founded in April 2006 and is headquartered in Houston, TX.
