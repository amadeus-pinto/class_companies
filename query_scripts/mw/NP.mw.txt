Neenah Paper, Inc. is a global manufacturer of premium, performance-based papers and specialty products. It operates through two segments: Technical Products and Fine Paper and Packaging. The Technical Products segment operates as an international producer of transportation and other filter media, durable, saturated and coated substrates for industrial products backings and a variety of other end markets. The Fine Paper and Packaging segment supplies premium writing, text and cover papers, bright papers and specialty papers in North America. Its products are used in commercial printing and imaging applications for corporate identity packages, invitations, personal stationery and corporate annual reports, as well as, premium labels and luxury packaging. Neenah Paper was founded in April 2004 and is headquartered in Alpharetta, GA.
