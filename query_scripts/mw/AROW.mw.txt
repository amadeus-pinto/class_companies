Arrow Financial Corp. is a multi-bank holding company, which engages in the provision of  banking and insurance services across Northeastern New York, including online and mobile banking, wealth management, money market and deposit accounts, mortgage, and consumer &amp; commercial loans through its subsidiaries. It also provides other services, such as trust administration, retirement plan administration, advice to its proprietary mutual funds and insurance products. The company was founded on March 21, 1983 and is headquartered in Glens Falls, NY.
