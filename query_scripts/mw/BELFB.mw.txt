Bel Fuse, Inc. engages in the development, manufacture, and trade of products, which powers, protects, and connects electronic circuits. It operates through the following geographic segments: North America, Asia and Europe. Its products include magnetic, power transformers, modules, circuit protection, fiber optic connectors, and interconnect devices. The company was founded by Elliot Bernstein in 1949 and is headquartered in Jersey City, NJ.
