Skyline Corp. engages in the design, production, and marketing of manufactured housing, modular housing, and recreational vehicles. It operates through Housing segment and Recreational Vehicle segments. The Housing segment offers and existing site-built home, apartments, condominiums, and townhouses. The Recreational Vehicles segment include travel trailers, fifth wheels, and park models. The company was founded in 1951 and is headquartered in Elkhart, IN.
