Forrester Research, Inc. engages in the provision of research, data, and advisory services. It operates through the following segments:  Research, Product, and Project Consulting. The Research segment includes research personnel who are responsible for writing the research and performing the webinars and inquiries for the company&#x27;s Research and Connect products. The Product segment consists of product management organization that is responsible for product pricing and packaging and the launch of new products. The project Consulting segment comprises of consultants that deliver the firm&#x27;s project consulting services. Its products and services include Forrester Research, Forrester Connect, Forrester Data, Forrester Consulting, and Forrester Events. The company was founded by George F. Colony on July 7, 1983 and is headquartered in Cambridge, MA.
