Dermira, Inc. is a biopharmaceutical company that focuses on bringing differentiated products to dermatologists and patients. It also focuses on medical dermatology, a field that concentrates on therapeutic solutions to treat skin conditions, such as psoriasis, hyperhidrosis and acne. Dermira was founded by Thomas G. Wiggans, Eugene Andrew Bauer, Luis C. Pe&#xF1;a, and Christopher M. Griffith on August 18, 2010 and is headquartered in Menlo Park, CA.
