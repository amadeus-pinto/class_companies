EVERTEC, Inc. engages in the provision of transaction services. It deals with payment processing, merchant acquiring, and business process management. It operates its business through the following segments: Merchant Acquiring, net; Payment Processing; and Business Solutions. The Merchant Acquiring, net segment offers services to merchants of all sizes, enabling them to accept all types of electronic payments. The Payment Processing segment provides financial institutions complete payment-processing services, such as card-issuer and point of sale processing. The Business Solutions segment supplies financial institutions, corporations, and government agencies a broad range of critical services including core bank processing; cash processing; and technology outsourcing. The company was founded on April 1, 2004 and is headquartered in San Juan, Puerto Rico.
