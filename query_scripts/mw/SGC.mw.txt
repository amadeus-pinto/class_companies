Superior Uniform Group, Inc. engages in the manufacture and sale of a wide range of uniforms, corporate identity apparel, career apparel, and accessories for the medical and health fields. It operates its business through the following segments: Uniforms and Related Products; Remote Staffing Solutions; and Promotional Products. The Uniforms and Related Products segment comprises of the sale of uniforms and related items. The Remote Staffing Solutions segment sells staffing solutions. The Promotional Products segment offers promotional products and other branded merchandise. The company was founded in 1920 and is headquartered Seminole, FL.
