BofI Holding, Inc. operates as a holding company, which engages in the management of internet-based savings bank. It operates through the following segments: Mortgage, Home Equity, Warehouse &amp; Other, Multi-family Real Estate, Commercial Real Estate, Auto and RV, Factoring, Commercial &amp; Industrial, and Other. The company was founded on July 6, 1999 and is headquartered in San Diego, CA.
