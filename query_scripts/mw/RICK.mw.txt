RCI Hospitality Holdings, Inc. owns and operates adult nightclubs, sports bars/restaurants and media divisions. It operates through the Nightclubs and Bombhells Restaurants and Bars segments. Its brands Include Rick&#x27;s Cabaret, Vivid Cabaret, Tootsie&#x27;s Cabaret, Club Onyx, Jaguars Club, XTC Cabaret and Bombshells. The company was founded by Robert L. Watters in 1983 and is headquartered in Houston, TX.
