Ferro Corp. engages in the business of manufacturing specialty materials. Its products include frits, porcelain, and other glass enamels, glazes, stains, decorating colors, pigments, inks, polishing materials, specialty dielectrics, electronic glasses, and other specialty coatings. It operates through the following segments: performance coatings; performance colors and glass; pigments, powders and oxides. The performance coatings segment refers to tile and porcelain enamel which offers products such as glazes, digital inks and digital glaze coatings for use in a variety of ceramic tile applications. The performance colors and glass segment offers color coatings and enamels for global glass industry such as automotive glass, flat glass, container glass, and a range of industrial products applications. The pigments, powders and oxides segment covers paint, plastics ceramics, and concrete applications, precious metal powders for conductive pastes and inks for a wide range of electronic applications, and polishing materials for ophthalmic lenses, auto finishes and display panels. The company was founded by Harry D. Cushman in 1919 and is headquartered in Mayfield Heights, OH.
