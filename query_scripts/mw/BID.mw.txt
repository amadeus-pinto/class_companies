Sotheby&#x27;s is a global art business company. It operates through two segments: Agency and Finance. The Agency segment matches buyers and sellers of authenticated fine art, decorative art, and jewelry through the auction or private sale process. The Finance segment conducts art-related financing activities by providing certain collectors and dealers with loans secured by works of art. It also involves in brand licensing activities. The company was founded by Samuel Baker in 1744 and is headquartered in New York, NY.
