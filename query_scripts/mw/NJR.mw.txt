New Jersey Resources Corp. is a holding company, which engages in the distribution of natural gas through a regulated utility, which provides other retail and wholesale energy services to customers and invests in clean energy projects and midstream assets. It operates through the following segments: Natural Gas Distribution, Clean Energy Ventures, and Energy Services and Midstream. The Natural Gas Distribution segment is consists of regulated natural gas services, off-system sales, capacity and storage management operations. The Energy Services segment is comprised of unregulated wholesale energy operations. The Clean Energy Ventures segment is the capital investments in clean energy projects. The Midstream segment consists of investments in the midstream natural gas market, such as natural gas transportation and storage facilities. The company was founded in 1981 and is headquartered in Wall, NJ.
