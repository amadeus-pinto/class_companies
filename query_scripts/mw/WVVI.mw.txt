Willamette Valley Vineyards, Inc. produces and sells premium, super premium and ultra premium varietal wines. Its product brands include Willamette Valley Pinot Noir, Whole Cluster Pinot Noir, Dijon Clone, Pinot Gris, Riesling, Founders Reserve Pinot Noir, Signature Cuvee Pinot Noir, Estate Vineyard Pinot Noir And Estate Vineyard Chardonnay. The company operates through the following segments: Direct Sales and Distributor Sales. The Direct Sales segment includes retail sales in its tasting room and remote sites, wine club sales, on-site events, kitchen and catering sales and other sales made directly to the consumer without the use of an intermediary. The Distributor Sales segment include all sales through a third party where prices are given at a wholesale rate. Willamette Valley Vineyards was founded by Oregon winegrower James W. Bernau in May 1988 and is headquartered in Turner, OR.
