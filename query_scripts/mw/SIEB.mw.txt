Siebert Financial Corp. operates as a bank holding company. It operates, through its subsidiary, Muriel Siebert &amp; Co., Inc., conducts retail discount brokerage and investment banking business. Its offerings include trading, fund exchange, competitive money funds, active traders, dynamic monitor, extended hours trading, research choices, news and account alerts, online bill payment, planning &amp; education, wireless, touchtone and newsletter. It operates through two divisions: Retail and Capital Markets Group. The Retail division includes discount brokerage and related services, independent retail execution services, retail customer service, retirement accounts, customer financing and information and communications systems. The Capital Markets Group division serves as a co-manager, underwriting syndicate member and selling group member on a wide spectrum of securities offerings for corporations and Federal agencies. This Division also provides investment banking and institutional equity execution services. Siebert Financial was founded on April 24, 1996 and is headquartered in New York, NY.
