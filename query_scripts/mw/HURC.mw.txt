Hurco Cos., Inc. is an industrial technology company. It designs, manufactures and sells computerized machine tools, consisting primarily of vertical machining and turning centers, to companies in the metal working industry. The company also provides machine tool components, software options, control upgrades, accessories and replacement parts for products, as well as customer service and training support. . Hurco was founded by Edward Humston and Gerald V. Roch in 1968 and is headquartered in Indianapolis, IN.
