Sonic Foundry, Inc. engages in the provision of video capture, management, and webcasting solutions in education, business, and government. The firm offers solutions for lecture capture, flipped &amp; blended learning, campus events, learning and development, corporate communications, live events and meetings, corporate YouTube, continuing medical education, procedures, simulations, and grand rounds. The company was founded by Monty R. Schmidt in 1991 and is headquartered in Madison, WI.
