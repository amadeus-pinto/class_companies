Myers Industries, Inc. engages in the manufacture and trade of polymer products. The company operates through the following segments: Material Handling and Distribution. The Material Handling segment designs, manufactures, and markets a variety of plastic and metal products. The Distribution segment distributes tools, equipment, and supplies used for tire, wheel, and under vehicle service on passenger, heavy truck, and off-road vehicles and the manufacturing of tire repair materials and custom rubber products. Myers Industries company was founded in 1933 and is headquartered in Akron, OH.
