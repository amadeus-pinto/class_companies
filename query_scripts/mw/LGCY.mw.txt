Legacy Reserves LP is an oil and natural gas limited partnership company. It focuses on the acquisition and development of oil and natural gas properties primarily located in the Permian Basin, Mid-Continent and Rocky Mountain regions of the United States. Its projects include East Texas, Spraberry/War San, Piceance Basin, Lea, Texas Panhandle, Deep Rock, Shafter Lake, Jalmat, Winchester, and Denton. The company was founded in October 26, 2005 and is headquartered in Midland, TX.
