Intersections, Inc. engages in the provision of consumer and corporate identity risk management services. It operates through the following segments: Personal Information Services, Insurance and Other Consumer Services, Pet Health Monitoring, and Bail Bonds Industry Solutions. The Personal Information Services segment focuses on helping consumers understand, monitor, manage and protect against the risks associated with their personal information. The Insurance and Other Consumer Services segment involves in helping consumers use insurance and other services to manage various personal risks and achieve personal goals. The Pet Health Monitoring segment offers VOYCE technology, and data-gathering solutions. The Bail Bonds Industry Solutions segment focuses on technology support for the bail bonds industry. The company was founded by Michael R. Stanfield and Thomas Lenox Kembner, Jr. in 1996 and is headquartered in Chantilly, VA.
