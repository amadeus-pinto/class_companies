Towerstream Corp. engages in the provision of fixed wireless solutions. It offers a range of services including wireless redundancy, virtual private networks, disaster recovery, bundled data and video services to business customers in New York City, Boston, Chicago, Los Angeles, San Francisco, Seattle, Miami, Dallas-Fort Worth, Houston, Philadelphia, Las Vegas-Reno and Providence-Newport. It operates through the Shared Wireless Infrastructure, and Fixed Wireless segments. The Shared Wireless Infrastructure segment offers wireless infrastructure platform primarily for co-location of customer owned antenna and related equipment and Wi-Fi access and offloading. The Fixed Wireless segment provides fixed wireless broadband services to commercial customers and deliver access over a wireless network transmitting over both licensed and unlicensed radio spectrum. The company was founded December 17, 1999 and is headquartered in Middletown, RI.
