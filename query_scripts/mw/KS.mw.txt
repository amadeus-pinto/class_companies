KapStone Paper &amp; Packaging Corp. engages in the manufacturing of paper container and packaging products. It operates through the Paper and Packaging and Distribution segments. The Paper and Packaging segment includes manufacturing and selling container board, corrugated products, and specialty paper. The Distribution segment involves in packaging solutions and services and distributes corrugated packaging materials. The company was founded on April 15, 2005 and is headquartered in Northbrook, IL.
