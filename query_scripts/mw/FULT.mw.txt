Fulton Financial Corp. is financial holding company, which engages in the provision of consumer and commercial banking products and services. It provides personal banking services include various checking account and savings deposit products, certificates of deposit and individual retirement accounts. The company also offers investment management, trust, brokerage, insurance and investment advisory services to consumer and commercial banking customers in the market areas serviced by the subsidiary banks. Fulton was founded on February 8, 1982 and is headquartered in Lancaster, PA.
