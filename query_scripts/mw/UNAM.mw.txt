Unico American Corp. is an insurance holding company which engages in the provision of underwriting property and casualty insurance through its insurance company subsidiary. It operates through Insurance Company Operations and Other Insurance Operations segments. The Insurance Company Operations segment includes property, casualty, health and life insurance. The Other Insurance Operations segment offers insurance premium financing, claim administration services, and membership association services. The company was founded in 1969 and is headquartered in Calabasas, CA.
