Republic Bancorp, Inc. operates as a financial holding company for Republic Bank &amp; Trust Co. and Republic Insurance Services, Inc. The company, through its subsidiaries offers residential mortgage loans, home equity loans &amp; lines, commercial real estate loans, C&amp;I loans, business loans &amp; lines of credit, equipment leasing consumer loans, and warehouse lines of credit. Its deposit products include savings, checking, and money market accounts; IRAs; and CDs. The company also offers personal and business online banking services, including apps for iPhone, Android, iPad and Android tablets; bill pay; and mobile deposit. It operates through the following segments: Traditional Banking, Warehouse Lending, Mortgage Banking and Republic Processing Group. Republic Bancorp was founded by Bernard M. Trager in 1974 and is headquartered in Louisville, KY.
