Boot Barn Holdings, Inc. engages in the operation of retail chain stores of western and work-related footwear, apparel, and accessories. Its products include boots, jeans, accessories, hats, gifts and home products, and work wear. It also focuses on providing service and selection of brands including Ariat, Wrangler, Justin, Cowgirl Tuff, Miss Me, Rock &amp; Roll Cowgirl, Grace in LA, Panhandle, Cinch, Scully, Rock &amp; Roll Cowboy. The company was founded on November 17, 2011 and is headquartered in Irvine, CA.
