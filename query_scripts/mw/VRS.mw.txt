Verso Corp. produces and supplies coated paper and products. It offers papers ranging from web, coated, digital, specialty and book, to supercalendered and uncoated. The firm&#x27;s printing papers are designed primarily for commercial printing, media and marketing applications, including magazines, catalogs, books, direct mail, corporate collateral and retail inserts. The company was founded on August 1, 2006 and is headquartered in Memphis, TN.
