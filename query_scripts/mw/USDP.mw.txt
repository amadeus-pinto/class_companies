USD Partners LP engages in the acquisition, development, and operation of energy-related logistic assets. It operates through the following segments: Terminalling Services; Fleet Services; and Corporate and Other. The Terminalling Services segment offers take-or-pay contracts for crude oil terminal services. The Fleet Services segment provides rail car fleet management. The Corporate and Other segment pertains to the firm&#x27;s administrative operations and financing transactions. The company was founded on June 5, 2014 and is headquartered in Houston, TX.
