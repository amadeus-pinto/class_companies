ModusLink Global Solutions, Inc. engages in the provision of end-to-end global supply chain management solutions. It operates under five operating segments: Americas, Asia, Europe, e-Business and ModusLink PTS. Its end-to-end solution enables clients to link supply and demand in real time, improve visibility and performance throughout the supply chain, and provide real-time access to information for greater collaboration and making informed business decisions. The company offers services to consumer electronics, communications, computing, software, and retail. ModusLink Global Solutions was founded in 1986 and is headquartered in Waltham, MA.
