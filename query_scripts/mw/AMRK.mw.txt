A-Mark Precious Metals, Inc. operates as a full-service precious metals trading company. Its products include gold, silver, platinum and palladium for storage and delivery in the form of coins, bars, wafers and grain. Its trading related services include financing, consignment, hedging and various customized financial programs. The company was founded by Steven C. Markoff on April 9, 1965 and is headquartered in Santa Monica, CA.
