Evolving Systems, Inc. engages in the provision of software and services to network operators. It operates through the License Fees and Services Revenue and Customer Support Revenue segments. The License Fees and Services Revenue segment represents the fees received from the license of software products and those services directly related to the delivery of the licensed products. The Customer Support Revenue segment includes annual support fees, recurring maintenance fees, fees for maintenance upgrades, and warranty services. The company was founded in 1985 and is headquartered in Englewood, CO.
