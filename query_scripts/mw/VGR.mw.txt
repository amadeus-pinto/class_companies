Vector Group Ltd. operates as a holding company, which manufactures and sells tobacco products. It operates through the following segments: Tobacco, E-Cigarettes and Real Estate. The Tobacco segment consists of the manufacture and sale of cigarettes. The E-Cigarettes segment includes the operations of the company&#x27;s e-cigarette business. The Real Estate segment includes investments in consolidated and non-consolidated real estate businesses. The company is headquartered in Miami, FL.
