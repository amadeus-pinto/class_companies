BJ&#x27;s Restaurants, Inc. owns and operates casual dining restaurants. It operates BJ&#x27;s Restaurant and Brewery, BJ&#x27;s Restaurant &amp; Brewhouse, BJ&#x27;s Pizza and Grill or BJ&#x27;s Grill. It offers pizzas, appetizers, specialty salads, soups, pastas, sandwiches, entr&#xE9;es, desserts, and proprietary craft beers.
