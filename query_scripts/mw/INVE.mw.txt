Identiv, Inc. engages in the business of security technology focuses on data, physical places and things. It operates through the following segments: Premises. Identity, and Credentials, and All Other. The Premises segment offers centralizes management of access and security operations across an organization including control of doors, gates, turnstiles, elevators and other building equipment, monitoring users as they move around a facility, preventing unwanted access, maintaining compliance and providing a robust audit trail. The Identity segment relates to definition and provisioning of access cards, and other devices such as contactless, portable and mobile smart card readers, tokens and terminals that are utilized around the world to enable logical access and security and identification applications, such as national ID, payment, e-Health and e-Government. The Credentials segment refers to NFC and radio frequency identification products including inlays and inlay-based cards-labels, tags and stickers, as well as other radio frequency and IC communications. The All Other segment provides products such as Chipdrive and Digital Media readers. The company was founded in 1990 and is headquartered in Fremont, CA.
