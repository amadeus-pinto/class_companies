Tile Shop Holdings, Inc. operates as a holding company through its subsidiary retails which manufactures natural stone tiles, setting and maintenance materials and related accessories. The company&#x27;s products include ceramic, porcelain, glass, stainless steel manufactured tiles, marble, granite, quartz, sandstone, travertine, slate and onyx natural tiles. It manufactures its own setting and maintenance materials, such as thinset, grout, and sealers. The company was founded by Robert A. Rucker on June 27, 2012 and is headquartered in Plymouth, MN.
