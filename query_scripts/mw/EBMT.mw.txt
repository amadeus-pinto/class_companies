Eagle Bancorp Montana, Inc. operates as a bank holding company with interest in providing commercial, residential and consumer loans. It provides services through its wholly-owned subsidiary Opportunity Bank of Montana. Its commercial real estate loans which include loans on multi-family dwellings, non-residential property and developed and undeveloped land. The bank provides residential mortgages and commercial real estate loans, real estate construction loans, home equity loans, consumer loans and commercial loans. The company was founded on October 28, 1997 and is headquartered in Helena, MT.
