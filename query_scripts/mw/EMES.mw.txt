Emerge Energy Services LP engages in the ownership, operation, acquisition and development of a diversified portfolio of energy service assets. It operates business through two segments: Sand and Fuel Processing and Distribution. The Sand segment consists of mining, processing and distributing silica sand, which is used in hydraulic fracturing of oil and natural gas wells. The Fuel Processing and Distribution segment consists of acquiring, processing and separating the transportation mixture or transmix. The company was founded on April 27, 2012 and is headquartered in Southlake, TX.
