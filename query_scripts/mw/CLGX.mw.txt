CoreLogic, Inc. engages in the provision of property, financial and consumer information services to mortgage originators, financial institutions and government organizations. It operates through the following business segments: Data &amp; Analytics, and Technology &amp; Processing Solutions. The Data &amp; Analytics segment offers access to data assets including real estate information, mortgage-backed securities information, criminal and eviction records, employment verification, flood and hazard information and under-banked credit information. The Technology &amp; Processing Solutions segment provides loan origination and closing-related services and solutions, including tax, flood and data services to mortgage originators and also it provides settlement services in connection with residential mortgage loans. CoreLogic was founded on June 1, 2010 and is headquartered in Irvine, CA.
