Par Pacific Holdings, Inc. owns, manages, and maintains interests in energy and infrastructure businesses. The company operates through the following segments: Refining, Retail, Logistics, Texadian, and Corporate and Other. The Refining segment involves the production of sulfur diesel, gasoline, jet fuel, marine fuel, and other associated refined products. The Retail segment engages in the sale of gasoline, diesel, and retail merchandise. The Logistics segments operates terminals, pipelines, single-point mooring and trucking operations to distribute refined products. The Texadian segment focuses on sourcing, marketing, transporting, and distributing crude oil and refined products in U.S. and Canada. The Corporate and Other segment includes administrative costs and several small non-operated oil and gas interests owned by the company&#x27;s predecessor. The company was founded on December 21, 1984 and is headquartered in Houston, TX.
