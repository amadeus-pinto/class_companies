HCI Group, Inc. is a property and casualty insurance holding company, which engages in the provision of property and casualty homeowners insurance, condominium-owners insurance, and tenants insurance to individuals owning property in Florida through its subsidiary. It operates through the Insurance Operations and Other Operations. The Insurance Operations includes Property and casualty insurance and reinsurance services. The Other Operations comprises of real estate and information technology services. The company was founded by Paresh Patel, Gregory Politis, and Martin A. Traber in 2006 and is headquartered in Tampa, FL.
