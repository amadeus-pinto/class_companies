Trecora Resources engages in the manufacture and sale of petrochemical products, synthetic waxes, and provision of custom processing services. It operates through the Specialty Petrochemical Products and Specialty Synthetic Wax. The Specialty Petrochemical Products segment is conducted through South Hampton Resources, Inc., which produces high purity hydrocarbons and other petroleum based products. The Specialty Synthetic Wax segment is conducted through Trecora Chemical, Inc., which produces specialty polyethylene and poly alpha olefin waxes. The company was founded on May 4, 1967 and is headquartered in Sugar Land, TX.
