Southern First Bancshares, Inc. operates as a bank holding company for Southern First Bank, NA. The company offers a wide range of traditional banking products and services for small-to medium-sized businesses, professionals and other individuals, which include commercial and consumer loan and deposit services, as well as mortgage services. It also offers other bank services including safe deposit boxes, traveler&#x27;s checks, direct deposit, and banking by mail; Internet banking, bill payment, and cash management services. The company was founded in March 1999 and is headquartered in Greenville, SC.
