Seneca Foods Corp. engages in the processing and sale of fruits and vegetables. It&#x27;s product offerings include canned, frozen and bottled produce and snack chips. It operates through the Packaging and sale of fruits and vegetables and Packaging and sale of chip products business segments. Its products are sold under private label, as well as national and regional brands that the company owns or licenses, including Seneca, Libby&#x27;s, Aunt Nellie&#x27;s Farm Kitchen, Stokely&#x27;s, Read, Taste of the West, Cimarron and Tendersweet. The company was founded by Arthur S. Wolcott in 1949 and is headquartered in Marion, NY.
