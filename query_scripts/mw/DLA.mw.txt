Delta Apparel, Inc. engages in the design, merchandise, sales, and marketing of lifestyle branded active wear apparel and headwear. It operates through the Branded and Basics segments. The Branded segment specializes in apparel garments and headwear in different brands, including Salt Life, Junkfood, and Soffe. The Basics segment focuses on the distribution of garment styles, such as Delta Activewear, Art Gun, Delta Pro Weight, and Delta Magnum Weight. The company was founded in 1903 and is headquartered in Greenville, SC.
