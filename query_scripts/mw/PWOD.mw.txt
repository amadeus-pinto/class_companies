Penns Woods Bancorp, Inc. operates as the holding bank of Jersey Shore State Bank and Luzerne Bank.it offers a range of products including accepting time, demand and savings deposits, including Super NOW accounts, regular savings accounts, money market certificates, investment certificates, fixed-rate certificates of deposit and club accounts. It also offer secured and unsecured commercial and consumer loans, financing commercial transactions, making construction and mortgage loans, and the renting of safe deposit facilities. The company was founded on January 7, 1983 and is headquartered in Williamsport, PA.
