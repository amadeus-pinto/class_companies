Unit Corp. engages in oil and gas exploration, production, contract drilling, and natural gas gathering and processing through its subsidiaries. It operates through the following segments: Oil and Natural Gas, Contract Drilling, and Mid-Stream. The Oil and Natural Gas segment involves in exploring, developing, acquiring, and producing oil and natural gas properties. The Contract Drilling segment offers contracts to drill onshore oil and natural gas. The Mid-Stream segment comprises of buying, selling, gathering, processing, and treating natural gas for third parties. The company was founded by King P. Kirchner and James Donald Bodard in 1963 and is headquartered in Tulsa, OK.
