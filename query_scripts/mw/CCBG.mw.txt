Capital City Bank Group, Inc. operates as a bank holding company. It provides traditional deposit and credit services, asset management, trust, mortgage banking, merchant services, bank cards, data processing, and securities brokerage services. The company&#x27;s four principal services are banking services, data processing services, trust and asset management services, and brokerage services. Capital City Bank Group was founded on December 13, 1982 and is headquartered in Tallahassee, FL.
