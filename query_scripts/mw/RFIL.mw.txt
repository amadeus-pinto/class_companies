RF Industries Ltd. engages in the design, manufacture, and marketing of interconnect products and systems. Its products include RF connectors, coaxial and custom cable assemblies, fiber optic cables, wiring harnesses and medical wiring. The company operates through three business segments: RF Connector &amp; Cable Assembly, Medical Cabling &amp; Interconnector, and Fiber Optics. RF Industries was founded by Howard F. Hill on November 1, 1979 and is headquartered in San Diego, CA.
