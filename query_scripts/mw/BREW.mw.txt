Craft Brew Alliance, Inc. is an independent craft brewing company, which engages in brewing, branding, and marketing of craft beers. It operates through Beer Related Operations, and Brewpubs Operations segment. The Beer Related Operations segment includes the brewing, and domestic and international sales of craft beers and ciders from the breweries of the company. The Brewpubs Operations segment is comprised of the brewpubs of the company, which are located adjacent to its beer related operations, other merchandise sales, and sales of its beers directly to customers. The company was founded by Gordon Bowker and Paul Shipman on May 4, 1981 and is headquartered in Portland, OR.
