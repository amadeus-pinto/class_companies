Income Opportunity Realty Investors, Inc. invests in equity interests in real estate through direct equity investments and partnerships, and financing real estate and real estate-related activities through investments in mortgage loans. It engages in the business of acquisition, development, financing, capital markets, construction management, property management, marketing, leasing and disposition of real estate and real estate-related investments. The company was founded on December 14, 1984 and is headquartered in Dallas, TX.
