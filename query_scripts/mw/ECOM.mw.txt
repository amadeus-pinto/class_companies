ChannelAdvisor Corp. engages in providing SaaS solutions that enable the retailer and branded manufacturer customers to integrate, manage and optimize their merchandise sales across hundreds of online channels. It enables the customers to connect with new and existing sources of demand for their products, including e-commerce marketplaces, such as eBay, Amazon, Newegg and Sears, search engines and comparison shopping websites, such as Google, Microsoft&#x27;s Bing, and Nextag and emerging channels, such as Facebook and Pinterest. The company was founded by M. Scot Wingo and Aris Antanas Buinevicius in June 2001 and is headquartered in Morrisville, NC.
