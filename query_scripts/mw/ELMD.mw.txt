Electromed, Inc. develops, manufactures, and markets airway clearance products, which apply High Frequency Chest Wall Oscillation therapy in pulmonary care for patients of all ages. It markets its products in the United States to the home health care and institutional markets for use by patients in personal residences, hospitals and clinics. It also sells internationally both directly and through distributors. The company was founded by Robert D. Hansen and Craig N. Hansen in 1992 and is headquartered in New Prague, MN.
