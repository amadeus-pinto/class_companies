VIVUS, Inc. operates as a biopharmaceutical company engages in the development and commercialization of novel therapeutic products. It offers the Qsymia for chronic weight management, as well as STENDRA and SPEDRA for erectile dysfunction. Its development also include Tacrolimus which is for the treatment of Pulmonary Arterial Hypertension. The company was founded by Virgil A. Place on April 16, 1991 and is headquartered in Mountain View, CA.
