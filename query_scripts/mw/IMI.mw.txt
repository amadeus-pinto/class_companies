Intermolecular, Inc. engages in the provision of solutions for the evaluation and development of engineered thin-film materials for next-generation technology products. It offers proprietary high productivity combinatorial platform and multi-disciplinary development team for the research and development of advanced materials in the semiconductor, displays, and glass and coatings industries. The company was founded by David E. Lazovsky in June 2004 and is headquartered in San Jose, CA.
