Aemetis, Inc. is a renewable fuels and biochemicals company, which focuses on the production of advanced fuels and chemicals through the acquisition, development, and commercialization of technologies. The company operates through the North America and India geographical segments. The North American segment comprises of the Keyes plant and research facilities in College Park, Maryland. The India segment includes the biodiesel plant; administrative offices; and holding companies. The company was founded by Eric Armstrong McAfee in 2005 and is headquartered in Cupertino, CA.
