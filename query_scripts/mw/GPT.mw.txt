Gramercy Property Trust operates as an investor and asset manager of commercial real estate, which engages in acquiring and managing single-tenant, net-leased industrial and office properties purchased through sale-leaseback transactions or directly from property developers and owners. It operates through the Investments/Corporate and Asset Management segments. The Investments/Corporate segment includes all of activities related to investment and ownership of commercial properties net leased to high quality tenants throughout the United States. The Asset Management segment covers substantially all of activities related to third-party asset and property management of commercial properties located throughout the United States and Europe. The company was founded in April 2004 and is headquartered in New York, NY.
