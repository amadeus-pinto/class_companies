ACNB Corp. operates as a bank holding company for ACNB Bank. It is engaged in providing banking and financial services to individuals and businesses, including commercial and retail banking, trust and investment management, and insurance through its banking and nonbanking subsidiaries. The company&#x27;s commercial lending services include commercial mortgages, real estate development and construction, accounts receivable and inventory financing, and agricultural loans. Its consumer lending programs include home equity loans and lines of credit, automobile and recreational vehicle loans, manufactured housing loans, and personal lines of credit. The company&#x27;s lending programs include personal residential mortgages, residential construction loans, and investment mortgage loans. It offers deposit, online banking, online bill payment and presentment, telephone banking, debit cards cash management, merchant services, consumer, student, auto, home equity, mortgage and business loans. The company was founded in 1982 and is headquartered in Gettysburg, PA.
