Great Southern Bancorp, Inc. is a bank holding and financial holding company. It Operates business through its subsidiary, Great Southern Bank provides banking products and services. The company offers residential and commercial real estate, construction, other commercial and consumer loans; regular savings, checking, and various money market accounts, fixed-interest rate certificates with varying maturities, and certificates of deposit; and insurance, travel, investment and other related services. Great Southern Bancorp was founded in July 1989 and is headquartered in Springfield, MO.
