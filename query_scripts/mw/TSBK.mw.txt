Timberland Bancorp, Inc. (Washington) is holding company engages in the provision of banking services. It offers a range of products and services including deposit instruments, including money market deposit accounts, checking accounts, negotiable order of withdrawal checking, regular savings accounts and certificates of deposit. The company&#x27;s loan products comprise of commercial real estate loans, construction and land development loans, land loans, commercial business loans, consumer loans and unsecured loans. Timberland Bancorp was founded on September 8, 1997 and is headquartered in Hoquiam, WA.
