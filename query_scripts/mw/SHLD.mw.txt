Sears Holdings Corp. engages in the provision of retail stores, through its subsidiaries. It operates through the Kmart and Sears Domestic segments. The Kmart segment offers consumer electronics, seasonal merchandise, outdoor living, toys, lawn and garden equipment, food and consumables, and apparel. The Sears Domestic segment involves in line stores specialty stores, commercial sales, and home services. The company was founded in 2004 and is headquartered in Hoffman Estates, IL.
