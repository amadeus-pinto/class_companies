Sevcon, Inc. is engaged in designing and selling motor controllers for zero emission electric and hybrid vehicles. The company offers products on electrically powered vehicles from fork lift trucks to electric cars and bikes. It operates through two segments: Electronic Controls and Capacitors. The Electronic Controls segment produces microprocessor based control systems for zero emission and hybrid electric vehicles. The Capacitor segment produces special metalized film capacitors for sale to electronic equipment manufacturers. Sevcon was founded by Marvin G. Schorr in 1961 and is headquartered in Southborough, MA.
