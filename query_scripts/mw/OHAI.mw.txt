OHA Investment Corp. is a business development company that invests in small and middle market companies located in the US. The fund focuses on companies with experienced management teams, stable free cash flows, diverse customer bases, and defensible market positions. It targets companies operating across a broad range of sectors including energy, niche manufacturing, distribution, business services, healthcare products and services, and consumer services. It provides financing in the form of unitranche, subordinated debt, convertible debt, preferred equity and project equity for acquisitions, buyouts, monetization, recapitalizations, restructuring, special situations, and growth capital requirements. Its investment size ranges from $5 to $100 million.
