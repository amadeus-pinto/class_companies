UQM Technologies, Inc. develops and manufactures power dense, electric motors, generators and power electronic controllers for the automotive, aerospace, military and industrial markets. It is developing products for the alternative-energy technologies sector, including propulsion systems for electric, hybrid electric, plug-in hybrid electric and fuel cell electric vehicles, under-the-hood power accessories and other vehicle auxiliary&#x27;s components. The company was founded on December 7, 1967 and is headquartered in Longmont, CO.
