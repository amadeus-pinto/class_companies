Marcus &amp; Millichap, Inc. is a brokerage company specializing in commercial real estate investment sales, financing, research and advisory services.  The company provides investment brokerage and financing services to investors of all sizes and types of commercial real estate properties. It serves clients with one property, multiple properties and investment portfolios. Marcus &amp; Millichap was founded by George M. Marcus and William A. Millichap in 1971 and is headquartered in Calabasas, CA.
