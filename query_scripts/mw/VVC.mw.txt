Vectren Corp. operates as an energy holding company. It offers energy-related products and services include infrastructure services, energy services, and energy marketing services throughout the U.S. through its wholly owned subsidiary, Vectren Utility Holdings, Inc. The firm provides energy delivery services to residential, commercial and industrial, and other customers in Indiana and west central Ohio. It operates through following segment groups: Utility Group, Nonutility Group, and Corporate &amp; Other. The Utility Group segment consists of the company&#x27;s regulated operations and other operations that provide information technology and other support services to those regulated operations. The firm segregates its regulated operations into a Gas Utility Services segment and an Electric Utility Services segment. The Gas Utility Services segment provides natural gas distribution and transportation services to Indiana and to West Central Ohio. The Electric Utility Services segment provides electric distribution services primarily to Southwestern Indiana, and includes the power generating and wholesale power operations. The Nonutility Group segment includes activities that are into Infrastructure Services and Energy Services business areas. The Infrastructure Services business provides underground construction and repair to utility infrastructure through its wholly owned subsidiary, Miller Pipeline, LLC and Minnesota Limited, LLC. The Energy Services business provides performance based energy contracting operations and renewable energy services performed through Energy Systems Group LLC. The Corporate &amp; Other Businesses group segment includes a variety of legacy, wholly owned operations and investments that have invested in energy-related opportunities and services, real estate and leveraged leases, among other investments. The company was founded on June 10, 1999 and is headquartered in Evansville, IN.
