Caesars Entertainment Corp. is a diversified casino-entertainment company. The company operates its business through its wholly owned subsidiary, Caesars Entertainment Operating Company, Inc. It owns an online gaming business, which provides real money casino, bingo and poker games in the United Kingdom, and has alliances with online gaming providers in Italy and France. The company&#x27;s resorts operate primarily under the Caesars, Harrah&#x27;s and Horseshoe brand names. Caesars Entertainment was founded by William Fisk Harrah in 1937 and is headquartered in Las Vegas, NV.
