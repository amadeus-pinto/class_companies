PharMerica Corp. is an institutional pharmacy service company, which engages in the provision of healthcare facilities in the pharmacy management services to hospitals. It operates through following segments: Institutional Pharmacy, Specialty Infusion Services, and Specialty Oncology Pharmacy. The Institutional Pharmacy segment offers products and services to residents and patients in skilled nursing facilities, nursing centers, assisted living facilities, hospitals, and long-term alternative care settings. The Specialty Infusion Services segment includes specialty infusion services focused on providing pharmaceutical products and clinical services to patients in client facilities, hospice, and outside of hospital or nursing home settings. The Specialty Oncology Pharmacy segment involves in dispensing of oncology drugs and care management to patients, oncology practices, and hospitals. The company was founded on July 31, 2007 and is headquartered in Louisville, KY.
