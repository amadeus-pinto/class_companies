Vishay Precision Group, Inc. designs, manufactures and markets components based on its resistive foil technology, sensors and sensor-based systems specializing in the markets of stress, force, weight, pressure and current measurements. The company operates through three reporting segments: Foil Technology Products, Force Sensors and Weighing &amp; Control Systems. The Foil Technology Products segment includes precision foil resistors and strain gages. The Force Sensors segment is comprised of transducers, load cells and modules. The Weighing &amp; Control Systems segment is comprised of instruments, complete systems for on-board weighing and overload monitor systems. Vishay Precision Group was founded on August 28, 2009 and is headquartered in Malvern, PA.
