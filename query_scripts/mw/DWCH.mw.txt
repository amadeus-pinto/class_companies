Datawatch Corp. designs, develops, markets and distributes business computer software products. Its services include implementation and support of its software products, as well as training on their use and administration. The company provides organizations the ability to integrate structured, unstructured and semi-structured sources like reports, PDF files and EDI streams with real time streaming data into visually rich analytic applications. Datawatch was founded in 1985 and is headquartered in Bedford, MA.
