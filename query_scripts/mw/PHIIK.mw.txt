PHI, Inc. helicopter services company, which is engaged in the oil and gas exploration, development, and production industry. It operates through the following segments: Oil and Gas; Air Medical; and Technical Services. The Oil and Gas segment provides helicopter services to oil and gas customers operating in the Gulf of Mexico and two foreign countries. The Air Medical segment provides helicopter services to hospitals and medical programs in several U.S. states, and also to individuals in which case the company is paid by either a commercial insurance company, federal or state agency, or the patient. The Technical Services segment provides helicopter repair and overhaul services for existing flight operations customers. It offers services to the offshore oil and gas, onshore mining, international, air medical, and technical services industries. The company was founded in February 1949 and is headquartered in Lafayette, LA.
