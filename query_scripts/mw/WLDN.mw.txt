Willdan Group, Inc. engages in the provision of technical and consulting services. It company operates through the following segments: Energy Efficiency Services, Engineering Services, Public Finance Services, and Homeland Security Services. The Energy Efficiency Services segment offers energy efficiency and sustainability consulting services to utilities, public agencies, and private industry. The Engineering Services segment provides civil engineering and city planning services, geotechnical, and other engineering consulting services. The Public Finance Services segment consists of economic and financial consulting services to public agencies. The Homeland Security Services segment includes national preparedness and interoperability services and communications and technology solutions. The company was founded in May 1964 and is headquartered in Anaheim, CA.
