Intricon Corp. engages in design, development, engineering and manufacturing body-worn medical and electronics devices. It serves the body-worn device market by designing, developing, engineering and manufacturing micro-miniature products, microelectronics, micro-mechanical assemblies and complete assemblies, primarily for bio-telemetry devices, hearing instruments and professional audio communication devices. Its technologies involves nanoLink wireless systems, PhysioLink wireless systems, BodyNet biotelemetry technology, nanoDSP signal processing. The company was founded in 1930 and is headquartered in Arden Hills, MN.
