Mueller Water Products, Inc. engages in the manufacture and marketing of water infrastructure, flow control, and piping component system products for use in water distribution networks and water treatment facilities. It operates though the following segments: Mueller Co., Anvil, and Mueller Technologies. The Mueller Co. segment involves in manufacturing of valves for water and gas systems including butterfly, iron gate, tapping, check, knife, plug and ball valves, and dry-barrel and wetbarrel fire hydrants. The Anvil segment offers manufacturing and sourcing of products including fittings, couplings, and hangers. The Mueller Technologies segment includes metering, leak detection, pipe condition assessment, and products and services for the water infrastructure industry. The company was founded on September 22, 2005 and is headquartered in Atlanta, GA.
