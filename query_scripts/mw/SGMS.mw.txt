Scientific Games Corp. engages in the development of technology-based products for the global lottery and regulated gaming industries. It operates its business through the following segments: Gaming, Lottery and Interactive. The Interactive business segment is comprised solely of their instant products operating segment, which provides instant lottery games; and related value-added services, as well as licensed brands that are printed on instant lottery games and other promotional lottery products. The Lottery Systems business segment is comprised solely of their lottery systems operating segment, which provides products and services generally comprised of a central system, customized computer software, data communication services, support and/or related equipment to lottery operators. The Gaming segment designs, develops, manufactures, markets, and distributes gaming products and services; and involves gaming operations, machine sales, systems, and table products. The Interactive segment refers to social gaming through casino-style, slot-based games; real money gaming; and SG Universe play-for-fun and play-for-free white label gaming services. The company was founded on July 2, 1984 and is headquartered in Las Vegas, NV.
