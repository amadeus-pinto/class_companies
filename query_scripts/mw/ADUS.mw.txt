Addus HomeCare Corp. engages in the provision of in-home personal care services. The firm offers assistance with bathing; grooming; oral care; skincare; feeding and dressing; medication reminders; meal planning and preparation; housekeeping and transportation services; and other activities of daily living. It also provides veteran care services. The company was founded on July 27, 2006 and is headquartered in Downers Grove, IL.
