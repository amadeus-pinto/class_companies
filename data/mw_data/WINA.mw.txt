Winmark Corp. engages in creating, supporting, and financing business. It operates through the Franchising and Leasing segments. The Franchising segment involves in franchising retail store concepts that buy, sell, trade, and consign merchandise. The Leasing segment includes Winmark Capital Corp. and Wirth Business Credit, Inc. that caters to large and medium-sized businesses and focuses on technology-based assets. The company was founded in 1988 and is headquartered in Minneapolis, MN.
