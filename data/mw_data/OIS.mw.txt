Oil States International, Inc. engages in the provision of specialty products and services to oil and natural gas companies. The company operates through the following segments: Offshore Products and Well Site Services. The Offshore Products segment manufactures deepwater capital equipment which is used on floating production platforms, subsea pipelines, floating drilling rigs and vessels around the world. The Well Site Services segment provides critical services at the well site, including completion services and land drilling services. Its completion services business provides completion and production related services and equipment and personnel throughout the active regions of the U.S. and Canada. Oil States International was founded in July 1995 and is headquartered in Houston, TX.
