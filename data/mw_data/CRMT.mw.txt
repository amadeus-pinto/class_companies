America&#x27;s Car-Mart, Inc. operates as an automotive retailer in U.S. It focuses on the integrated auto sales and finance segment of the used car market. The company&#x27;s operations are principally conducted through its two operating subsidiaries, America&#x27;s Car-Mart, Inc. and Colonial Auto Finance, Inc., which sell older model used vehicles and provides financing for its customers. America&#x27;s Car-Mart was founded in 1981 and is headquartered in Bentonville, AR.
