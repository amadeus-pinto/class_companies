Reliv International, Inc. engages in the developing, manufacturing and marketing of a proprietary line of nutritional supplements addressing basic nutrition, specific wellness needs, weight management and sports nutrition. It offers a ready-to-drink product and a line of skin care and food products. The company operates network marketing, which sell nutritional and dietary products to a sales force of independent distributors that sell the products directly to customers. Reliv International was founded by Robert L. Montgomery, Jr. and Sandra S. Mortgomery on February 11, 1985 and is headquartered in Chesterfield, MO.
