Gastar Exploration Inc. is an independent energy company, which is engaged in the exploration, development and production of oil, natural gas, condensate and natural gas liquids in the U. S. Its operates in Mid-Continent and Appalachia. The company is currently pursuing the development of liquids-rich natural gas in the Marcellus Shale in West Virginia and is also in the early stages of exploring and developing the Hunton Limestone horizontal oil play in Oklahoma. Gastar Exploration was founded by Thomas E. Robinson  in 1987 and is headquartered in Houston, TX.
