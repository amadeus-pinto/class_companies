Lattice Semiconductor Corp. designs, develops and markets programmable logic products and related software. It operates through the Lattice and Qterics business segments. The Lattice segment includes intellectual property and semiconductor devices. The Qterics segment is a discrete software-as-a-service business unit in the Lattice legal entity structure. The company was founded in 1983 by Samir Palnitkar and is headquartered in Portland, OR.
