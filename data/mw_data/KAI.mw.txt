Kadant, Inc. engages in designing and manufacturing products used in industries ranging from paper to plastics and textiles to tires. The company operates through the following segments: Papermaking Systems and Wood Processing Systems. The Papermaking Systems segment engages in the development, manufacture and sale of equipment and products for the global papermaking, paper recycling and process industries. he Wood Processing Systems segment engages in the design, manufacture, and marketing of stranders and related equipment used in the production of oriented strand board, and sell debarking and wood chipping equipment used in the forest products and the pulp and paper industries. Kadant was founded in November 1991 and is headquartered in Westford, MA.
