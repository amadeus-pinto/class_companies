RiceBran Technologies engages in the processing and distribution of stabilized rice bran and other proprietary rice bran-based ingredients and formulations. It operates through the USA and Brazil segments. The USA segment manufactures and distributes stabilized rice bran. The Brazil segment extracts crude rice bran oil and defatted rice bran products for both the human and animal food markets in Brazil and internationally. The company was founded on March 18, 1998 and is headquartered in Scottsdale, AZ.
