Medidata Solutions, Inc. provides cloud based solutions for life sciences that enhance the efficiency of customer&#x27;s clinical development processes from concept to conclusion, optimizing their research and development investments. It provides technology solutions and data analytics aimed at enabling effective and safe development, using secure cloud and hybrid cloud infrastructure to instantly connect users over the Internet. The company customers include pharmaceutical, biotechnology, and medical device companies, academic institutions, contract research organizations and other organizations engaged in clinical trials. The company was founded by Glen M. de Vries, Edward F. Ikeguchi and Tarek A. Sherif in June 1999 and is headquartered in New York, NY.
