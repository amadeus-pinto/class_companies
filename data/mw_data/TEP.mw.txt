Tallgrass Energy Partners LP owns, operates, acquires and develops midstream energy assets in North America. It operates through following segments: Natural Gas Transportation &amp; Logistics, Crude Oil Transportation &amp; Logistics and Processing &amp; Logistics. The Natural Gas Transportation and Logistics segment owns and operates FERC-regulated interstate natural gas pipelines and integrated natural gas storage facilities. The Crude Oil Transportation and Logistics segment owns and operates crude oil pipeline system. The Processing and Logistics segment owns and operates natural gas processing, treating and fractionation facilities, as well as water business services provided primarily to the oil and gas exploration and production industry. The company Partners was founded on February 6, 2013 and is headquartered in Leawood, KS.
