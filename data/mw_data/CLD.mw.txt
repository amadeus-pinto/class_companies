Cloud Peak Energy, Inc. engages in coal mines operations. Its activities include: Antelope Mine, Cordero Rojo Mine, and Spring Creek Mine. It operates through the following segments: Owned &amp; Operated Mines and Logistics &amp; Related Activities. The Owned &amp; Operated Mines segment includes thermal coal production where the sale occurs at the mine site. The Logistics &amp; Related Activities segment includes services provided by the company to its international and domestic customers. The company was founded on July 31, 2008 and is headquartered in Gillette, WY.
