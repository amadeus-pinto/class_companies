Gas Natural Inc. is a natural gas company, which engages in the distribution and selling of natural gas to end-use residential, commercial, and industrial customers. It operates through the following segments: Natural Gas Operations, Marketing and Production, and Corporate and Other. The Natural Gas Operations segment involves in the distribution of natural gas. The Marketing and Production segment markets natural gas to commercial and industrial customers; and manage midstream supply and production assets for transportation customers and utilities. The Corporate and Other segment encompasses the results of corporate acquisitions and equity transactions. The company was founded in 1909 and is headquartered in Cleveland, OH.
