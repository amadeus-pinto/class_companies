Acme United Corp. engages in the supply of cutting devices, measuring, first aid. and sharpening products to the to the school, home, office, hardware, sporting goods, and industrial markets. The firm operates its business through the following segments: Unites States. Canada, and Europe.  Its products include scissors, shears, knives, rulers, pencil sharpeners, first aid kits, and related products. The company was founded in 1867 and is headquartered in Fairfield, CT.
