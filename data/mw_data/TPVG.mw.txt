TriplePoint Venture Growth BDC Corp. is a business development company that seeks investment opportunities in companies located primarily in the US, UK, and Israel. The fund focuses on companies operating in the fields of biofuels, business applications software, wireless communications equipment, e-commerce, clothing &amp; accessories, conferencing equipment &amp; services, personal goods, financial institutions &amp; services, entertainment, mixed retailing, and healthcare services. It provides financing in the form of growth capital loans with an investment size ranging from $5 million to $50 million, equipment financings with an investment ranging from $5 million to $25 million, revolving loans with an investment ranging from $1 million to $25 million, direct equity investment transactions with an investment size ranging from $0.1 to $5 million, and warrants.
