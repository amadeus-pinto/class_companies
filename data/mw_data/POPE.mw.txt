Pope Resources LP engages in the ownership and management of timberland and development properties. It operates through the following segments: It operates through the following segments: Fee Timber, Timberland Management, and Real Estate. The Fee Timber segment relates to the growing, managing, harvesting, and marketing timber. The Timberland Management segment is the engaged in organizing and managing private equity timber funds using capital invested by third parties and the Partnership. The Real Estate segment manages development properties and involves in rental of properties. The company was founded in 1985 and is headquartered in Poulsbo, WA.
