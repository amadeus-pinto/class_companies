KLX, Inc. engages in the provision of aerospace fasteners and consumables. It operates through the Aerospace Solutions Group and Energy Services Group segments. The Aerospace Solutions Group segments offers fasteners, chemicals, honeywell proprietary parts, bearing. electrical, clamps, and lightning. The Energy Services Group segment involves in providing onshore completion services, wire line services, fishing services and tools, pressure control, remanufacturing shop, and accommodations, and surface rental equipment. The company was founded by Amin J. Khoury on July 31, 2014 and is headquartered in Wellington, FL.
