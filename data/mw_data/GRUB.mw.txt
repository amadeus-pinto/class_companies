GrubHub, Inc. operates as an online and mobile food-ordering company, which connects hungry diners with local takeout restaurants. Its online and mobile ordering platforms allow diners and corporate businesses to order directly from takeout restaurants in the United States and London. The company&#x27;s brands include GrubHub, Seamless, MenuPages and Allmenus. The company was founded on August 8, 2013 and is headquartered in Chicago, IL.
