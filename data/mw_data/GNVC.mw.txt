GenVec, Inc. is a clinical-stage gene delivery company, which engages in the development of therapeutics and vaccines. The firm designs, tests, and manufactures adenoviral-based product candidates using its proprietary AdenoVerse platform. Its products include CGF166-Hearing Loss; GV2311-RSV Vaccine; and GV2207-HSV-2 Immunotherapeutic. The company was founded in December 1992 and is headquartered in Gaithersburg, MD.
