GWG Holdings, Inc. operates as a financial services company. It engages in the creation of life insurance secondary market for the economic benefit of seniors with life insurance who are seeking post-retirement financial solutions, investors seeking yield derived from non-correlated assets, financial advisors seeking value-added products and services for their clients, and shareholders seeking to gain from the realization of the marketplace. The company was founded by Jon R. Sabes, Paul A. Siegert, and Steven F. Sabes in February 2006 and is headquartered in Minneapolis, MN.
