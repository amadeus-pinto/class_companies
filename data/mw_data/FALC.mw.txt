FalconStor Software, Inc. is a software defined storage company. It offers converged data services software platform that is hardware agnostic. The company&#x27;s product FreeStor reduces vendor lock-in and gives enterprises the freedom to choose the applications and hardware components that make the best sense for their business. It empowers organizations to modernize their data center with the right performance, in the right location, all while protecting existing investments. FalconStor Software was founded in 1994 and is headquartered in Melville, NY.
