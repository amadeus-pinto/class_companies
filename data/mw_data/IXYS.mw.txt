IXYS Corp. is a multi-market integrated semiconductor company, which engages in the development, manufacture and marketing of power semiconductors, advanced mixed-signal integrated circuits, application specific integrated circuits, microcontrollers, systems and radio frequency, power semiconductors. It designs power semiconductor, integrated circuits and system and radio frequency semiconductor products primarily for industrial and business applications, rather than for use in consumer electronics. The company was founded by Nathan Zommer in 1983 and is headquartered in Milpitas, CA.
