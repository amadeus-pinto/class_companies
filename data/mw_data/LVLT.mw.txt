Level 3 Communications, Inc. provides integrated communications services, non-switched and local-access telecommunications services. It provides IP-based communications services to enterprise, content, government and wholesale customers. The company products include Content distribution &amp; performance, Data networks, Managed &amp; professional services, Security, Voice UC &amp; C and Cloud security. It operates through four reportable segments: North America, Europe, the Middle East and Africa, Latin America and TW telecom. Level 3 Communications was founded by Kevin J. O&#x27;Hara in 1985 and is headquartered in Broomfield, CO.
