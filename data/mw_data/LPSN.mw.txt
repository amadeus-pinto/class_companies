LivePerson, Inc. engages in the provision of mobile and online messaging solutions. It operates through the following segments: Business, Consumer, and Corporate. The Business segment manages real-time online transactions which includes chat, voice, and content delivery. The Consumer segment deals with transactions between experts and users then sells its services to its clients. The company was founded by Robert P. Locascio on November 29, 1995 and is headquartered in New York, NY.
