Royal Bancshares of Pennsylvania, Inc. operates as the bank holding company of Royal Bank America, which engages in general banking business. It operates through Community Banking, and Tax Liens segments. The Community Banking segment is consist of commercial and retail banking, and equipment leasing. The Tax Liens segment is comprised of purchasing delinquent tax certificates from local municipalities at auction. The company was founded on June 3, 1994 and is headquartered in Bala Cynwyd, PA.
