Papa Murphy&#x27;s Holdings, Inc. engages in the operation of Take &#x27;N&#x27; Bake pizza chain. It operates through the following segments: Domestic Franchise, Domestic Company Stores and International. The Domestic Franchise segment consists of domestic franchised stores, which represent the majority of its system-wide stores. The Domestic Company Stores segment includes company-owned stores in the U.S. The International segment comprises of stores outside of the U.S., all of which are franchise stores. The company was founded in 1981 and is headquartered in Vancouver, WA.
