Mastech Digital, Inc. engages in the provision of information technology staffing for both mainstream and digital technologies, and digital transformation services. Its digital transformation services in comprised of salsesforce.com, SAP HANA, and Digital learning services. The company was founded by Ashok Trivedi and Sunil Tekchand Wadhwani on September 30, 2008 and is headquartered in Moon Township, PA.
