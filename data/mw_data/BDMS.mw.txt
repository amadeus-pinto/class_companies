Birner Dental Management Services, Inc. is a dental business service organization, which engages in servicing geographically dense dental practice networks in Colorado, New Mexico and Arizona. It provides general dentistry services including crowns and bridges, fillings, implants, and aesthetic procedures such as porcelain veneers and bleaching. The company offers specialty dental services, such as orthodontics, oral surgery, pediatrics, endodontics, and periodontics. Birner Dental Management Services was founded by Frederic W.J. Birner, Mark A. Birner and Dennis N. Genty in May 1995 and is headquartered in Denver, CO.
