Vishay Intertechnology, Inc. engages in the manufacturing and suppling of discrete semiconductors and passive electronic components. Its components are used in electronic devices and equipment, in the industrial, computing, automotive, consumer, telecommunications, military, aerospace, power supplies and medical markets. The company&#x27;s products are categorized into two components: Semiconductor and Passive. The Semiconductor components are used for a wide variety of functions, including power control, power conversion, power management, signal switching, signal routing, signal blocking, signal amplification, two-way data transfer, one-way remote control, and circuit isolation. Semiconductor components are categorized in to three segments: MOSFETs, Diodes, Optoelectronic Components, Resistors &amp; Inductors, and Capacitors. Vishay Intertechnology was founded by Felix Zandman in 1962 and is headquartered in Malvern, PA.
