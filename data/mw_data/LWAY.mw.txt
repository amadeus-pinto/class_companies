Lifeway Foods, Inc. engages in the manufacture of probiotic, cultured, functional dairy and non-dairy health food products. Its products include Drinkable Kefir, Lifeway Farmer Cheese, ProBugs, and Frozen Kefir. The company was founded by Michael Smolyansky and Ludmila Smolyansky in February 1986 and is headquartered in Morton Grove, IL.
