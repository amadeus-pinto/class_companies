AMREP Corp. engages in the real estate and media services businesses. It operates its business in through the following segments: Subscription Fulfillment Services and Real Estate operations. The Subscription Fulfillment Services segment operations include the performance of subscription fulfillment and other related services on behalf of publishers and other customers. Real Estate operations segment include land sales and lease activities. AMREP was founded 1961 and is headquartered in Princeton, NJ.
