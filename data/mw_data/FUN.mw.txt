Cedar Fair LP engages in the operation of amusement parks, outdoor and indoor water parks, and hotels. Its amusement parks include Cedar Point, Kings Island, Canada&#x27;s Wonderland, Dorney Park and Wildwater Kingdom, Valleyfair, Kings Dominion, Worlds of Fun, Knott&#x27;s Berry Farm, and California&#x27;s Great America. The company was founded on May 13, 1983 and is headquartered in Sandusky, OH.
