Park National Corp. operates as a holding company, which engages in owning and supervising its subsidiaries. The company operates its business through three subsidiaries as its business segments: The Park National Bank, SE Property Holdings, LLC and Guardian Financial Services Co. The Park National Bank segment engages in providing banking and financial services in Ohio and Kentucky. The SE Property Holdings, LLC segment is a non-bank subsidiary of the company, holding OREO property, performing lending services in Ohio and Florida. The Guardian Financial Services segment offers consumer finance services in the central Ohio region. Park National was founded in 1987 and is headquartered in Newark, OH.
