MAG Silver Corp. explores and develops mineral properties. It is a mineral exploration and predevelopment company focused on the acquisition, exploration and development of district scale projects located within the Mexican silver belt. The company is involved in the following projects: Juanicipio, Cinco de Mayo, Guigui and Salamandra. MAG Silver was founded by Frank R. Hallam, Peter Kenneth McNeill Megaw, R. Michael Jones on April 21, 1999 and is headquartered in Vancouver, Canada.
