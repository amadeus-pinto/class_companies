Triumph Bancorp, Inc. operates as a financial holding company which offers traditional banking and financial solutions. Is products lending and deposit products and services focused on local market areas to businesses. It operates through the following segments: Factoring, Banking, Asset Management, and Corporate. The Factoring segment includes the operations of Triumph Business Capital which offers factoring services. The Banking segment relates to operations of TBK Bank, including loans originated under Triumph Commercial Finance, Triumph Healthcare Finance, and Triumph Premium Finance brands. The Asset Management segment provides and manages other services relates to collateralized loan obligation funds. The Corporate segment refers to the financing and investment activities, as well as the management and administrative expenses. The company was founded by Aaron P. Graft in November 2010 and is headquartered in Dallas, TX.
