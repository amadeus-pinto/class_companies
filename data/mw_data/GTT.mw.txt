GTT Communications, Inc. engages in the provision of telecommunication services. It provides cloud networking services to multinational clients. Its products include wide area network services, internet services, managed network and security, as well as voice and unified communication services. It also offers the Tier 1 IP network which allows client to connect in the world and any application in the cloud. The company was founded by H. Brian Thompson on January 3, 2005 and is headquartered in McLean, VA.
