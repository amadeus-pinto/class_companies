Broadwind Energy, Inc. engages in the provision of technologically advanced high-value products to energy, mining, and infrastructure sector customers. It operates through Towers and Weldments, and Gearing. The Towers and Weldments segment is the manufacturing of towers for wind turbines, specifically the large and heavier wind towers that are designed for multiple megawatt wind turbines. The Gearing segment is comprised of engineers that build, and remanufactures precision gears and gearing systems for oil and gas, wind energy, mining, steel, and other industrial applications.
