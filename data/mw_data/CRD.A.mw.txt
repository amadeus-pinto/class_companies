Crawford &amp; Co. engages in the provision of claims management solutions to the risk management and insurance industry, as well as to self-insured entities. It operates through the following segments: U.S. Services, International, Broadspire, and Garden City Group. The U.S. Services segment offers claims management services. The International segment derives from the insurance company and third-party administration markets. The Broadspire segment includes claims and risk management services to clients in the self-insured or commercially insured marketplace. The Garden City Group segment comprises of legal settlement administration services related to securities, anti-trust, employment, product liability, and other class action settlements, as well as mass tort and bankruptcy. The company was founded by Jim Crawford on May 27, 1941 and is headquartered in Atlanta, GA.
