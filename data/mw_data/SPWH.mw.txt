Sportsman&#x27;s Warehouse Holdings, Inc. engages in the provision of sporting and athletic goods through wholly owned subsidiaries, Sportsman&#x27;s Warehouse, Inc., and Minnesota Merchandising Corp. It products include hunting and shooting; archery; fishing; camping; boating accessories; optics and electronics; knives and tools; and footwear. The company was founded in 1986 and is headquartered in Midvale, UT.
