Good Times Restaurants, Inc. owns and operates restaurant franchises. It operates through its wholly owned subsidiary, Good Times Drive Thru, Inc., which engages in the business of developing, owning, operating and franchising hamburger-oriented drive-through restaurants. It operates through the Good Times Burgers and Frozen Custard Restaurants, and Bad Daddy&#x27;s Burger Bar Restaurants segments. The company was founded in 1987 and is headquartered in Lakewood, CO.
