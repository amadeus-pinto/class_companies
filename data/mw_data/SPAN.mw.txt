Span-America Medical Systems, Inc. engages in the manufacture and trade of medical products. It operates through the Medical and Custom Products segments. The Medical segment involves in producing and marketing therapeutic support surfaces, medical bed frames, patient positioners, mattress overlays, and fall protection products. The Custom Products segment includes manufacturing bedding products, mattress overlays, and foam. The company was founded by Donald C. Spann on September 21, 1970 and is headquartered in Greenville, SC.
