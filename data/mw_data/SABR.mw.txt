Sabre Corp. is a technology solutions provider to the global travel and tourism industry. It offers technology solutions including, data-driven business intelligence, mobile, distribution and software-as-a-service solutions. The company operates through two segments: Travel Network and Airline &amp; Hospitality Solutions. The Travel Network segment is a global B2B travel marketplace for travel suppliers and travel buyers. The Airline &amp; Hospitality Solutions segment provides software solutions primarily for airlines and hotel properties. Sabre was founded in December 2006, and is headquartered in Southlake, TX.
