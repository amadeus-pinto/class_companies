Belden, Inc. engages in the provision of innovative signal transmission solutions. Its comprehensive portfolio of signal transmission solutions provides industry secure and reliable transmission of data, sound and video for mission critical applications. It operates through the following segments: Broadcast Solutions, Enterprise Connectivity Solutions, Industrial Connectivity Solutions, Industrial IT Solutions, and Network Security Solutions. The Broadcast Solutions segment provides production, distribution, and connectivity systems for television broadcast, cable, satellite, and IPTV industries. The Enterprise Connectivity Solutions segment offers network infrastructure solutions for enterprise customers. The Industrial Connectivity Solutions segment comprises of high performance networking components and machine connectivity products. The Industrial IT Solutions segment consists of mission-critical networking systems that provide the end-users with the highest confidence of reliability, availability, and security. The Network Security Solutions segment includes advanced threat protection, security, and compliance solutions for mission-critical networks. The company was founded by Joseph C. Belden in 1902 and is headquartered in St. Louis, MO.
