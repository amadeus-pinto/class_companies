American Electric Technologies, Inc. provides power delivery solutions to the traditional and renewable energy industries. It provides custom designed power distribution, power conversion, and automation and control systems. The power distribution products include low and medium voltage switchgear, arc managed and arc resistant switchgear. Its power conversion solutions include analog, digital silicon controlled rectifier and alternating current variable frequency drive systems. The automation and control solutions are programmable logic controllers based systems designed for the management and control of power in a user&#x27;s application. It also provides their services to oil and gas and marine and industrial sectors. The company was founded in 1946 and is headquartered in Houston, TX.
