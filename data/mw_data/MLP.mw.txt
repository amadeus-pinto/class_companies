Maui Land &amp; Pineapple Co., Inc. is a landholding and operating company, dedicated to agriculture, resort operation and the creation and management of holistic communities. The company operates through the following business segments: Real Estate, Leasing, Utilities and Resort Amenities. The Real Estate segment includes all land planning and entitlement, development, and sales. The Leasing segment includes commercial, industrial and agricultural land and facilities leases, licensing of its registered trademarks and trade names, and stewardship and conservation efforts. The Utilities segment operates two publicly-regulated utility companies which provide potable and non-potable water and sewage transmission services to the kapalua resort. It also manages ditch, reservoir and well systems which provide non-potable irrigation water to west and upcountry Maui areas. The Resort Amenities segment manages a full-service spa, a beach club, and a private club membership program. The company was founded in 1969 and is headquartered in Maui, HI.
