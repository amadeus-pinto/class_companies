Insignia Systems, Inc. develops and markets in-store advertising products, programs, and services to retailers and consumer packaged goods manufacturers. It focuses on providing in-store services through the Insignia Point-Of-Purchase Services in-store advertising program which includes thermal sign card supplies for the SIGNright and Impulse systems, Stylus software, laser printable cardstock, and label supplies. The company was founded in 1990 and is headquartered in Minneapolis, MN.
