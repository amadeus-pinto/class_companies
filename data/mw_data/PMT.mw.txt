PennyMac Mortgage Investment Trust is a finance company which through its subsidiaries invests primarily in residential mortgage loans and mortgage-related assets. It operates through the Correspondent Lending and Investment Activities segments. The Correspondent Lending segment focuses on the purchase for resale of newly originated mortgage loans. The Investment Activities segment focuses on mortgage assets that are acquired and held for investment purposes. The company was founded on May 18, 2009 and is headquartered in Westlake Village, CA.
