Knoll, Inc. engages in the design and manufacture of workplace furnishings, textiles, and fine leathers. It operates through the following segments: Office, Studio, and Coverings. The Office segment includes systems, seating, storage, tables, desks, and KnollExtra ergonomic accessories. The Studio segment includes KnollStudio division and the company&#x27;s European subsidiaries which sell KnollStudio products. The Coverings segment includes, KnollTextiles, Spinneybeck, and Edelman Leather. The company was founded by Hans G. Knoll and Florence Knoll in 1938 and is headquartered in East Greenville, PA.
