United States Cellular Corp. provides wireless telecommunication services. It provides voice call, messaging and data pricing, plan minutes, incoming calls, texts, picture messages, minutes, mobile-to-mobile service, voice turn-by-turn directions, bluetooth headsets, free incoming calls, e-mail, mobile Internet access, text messaging, Internet access, text and picture messaging, and voice turn-by-turn directions for its postpaid and prepaid customers. The company operates as subsidiary of Telephone and Data Systems, Inc. which owns, operates and invests in wireless markets throughout the United States. United States Cellular was founded by Leroy T. Carlson in 1983 and is headquartered in Chicago, IL.
