Mueller Industries, Inc. is a manufacturer of copper, brass, plastic and aluminum products. It operates through the following segments: Piping Systems, Industrial Metals, and Climate. The Piping Systems segment composes of domestic piping systems group, Canadian operations, and European operations, trading group, Jiangsu Mueller-Xingrong Copper Industries Ltd. and Jungwoo Metal Ind. co., Ltd. The Industrial Metals segment composes of brass rod &amp; copper bar products, impacts &amp; micro gauge, and brass value-added products. The Climate segment composes of refrigeration products, fabricated tube products, Westermeyer industries, Inc. and Turbotec products, Inc. Its products include: copper tube and fittings; line sets; brass and copper alloy rod, bar, and shapes; aluminum and brass forgings; aluminum impact extrusions; plastic fittings and valves; refrigeration valves and fittings; fabricated tubular products; and steel nipples. The company was founded on October 3, 1990 and is headquartered in Memphis, TN.
