STAAR Surgical Co. engages in the development, manufacture, production, market, and trade of implantable lenses for the eye and delivery systems. Its products include intraocular lens (IOL) and implantable collamer lens (ICL) under the following brands: Collamer IOLs, Preloaded IOLs, EVO Visian ICL, EVO&#x2B; Visian ICL, and Visian ICL. The company was founded in 1982 and is headquartered in Monrovia, CA.
