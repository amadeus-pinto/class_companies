Navigant Consulting, Inc. engages in the provision of management, financial and information technology consulting services. It operates through the following segments: Disputes, Investigations and Economics; Financial, Risk, and Compliance; Healthcare; and Energy. The Disputes, Investigation, and Economics segment includes valuation and economic analysis; as well as accounting, regulatory, construction, and computer forensic expertise. The Financial, Risk, and Compliance segment provides strategic, operational, valuation, risk management, investigative, and compliance advisory services. The Healthcare segment involves consulting services and business process management services. The Energy segment engages in the provision of management advisory services to utility, government, and commercial clients. The company was founded in 1996 and is headquartered in Chicago, IL.
