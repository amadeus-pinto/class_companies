NewMarket Corp. operates as a holding company, which through its subsidiaries Afton Chemical Corporation (Afton); Ethyl Corporation(Ethyl); NewMarket Services Corporation(NewMarket Services); and NewMarket Development Corporation engages in the provision of petroleum additives. Afton develops and manufactures highly formulated lubricant and fuel additive packages; as well as markets and sells these products worldwide. Ethyl comprises of the sale of tetraethyl lead. NewMarket Development manages properties owned in Virginia. NewMarket Services provides various administrative services.The company was founded on February 11, 1887 and is headquartered in Richmond, VA.
