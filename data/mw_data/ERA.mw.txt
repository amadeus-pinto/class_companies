Era Group, Inc. engages in the provision of helicopter transportation services to personnel to from and between offshore oil and gas production platforms, drilling rigs, and other installations. The primary customers of the company are comprises of international, independent, and major integrated oil and gas exploration, development, and production companies. Its services include search and rescue, emergency medical services, flightseeing, utility, leasing, fleet support and unmanned aerial solutions. The company was founded on April 29, 1999 and is headquartered in Houston, TX.
