NewStar Financial, Inc. is an internally-managed commercial finance company, which engages in the provision of lending platforms focused on meeting the complex needs of companies and private investors in the middle market. Its lending platforms provides a range of senior debt financing options to mid-sized companies to fund working capital, growth strategies, acquisitions, and recapitalizations, as well as, purchases of equipment and other capital assets. The company was founded by Timothy J. Conway in June 2004 and is headquartered in Boston, MA.
