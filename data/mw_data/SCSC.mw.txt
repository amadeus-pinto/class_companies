ScanSource, Inc. engages in the development and provision of technology products and services. It operates through the Barcode &amp; Security; and Communications &amp; Services segments. The Barcode &amp; Security segment offers automatic identification and data capture, point-of-sale, electronic physical security, and three-dimensional printing technologies. The Communications and Services segment includes voice, video conferencing, wireless, data networking, and converged communications solutions. The company was founded by Michael L. Baur and Steven H. Owings in December 1992 and is headquartered in Greenville, SC.
