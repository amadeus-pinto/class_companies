KCAP Financial, Inc. is a business development company that invests in middle market companies with an EBITDA of $10 - $50 million generating consistent cash flows located in North America. The fund targets companies operating across a broad range of sectors such as aerospace &amp; defense, automotive, beverage, food &amp; tobacco, broadcasting &amp; entertainment, buildings &amp; real estate, personal &amp; cargo transport, chemicals, plastics &amp; rubber, containers, packaging &amp; glass, diversified or conglomerate service, ecological, electronics, finance, healthcare, education &amp; childcare, home &amp; office furnishings, house wares, non durable &amp; durable consumer products, hotels, motels, inns, &amp; gaming, insurance, leisure, amusement, motion pictures, entertainment, machinery, mining, steel, iron &amp; non-precious metals, oil &amp; gas, food, printing &amp; publishing, retail stores, telecommunications, money market accounts, and utilities. It provides financing in the form of senior secured term loans, mezzanine debt, and equity. It invests in both public and private companies and acquires minority or controlling interests. It also makes co-investments.
