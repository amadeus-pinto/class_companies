Blucora, Inc. provides technology enabled financial solutions to consumers, small business owners and tax professionals. The company operates through two reportable segments: Wealth Management and Tax Preparation. The Wealth Management business distributes products and services through financial advisors who affiliate with HD Vest&#x27;s subsidiaries as independent contractors. The Tax Preparation business provides digital do it yourself tax preparation solutions for consumers, small business owners, and tax professionals. The company was founded in March 1996 and is headquartered in Bellevue, WA.
