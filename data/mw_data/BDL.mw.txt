Flanigan&#x27;s Enterprises, Inc. operates as a chain of small cocktail lounges and package liquor stores. The company operates into two segments: Package Stores and Restaurants segment. The Package Stores segment is the distribution of liquors, beer and wines. The Restaurants segment refers to the operation of typical casual, standardized dining experience, typical of casual restaurant chains. The company was founded by Joseph Flanigan in 1959 and is headquartered in Fort Lauderdale, FL.
