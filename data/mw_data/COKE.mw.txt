Coca-Cola Bottling Co. Consolidated engages in the provision of producing, marketing and distributing nonalcoholic beverages. Its products include POWERade, vitaminwater, Minute Maid Juices To Go and Dasani water and other products, including Dr Pepper, Sundrop and Monster Energy. The company was founded by J. Frank Harrison III in 1980 and is headquartered in Charlotte, NC.
