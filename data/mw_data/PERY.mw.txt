Perry Ellis International, Inc. engages in the design, distribution, and licenses men&#x27;s, women&#x27;s and children&#x27;s apparel, accessories and fragrances. It offers men&#x27;s wear, including career and casual sportswear, bottoms, pants, jeans wear, golf apparel, sweaters, sports apparel, swimwear and swim accessories, active wear, outerwear and leather accessories; and women&#x27;s wear comprising dresses, sportswear, swimwear, and swim accessories. It operates its business through the following segments: Men&#x27;s Sportswear &amp; Swim, Women&#x27;s Sportswear, Direct-to-Consumer and Licensing. The Men&#x27;s Sportswear &amp; Swim segment offerings include career and casual sportswear and bottoms, dress shirts and pants, jeans wear, golf apparel, sweaters, sports apparel, swimwear and swim accessories, active wear, outerwear and leather accessories. The Women&#x27;s Sportswear segment offerings include dresses, sportswear, swimwear and swim accessories. The Direct-to-Consumer segment sells the company&#x27;s branded and licensed products through its retail stores and e-commerce platform. The Licensing segment provides licensees to the companies for using its brand names, Perry Ellis, Jantzen, John Henry, Original Penguin, Gotcha, Farah, Savane, Pro Player, Manhattan and Munsingwear. The company was founded by George Feldenkreis in 1967 and is headquartered in Miami, FL.
