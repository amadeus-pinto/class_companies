Institutional Financial Markets, Inc. is a financial services company, which engages in fixed income markets. It operates through the following business segments: Capital Markets, Principal Investing, and Asset Management. The Capital Markets segment consists of fixed income sales, trading, and matched book repo financing as well as new issue placements in corporate and securitized products and advisory services, operating primarily through its subsidiaries. The Principal Investing segment provides investments that made using own capital to support capital markets business segment. The Asset Management segment manages assets through investment vehicles, such as collateralized debt obligations, managed accounts, and investment funds. The company was founded in 1999 and is headquartered in Philadelphia, PA.
