Command Security Corp. engages in the provision of uninformed security officers and aviation security services to commercial, financial, industrial, aviation and governmental customers throughout the United States. It operates through Security, and Aviation Safeguards segments. The Security segment provides security services to governmental, quasi-governmental and financial institutions, healthcare facilities, colleges and universities, residential communities, commercial real estate, industrial, distribution, logistics and retail customers. The Aviation Safeguards segment provides aviation security and passenger assistance to domestic and international airlines, airports, airports authorities and the general aviation community. The company was founded in 1980 and is headquartered in Herndon, VA.
