Natural Alternatives International, Inc. is a formulator, manufacturer and marketer of nutritional supplements. It operates through the following segments: Private-Label Contract Manufacturing, Patent &amp; Trademark Licensing and Branded Products. The Private Label Contract Manufacturing segment provides manufacturing services to companies that market and distribute nutritional supplements and other health care products. The Patent &amp; Trademark Licensing segment primarily includes royalty income from its license and supply agreements associated with the sale and use of beta-alanine under its CarnosSyn trade name and the sale of beta-alanine raw material. The Branded Products segment relates to the marketing and distribution of its branded nutritional supplements and consists primarily of the products sold under its Pathway to Healing product line. The company was founded by Mark A. LeDoux in 1980 and is headquartered in Carlsbad, CA.
