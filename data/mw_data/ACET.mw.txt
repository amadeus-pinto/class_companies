Aceto Corp. engages in the development, marketing, sales, and distribution of chemical compounds used in the pharmaceutical, nutraceutical, agricultural, coatings, and industrial chemical industries. It operates through the following segments: Human Health, Pharmaceutical Ingredients and Performance Chemicals. The Human Health segment develops and sells finished dosage form generic drugs and nutraceutical products. The Pharmaceutical Ingredients segment supplies active pharmaceutical ingredients and pharmaceutical intermediates. The Performance Chemicals segment comprises of specialty chemicals and agricultural protection products. The company was founded in 1947 and is headquartered in Port Washington, NY.
