Mitcham Industries, Inc. engages in the lease and sale of geophysical and other equipment used primarily by seismic data acquisition contractors to perform seismic data acquisition surveys on land in transition zones and deep water marine areas. It operates through the Equipment Leasing and Manufacturing segment. The Equipment Leasing segment engages in the leasing of seismic equipment to companies in the oil and gas industry throughout the world. The Manufacturing segment engages in the design, production and sale of marine seismic equipment. The company was founded on January 29, 1987 and is headquartered in Huntsville, TX.
