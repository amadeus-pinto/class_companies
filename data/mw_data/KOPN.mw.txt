Kopin Corp. invents, develops, manufactures and sells wearable technologies which include components and systems. Its portfolio includes ultra-small displays, optics, speech enhancement technology system, and hands-free control software, low-power ASICs, and smart headset reference systems. It uses semiconductor material technology to design, manufacture and market its component products for use in military, enterprise and consumer electronic applications, training and simulation equipment and 3D metrology equipment. Kopin was founded by John C.C. Fan on April 23, 1984 and is headquartered in Westborough, MA.
