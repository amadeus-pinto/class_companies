ABM Industries, Inc. engages in the provision of facility services for commercial, industrial, and institutional buildings. It operates trough the following segments: Business and Industry, Aviation, Emerging Industries Group, Technical Solutions, and Government Services. The Business and Industry segment offers janitorial, facilities engineering, and parking services to commercial real estate industries. The Aviation segment includes services supporting airlines and airports. The Emerging Industries Group segment encompasses janitorial, facilities engineering, and parking services for education, high tech, and healthcare industries. The Technical Solutions segment involves in providing mechanical and electrical services. The Government Services segment comprises of construction management; energy efficiency upgrades; healthcare support; leadership development; military base operations, and mission support services. The company was founded by Morris Rosenberg in 1909 and is headquartered in New York, NY.
