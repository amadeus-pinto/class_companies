ON Semiconductor Corp. is a supplier of silicon solutions for energy electronics, which engages in the design, manufacture, and market portfolio of semiconductor components that address the design needs of electronic systems and products. It operates through the following business segments: Application Products Group, Image Sensor Group, Standard Products Group, and System Solutions Group. The Application Products Group segment designs and develops analog, mixed-signal, and advanced logic application-specific integrated circuit and application specific standard product solutions for a broad base of end-users in the automotive, consumer electronics, computing, industrial, communications, medical and military or aerospace markets. The Image Sensor Group segment designs and develops complementary metal oxide semiconductor and charge-coupled device image sensors and image processors for a broad base of end-users in the automotive, industrial, consumer electronics, wireless, medical, and military or aerospace markets. The Standard Products Group segment offers discrete and integrated semiconductor products that perform multiple application functions, including power switching, signal conditioning, circuit protection, signal amplification and voltage reference functions. The System Solutions Group segment comprises of analog and mixed signal integrated circuits, digital signal processor, analog and digital tuners, intelligent power modules, memory and discrete semiconductors for the automotive, communications, consumer and industrial end-markets. The company was founded on August 4, 1999 and is headquartered in Phoenix, AZ.
