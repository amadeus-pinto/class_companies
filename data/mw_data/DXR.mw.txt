Daxor Corp. is a biotechnology company. It develops and manufactures BVA-100 blood volume analyzer, an instrument that measures human blood volume. The company&#x27;s IDANT division provides autologous blood storage; and andrology services, such as semen analysis, sperm washing and general lab testing. Daxor was founded by Joseph Feldschuh in 1970 and is headquartered in New York, NY.
