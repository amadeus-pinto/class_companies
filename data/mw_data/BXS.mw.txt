BancorpSouth, Inc. is a financial holding company, which engages in the banking activities through, BancorpSouth Bank. It operates through the following segments: Community Banking, Insurance Agencies and General Corporate and Other. The Community Banking involves in providing deposit products, commercial loans, and consumer loans. The Insurance Agencies segment includes lines of property, casualty, life, health, and employee benefit products and services. The General Corporate and Other segment offers leasing, mortgage lending, and trust services; credit card activities; and investment services. The company was founded in 1982 and is headquartered in Tupelo, MS.
