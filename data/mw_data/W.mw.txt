Wayfair, Inc. operates an online home furnishing store. It offers a selection of home furnishings and decor across all styles and price points. The company operates e-commerce business model, which offers visually inspiring browsing, compelling merchandising and easy product discovery for various products across five distinct brands Wayfair.com, Joss &amp; Main, AllModern, DwellStudio and Birch Lane. Wayfair was founded by Steven K. Conine and Niraj S. Shah in May 2002 and is headquartered in Boston, MA.
