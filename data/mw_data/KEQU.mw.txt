Kewaunee Scientific Corp. engages in designing, manufacturing, and installing of laboratory, technical, and laminate furniture products. It operates its business through the Domestic and International segments. The Domestic business segment principally designs, manufactures, and installs scientific and technical furniture, including steel and wood laboratory cabinetry, fume hoods, laminate casework, flexible systems, work surfaces, workstations, workbenches, and computer enclosures. The International business segment provides the company&#x27;s products and services, including facility design, detailed engineering, construction, and project management from the planning stage through testing and commissioning of laboratories. The company was founded in 1906 and is headquartered in Statesville, NC.
