Lands&#x27; End, Inc. is a multi-channel retailer of casual clothing, accessories and footwear, as well as home products. The company operates its business through two segments: Direct and Retail. The Direct segment sells its products through its U.S. and international e-commerce websites and via direct mail catalogs. The Retail segment sells products and services through standalone Lands&#x27; End Inlet stores and dedicated Lands&#x27; End Shops at Sears across the United States. Lands&#x27; End was founded by Gary C. Comer in 1963 and is headquartered in Dodgeville, WI.
