Stock Yards Bancorp, Inc. is a holding company, which engages in the provision of commercial and personal banking services through its subsidiary. It also offers secured loans, including commercial lines of credit, commercial term loans, real estate, construction, home equity, consumer and other loans. It operates through Commercial Banking and Investment Management &amp; Trust segments. The Commercial Banking segment provides a full range of loan and deposit products to individual consumers and businesses. The Investment Management and Trust segment offers wealth management services including investment management, trust and estate administration, and retirement plan services. The company was founded in 1988 and is headquartered in Louisville, KY.
