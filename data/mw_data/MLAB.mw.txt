Mesa Laboratories, Inc. engages in the designing, manufacturing, and marketing instruments and disposable products utilized in healthcare, pharmaceutical, food, and beverage, medical device, and petrochemical industries. It operates through the following segments: Instruments, Biological Indicators, Continuous Monitoring, and Cold Chain. The Instrument segment involves in manufacturing and marketing control instruments and disposable products. The Biological Indicators segment offers producing, selling, and distributing biological and chemical indicators used to assess the effectiveness of sterilization processes. The Continuous Monitoring segments includes designing, developing, and marketing systems which are used to monitor environmental parameters such as temperature, humidity and differential pressure. The Cold Chain comprises of consulting services such as compliance monitoring, packaging development, and validation or mapping of transport and storage containers, and thermal packaging products. The company was founded by Luke R. Schmieder on March 26, 1982 and is headquartered in Lakewood, CO.
