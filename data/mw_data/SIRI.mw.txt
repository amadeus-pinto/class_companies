Sirius XM Holdings, Inc. is a radio company. The company offers commercial free music; premier sports and live events; news and comedy; exclusive talk and entertainment and a variety of latin music, sports, and talk programming. Its brand channels include SiriusXM Traffic, SiriusXM Travel Link, NavTraffic, NavWeather, SiriusXM Aviation and SiriusXM Marine. The company was founded in 1990 and is headquartered in New York, NY
