Monro Muffler Brake, Inc. engages in the provision of automotive undercar repair, maintenance, and tire services. Its products include air condition system, fluids, brakes, exhaust system, mufflers, shocks and struts, steering and suspension, batteries, wiper blades, and wheel alignment. It offers oil change, state inspection, tune-ups, wheel alignment, and flat tire repair services. The company was founded by Charles J. August in 1957 and is headquartered in Rochester, NY.
