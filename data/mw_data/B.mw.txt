Barnes Group, Inc. engages in the manufacture of engineered products and solutions for the transportation, communication, manufacturing, and technology industries. It operates through the Industrial and Aerospace segments. The Industrial segment designs and produces customized hot runner systems, precision mold assemblies, mechanical springs, nitrogen gas products, and retention rings. The Aerospace segment offers precision-machined and fabricated components and assemblies for original equipment manufacturer turbine engine, airframe, and industrial gas turbine builders. The company was founded by Wallace Barnes in 1857 and is headquartered in Bristol, CT.
