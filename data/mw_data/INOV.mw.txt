Inovalon Holdings, Inc. is a leading technology company providing cloud-based platforms empowering a data-driven transformation from volume-based to value-based models throughout the healthcare industry. It offers large-scale data interconnectivity capabilities, unparalleled proprietary data sets, advanced analytics, data-driven intervention systems, and industry-leading subject matter expertise. The company engages in the assessment and improvement of clinical and quality outcomes and financial performance across the healthcare ecosystem. Inovalon Holdings was founded on September 11, 2014 and is headquartered in Bowie, MD.
