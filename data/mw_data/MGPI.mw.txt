MGP Ingredients, Inc. is a holding company, which engages in the manufacture and trade of food, beverage, and household products, through its subsidiaries. It operates through the Distillery Products and Ingredient Solutions segments. The Distillery Products segment involves in processing corn and grains into food grade alcohol, corn oil, fuel grade alcohol, and distillers feed. The Ingredient Solutions segment comprises of producing and distributing wheat starches and wheat proteins. The company was founded in 2011 and is headquartered in Atchison, KS.
