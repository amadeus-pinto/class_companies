VASCO Data Security International, Inc. engages in the design, development, and markets security solutions that secure and manage access to digital assets and protect transactions. It also provides Two-Factor Authentication and Electronic Signature solutions to financial institutions. Two-factor authentication (also known as 2FA) strengthens the process of verifying the identity of users by means of a combination of two different components. The company was founded by T. Kendall Hunt in 1991 and is headquartered in Oakbrook Terrace, IL
