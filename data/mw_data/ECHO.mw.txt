Echo Global Logistics, Inc. engages in the provision of technology enabled transportation and supply chain management services, delivered on a proprietary technology platform serving the transportation and logistics needs of its clients. Its web-based technology, dedicated service teams and robust procurement power enables its clients to see significant transportation savings while receiving best-in-class services; and it also focuses on transportation arrangements across truckload and less than truck load, and also offers small parcel, inter-modal, domestic air, expedited and international transportation services. The company was founded by Bradley A. Keywell and Eric P. Lefkofsky in January 2005 and is headquartered in Chicago, IL.
