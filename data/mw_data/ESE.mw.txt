ESCO Technologies, Inc. is a producer of engineered products and systems company, which engages in the provision of utility, industrial, aerospace and commercial applications. It operates through the Filtration/Fluid Flow and RF Shielding and Test segments. The Filtration/Fluid Flow segment involves in the design and manufacture specialty filtration products including hydraulic filter elements and fluid control device, through PTI Technologies Inc., VACCO Industries, Crissair, Inc., and Thermoform Engineered Quality LLC. The RF Shielding and Test segment offers customers to identify, measure, and contain magnetic, electromagnetic, and acoustic energy, through ETS-Lindgren Inc. The company was founded in August 1990 and is headquartered in St. Louis, MO.
