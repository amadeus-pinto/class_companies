Health Insurance Innovations, Inc. is a developer, distributor and virtual administrator of affordable individual and family health insurance plans and supplemental products. The company sells individual and family insurance plans that include short-term medical insurance plans and hospital indemnity plans. It also offer a variety of additional insurance and non-insurance products such as pharmacy benefit cards, dental plans, vision plans, cancer &amp; critical illness plans, deductible and gap protection plans and life insurance policies that are frequently purchased as supplements to IFP. The company was founded by Michael W. Kosloske in 2008 and is headquartered in Tampa, FL.
