IRIDEX Corp. engages in the development and commercialization of products and procedures used to treat sight-threatening eye conditions. Its products consist of laser consoles, delivery devices and consumable instrumentation, including laser probes.  It operates through the following geographical segments: United State, Europe, Rest of Americas, and Asia/Pacific Rim. he company was founded by Eduardo Arias, Theodore A. Boutacoff, and James L. Donovan in February 1989 and is headquartered in Mountain View, CA.
