Delta Natural Gas Co., Inc. engages in the distribution and transportation of natural gas. It operates its business through following segments: Regulated and Non-Regulated. The Regulated segment serves residential, commercial and industrial customers. In addition, it transports natural gas to industrial customers on their system who purchase their natural gas in the open market. It also transports natural gas on behalf of local producers and other customers not on their distribution system. The Non-Regulated segment participates in related ventures, consisting of natural gas marketing, natural gas production and sales of natural gas liquids. Delta Natural Gas was founded on October 7, 1949 and is headquartered in Winchester, KY.
