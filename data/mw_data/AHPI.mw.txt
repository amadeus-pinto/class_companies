Allied Healthcare Products, Inc. manufactures a variety of respiratory products used in the health care industry in a range of hospital and alternate site settings, including sub-acute care facilities, home health care and emergency medical care. Its products include respiratory care products, medical gas equipment, and emergency medical products. The respiratory care products comprise respiratory care, anesthesia and home respiratory care products. The medical gas equipment includes construction products Regulation Devices, Disposable Cylinders and suction equipment. The emergency medical products comprises of Respiratory/Resuscitation products, Trauma and Patient Handling Products. The company sells its products primarily to hospitals, hospital equipment dealers, hospital construction contractors, home health care dealers, emergency medical products dealers, and others. Allied Healthcare Products was founded in 1979 and is headquartered in St. Louis, MO.
