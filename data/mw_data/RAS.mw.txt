RAIT Financial Trust engages in the provision of set of debt financing options to the commercial real estate industry and provide asset and property management services. It operates through the following segments: Real Estate Lending, Owning and Managing; Independence Realty Trust, Inc. (IRT), and Taberna Securitization. The Real Estate Lending, Owning and Managing segment offers lending, owning, and managing of commercial real estate assets. The IRT segment includes ownership of apartment properties. The Taberna Securitization segment involves in the ownership and management of real estate trust preferred securitizations. The company was founded by Betsy Z. Cohen in August 1997 and is headquartered in Philadelphia, PA.
