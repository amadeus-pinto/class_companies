Synacor, Inc. provides technology based services. The company is the technology development, multiplatform services and revenue partner for video, Internet and communications providers, and device manufacturers. Its technology offers solutions by providing their consumers access to online products and services. Its customers offer the company&#x27;s services under their own brands on Internet-enabled devices such as PCs, tablets, smartphones and connected TVs. The company was founded by George G. Chamoun in January 1998 and is headquartered in Buffalo, NY.
