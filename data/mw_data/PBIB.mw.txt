Porter Bancorp, Inc. operates as a bank holding company for PBI Bank, Inc. It is community bank with commercial and personal banking products, including wealth management and trust services, and an innovative on-line bank which delivers competitive deposit products and services through an on-line banking division operating under the name of Ascencia. The company provides financial services through its offices in Central Kentucky and Louisville. Its primary deposit products are checking, savings, and term certificate accounts, and its primary lending products are residential mortgage, commercial, and real estate loans. Porter Bancorp was founded by Maria L. Bouvette in 1988 and is headquartered in Louisville, KY.
