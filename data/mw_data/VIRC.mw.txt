Virco Manufacturing Corp. is engaged in the designing, production and distribution of furniture for the commercial and education markets. It manufactures wide assortment of products, including mobile tables, mobile storage equipment, desks, computer furniture, chairs, activity tables, folding chairs and folding tables. The company manufactures its products in Torrance, California, and Conway, Arkansas, for sale primarily in the United States. Virco Manufacturing was founded on February, 1950 and is headquartered in Torrance, CA.
