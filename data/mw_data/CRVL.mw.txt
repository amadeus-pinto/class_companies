CorVel Corp. engages in managed care services. The firm provides worker&#x27;s compensation solutions for employers, third party administrators, insurance companies, and government agencies seeking to control costs and promote positive outcomes. Its services include claims management, bill review, preferred provider networks, utilization management, case management, pharmacy services, directed care, and Medicare services. The company was founded by V. Clemons Gordon in 1987 and is headquartered in Irvine, CA.
