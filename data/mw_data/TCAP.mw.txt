Triangle Capital Corp. is a traded business development company that invests in lower middle market companies located in the US. The fund focuses on companies with revenues of $20 to $200 million and EBITDA of $3 to $35 million. It targets companies operating in the fields of manufacturing, distribution, transportation, energy, communications, health services, restaurants, and others. It provides financing for acquisitions, LBOs, MBOs, growth, mezzanine, debt, recapitalizations, and ESOPs. It provides financing in the form of subordinated debt with warrants, first and second lien loans, one-stop and uni-tranche structures, with an investment size ranging from $5 to $35 million. It also makes co-investments.
