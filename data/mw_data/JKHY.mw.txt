Jack Henry &amp; Associates, Inc. engages in the provision of computer systems and data processing services. It operates through the Bank Systems &amp; Services and Credit Union Systems &amp; Services segments. The company offers core solutions, retail delivery, online and mobile banking, imaging and payment solutions, information security, and risk management activities. Jack Henry &amp; Associates was founded by Jerry D. Hall and John W. Henry in 1976 and is headquartered in Monett, MO.
