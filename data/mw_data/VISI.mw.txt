Volt Information Sciences, Inc. provides staffing services, managed service programs, technology outsourcing services and information technology infrastructure services. The company operates its business through following segments: Staffing Services and Other. The Staffing Services segment consist of workforce solutions that include providing contingent workers, personnel recruitment services, and managed staffing services programs supporting primarily professional administration, technical, information technology and engineering positions. The Other segment consists of its information technology and telecommunication infrastructure and security businesses, as well as its Uruguay telephone directory publishing and printing business. Volt Information Sciences was founded by Shaw Jerome and Shaw William in 1950 and is headquartered in New York, NY.
