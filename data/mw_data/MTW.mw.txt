The Manitowoc Co., Inc. designs, manufactures and distributes a line of crawler mounted lattice boom cranes, under the Manitowoc brand name. The company also designs and manufactures a line of top slewing and self erecting tower cranes, under the Potain brand name; mobile telescopic cranes, under the Grove brand name; and a line of hydraulically powered telescopic boom trucks, under the National Crane brand name. It also provides crane product parts and services and crane rebuilding, remanufacturing and training services, which are delivered under the Manitowoc Crane Care brand name. The company was founded in 1902 and is headquartered in Manitowoc, WI.
