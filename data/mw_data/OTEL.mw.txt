Otelco, Inc. is a telecommunications company, which provides telecommunications services on a retail and wholesale basis. Its services include local and long distance calling; network access to and from customers; data transport; digital high-speed and dial-up Internet access; cable, satellite and Internet protocol television; wireless; and other telephone related services. The company also resells satellite services for DirecTV; engages in the rental of fiber to transport data and other telecommunications services in Maine. It operates through three divisions: Alabama, Missouri and New England. Otelco was founded in 1998 and is headquartered in Oneonta, AL.
