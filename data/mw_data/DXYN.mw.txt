The Dixie Group, Inc. engages in the manufacture, market, and sale of carpet, rugs, and luxury vinyl tiles. The firm also provides manufacturing support to its carpet businesses through its separate processing operations. It offers the following brands: Fabrica, Masland Residential, Dixie Home, Atlas Carpet Mills, Masland Contract, and Avant Contract. The Fabrica brand supplies carpet and custom rugs to residential markets. The Masland Residential brand covers design-driven carpet and rugs for the high-end residential marketplace. The Dixie Home brand markets differentiated products to the the more moderately priced sector of the high-end broadloom carpet market.  The Atlas Carpet Mills brand designs and manufactures broadloom and carpet tile products. The Masland Contract provides brand styled products to the high-end commercial marketplace through the architectural and specified communities and directly to corporate end-users. The Avant Contract retails products for the architectural and interior design markets. The company was founded in 1920 and is headquartered in Dalton, GA.
