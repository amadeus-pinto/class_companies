MarineMax, Inc. engages in the provision of boating-related activities. It is involved in the sale of used and new recreational boats through retail store such as sport boats, sport cruisers, sport yachts, and fishing boats. It offers marine engines and propellers, primarily to retail customers as replacements for their existing engines, and propellers, as well as a variety of parts and accessories. It also provides services which include maintenance, repair, slip, and storage services. The company was founded in January 1998 and is headquartered in Clearwater, FL.
