Bankrate, Inc. operates as a publisher and aggregator and distributor of personal finance content on the Internet. The company provides consumers with proprietary, researched, comprehensive, independent and objective personal finance editorial content across multiple vertical categories including mortgages, deposits, insurance, credit cards, and other categories, such as retirement, automobile loans and taxes. It operates through the following segments: Banking, Credit Cards and Insurance. The Banking segment offers information on rates for various types of mortgages, home lending and refinancing options. The Credit Cards segment consumer and business credit and prepaid cards, provides detailed information and comparison capabilities, and host news and advice on personal finance, credit card and bank policies, as well as tools and calculators to estimate credit scores and card benefits. The Insurance segment provides advice and detailed descriptions of insurance terms, and produce articles on topical subjects. The company was founded in 1976 and is headquartered in New York, NY.
