Tel-Instrument Electronics Corp. engages in the designing and manufacturing of avionics test and measurement solutions. It operates through the Avionics Government and Avionics Commercial segments. The Avionics Government segment involves in the design, manufacture, and sale of test equipment. The Avionics Commercial segment includes design, manufacture, and sale of test equipment to domestic and foreign airlines, directly or through commercial distributors, and to general aviation repair and maintenance shops. The company was founded in 1947 and is headquartered in East Rutherford, NJ.
