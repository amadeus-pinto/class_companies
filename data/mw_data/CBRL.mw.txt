Cracker Barrel Old Country Store, Inc. engages in the operation and development of restaurant and retail concepts. It operates restaurants under the Cracker Barrel Old Country Store name. Its offers breakfast, lunch, dinner, kid&#x27;s and dessert menu, daily specials, meals to-go-carry-out, cracker barrel by mail, restaurant virtual tour, menu de desayuno, menu de almuerzo y cena, para los ninos, and Postres. The company was founded by Dan W. Evins on September 19, 1969 and is headquartered in Lebanon, TN.
