AstroNova, Inc. develops, manufactures, and sells specialty printing systems for rugged military and aerospace applications and for packaging identification applications. It also develops, manufactures, and sells high-quality portable data acquisition systems. The company markets these products under the brand names Astro-Med Test &amp; Measurement and QuickLabel Systems. It operates through two business segments: Test &amp; Measurement and QuickLabel Systems. The Test &amp; Measurement segment produces data recording equipment used worldwide for a variety of recording, monitoring and troubleshooting applications for the aerospace, automotive, metal mill, power and telecommunications industries. The QuickLabel System segment produces an array of high-technology digital label printers, labeling software and consumables for a variety of commercial industries worldwide. The company was founded by Albert W. Ondis on January 9, 1969 and is headquartered in West Warwick, RI.
