Portland General Electric Co. is a vertically integrated electric utility company which engages in the generation, purchase, transmission, distribution, and retail sale of electricity in the state of Oregon. It also sells electricity and natural gas in the wholesale market to utilities, brokers, and power marketers. It serves residential customers, including single family housing, multiple family housing, commercial customers, including non-residential customers, such as small industrial companies. The company was founded in 1888 and is headquartered in Portland, OR.
