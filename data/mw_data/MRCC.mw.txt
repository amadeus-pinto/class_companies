Monroe Capital Corp. is a business development company that invests in middle-market and lower middle-market companies located in the US and Canada with an EBITDA of between $3 million and $50 million and revenues of between $20 million and $500 million. The fund invests across a broad range of sectors including healthcare &amp; pharmaceuticals, business services, media, advertising, printing &amp; publishing and automotive. It provides senior, junior, unitranche secured debt, unsecured subordinated debt and equity co-investments for recapitalizations and acquisition requirements with an investment size ranging between $2 million and $18 million each. It makes minority equity co-investments.
