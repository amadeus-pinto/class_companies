InterGroup Corp. engages in buying, developing, rehabilitating, and disposing real properties. It operates through the following segments: Hotel Operations, Real Estate Operations, and Investment Transactions. The Hotel Operations segment consists of the operation of the hotel and garage. The Real Estate Operations segment offers rental properties. The Investment Transactions segment involves in the investment of its cash in marketable securities. The company was founded in 1985 and is headquartered in Los Angeles, CA.
