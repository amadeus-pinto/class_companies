Viad Corp. engages in the provision of marketing services, and travel and recreation services. It operates its business through the following groups: Marketing and Events Group; and Travel and Recreation. The Marketing and Events Group is divided into reportable segments based on geography: The Marketing and Events U.S. Segment and Marketing and Events International Segment. The Marketing and Events U. S. segment has full-service operations in every major exhibition market in the United States. The Marketing and Events International segment includes all foreign operations. The Travel and Recreation group offers tourism products and experiential services, including world-class attractions, hotel and concession operations, transportation services and package tour operations. The company was founded in 1996 and is headquartered in Phoenix, AZ.
