Silicon Laboratories, Inc. provides silicon, software, and solutions for the Internet of Things, Internet infrastructure, industrial control, consumer, and automotive markets. Its products include microcontroller, wireless, sensors, interface, USB bridges, analog, clocks and oscillators, isolators, power, audio and radio, modems and Direct Access Arrangements, television and video, and voice. The company was founded by Navdeep S. Sooch, David R. Welland, and Jeffrey W. Scott in August 1996 and is headquartered in Austin, TX.
