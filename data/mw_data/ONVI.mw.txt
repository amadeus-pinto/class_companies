Onvia, Inc. provides business information and research solutions that help companies plan, market and sell to targeted public sector buyers throughout the United States. The company&#x27;s business solution offers clients online access to a proprietary database of government procurement opportunities across the federal, state, local, and education sectors. Its solutions include access to the Onvia Online Database, Spending Forecast Center, and the Onvia Guide. The company was founded in 1996 and is headquartered in Seattle, WA.
