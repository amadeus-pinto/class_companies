Smart &amp; Final Stores, Inc. is a value-oriented food and everyday staples retailer that serves household and business customers. The company operates through following segments: Smart &amp; Final and Cash &amp; Carry. The Smart and Final segment focuses on both household and business customers. The Cash and Carry segment focuses primarily on restaurants, caterers and a wide range of other foodservice businesses. Smart &amp; Final Stores was founded on October 5, 2012 and is headquartered in Commerce, CA.
