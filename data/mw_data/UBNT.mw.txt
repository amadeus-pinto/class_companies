Ubiquiti Networks, Inc. engages in the development of networking technology for service providers and enterprises. The firms&#x27; product platforms provide wireless local area network infrastructure, video surveillance products, voice over internet protocol phones, switches, and machine to machine communication components. Its technology platforms include airMAX, airFiber, EdgeMAX, UniFi, UniFi Video and mFi. The company was founded by Robert J. Pera in September 2003 and is headquartered in San Jose, CA.
