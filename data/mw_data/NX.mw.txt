Quanex Building Products Corp. manufactures components sold to original equipment manufacturers in the building products industry. It also designs and produces energy-efficient fenestration products in addition to kitchen and bath cabinet components. The company operates through the following segments: North American Engineered Components, European Engineered Components, North American Cabinet Components, and Unallocated Corporate &amp; Others. The North American Engineered Components segment manufactures vinyl profiles, insulating glass spacers, screens and other fenestration components. The European Engineered Components segment manufactures vinyl profiles and conservatories. This segment also includes European insulating glass business, which manufactures IG spacers. The North American Cabinet Components segment comprises cabinet door and components business. The company was founded on April 23, 2008 and is headquartered in Houston, TX.
