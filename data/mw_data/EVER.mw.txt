EverBank Financial Corp. operates as a bank holding company for EverBank, which engages in the provision of banking and financial services. It operates its business through three segments: Consumer Banking, Commercial Banking and Corporate Services. The Consumer Banking includes consumer deposit services and activities, residential lending and servicing, wealth management and capital markets. The Commercial Banking includes commercial deposit services and activities, commercial and commercial real estate lending, lender finance, equipment finance and mortgage warehouse finance. The Corporate Services segment provides support services to the Consumer and Commercial Banking segments. The company was founded in 2004 and is headquartered in Jacksonville, FL.
