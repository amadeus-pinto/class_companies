FTI Consulting, Inc. engages in the provision of business advisory services. It operates through the following segments: Corporate Finance &amp; Restructuring; Forensic and Litigation Consulting; Economic Consulting; Technology; and Strategic Communications. The Corporate Finance &amp; Restructuring segment offers consultancy services on bankruptcy, interim management, mergers and acquisitions integration, valuations, and tax issues. The Forensic and Litigation Consulting segment provides dispute advisory investigations, forensic accounting, business intelligence assessments, data analytics, compliance and monitoring, business insurance claims, and risk mitigation. The Economic Consulting segment deals with issues in antitrust and competition, business valuation, intellectual property, international arbitration, labor and employment, financial litigation, and public policy. The Technology segment includes e-Discovery software, managed document review, computer forensics, information governance, and investigations. The Strategic Communications segment consists of corporate reputation, public affairs and government relations, digital communications, and employee engagement. The company was founded in 1982 and is headquartered in Washington, DC.
