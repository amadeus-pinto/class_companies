Libbey, Inc. manufactures and markets glass tableware products. It engages in design and sale of tableware and other products. The company also source glass tableware, ceramic dinnerware, metal flatware, and hollowware and serveware products. Its brands include Libbey, Libbey Signature, Masters Reserve, World Tableware, Syracuse China, Crisa Royal Leerdam and Crisal Glass. The company was founded in 1888 and is headquartered in Toledo, OH.
