Bank Mutual Corp. is a holding company that engages in the provision of banking and financial services. Its primary business is community banking, which includes attracting deposits from and making loans to the general public and private businesses, as well as governmental and non-profit entities. Its loan portfolio consists of loans to both commercial and retail borrowers. Loans to commercial borrowers include loans secured by real estate such as multi-family properties, non-residential commercial properties and construction and development projects secured by these same types of properties, as well as land, in addition, commercial loans include loans to businesses that are not secured by real estate. The company is headquartered in Milwaukee, WI.
