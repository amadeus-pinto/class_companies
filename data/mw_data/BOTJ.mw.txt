Bank of the James Financial Group, Inc.  is a bank holding company, which engages in general retails and commercial banking business. It operates through the Community Banking and Mortgage segments. The Community Banking segment includes its Bank of the James subsidiary which provides loans, deposits, investments, and insurance to retail and commercial customers. The Mortgage segment provides mortgage loan products. The company was founded on October 3, 2003 and is headquartered in Lynchburg, VA.
