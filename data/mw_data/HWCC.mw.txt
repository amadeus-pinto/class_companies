Houston Wire &amp; Cable Co. engages in the manufacture and trade of electrical and mechanical wire and cable. Its products include continuous and interlocked armor cable; control and power cable; electronic wire and cable; flexible and portable cords; instrumentation and thermocouple cable; lead and high-temperature cable; medium voltage cable; wire rope and wire rope slings; nylon slings; chains; and shackles. The company was founded in 1975 and is headquartered in Houston, TX.
