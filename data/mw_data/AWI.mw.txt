Armstrong World Industries, Inc. engages in the design, manufacture, and trade of floor and ceiling systems. It operates through the following segments: Building Products, Resilient Flooring, Wood Flooring, and Unallocated Corporate. The Building Products segment produces suspended mineral fiber, soft fiber and metal ceiling systems. The Resilient Flooring segment produces floor coverings such as vinyl sheets, linoleum, adhesives, and laminate flooring products. The Wood Flooring segment offers pre-finished solid and engineered wood floors. The Unallocated Corporate segment includes income tax accounts, cash equivalents, and administrative operations. The company was founded by Thomas M. Armstrong in 1860 and is headquartered in Lancaster, PA.
