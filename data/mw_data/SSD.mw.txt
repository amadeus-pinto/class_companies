Simpson Manufacturing Co., Inc. engages in the manufacture and trade of construction materials. It operates through the following geographical segments: North America, Europe, and Asia/Pacific. It offers wood connector, truss plates, fasteners, screw drivers, moment frames, pre-fabricated wood and steel shear walls, rod tie down solutions, anchoring systems, fiberglass jackets, and commercial clips. The company was founded by Barclay Simpson in 1956 and is headquartered in Pleasanton, CA.
