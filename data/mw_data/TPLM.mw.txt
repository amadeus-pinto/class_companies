Triangle Petroleum Corp. engages in the business of exploring oil and natural gas. The company operates through two operating segments: Exploration and Production and Oilfield Services. The Exploration and Production Operating segment is responsible for finding and producing oil and natural gas. The Oilfield Services segment is responsible for a variety of oilfield and complementary services for both Triangle-operated wells and wells operated by third-parties. Triangle Petroleum was founded on December 11, 2003 and is headquartered in Denver, CO.
