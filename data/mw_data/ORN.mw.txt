Orion Group Holdings, Inc. engages in the provision of solutions for marine construction and specialty services on, over, and under the water along the Gulf Coast, the Atlantic Seaboard, West Coast, Canada, and the Caribbean Basin. Its marine construction services include construction of marine transportation facilities, marine pipelines, bridges and causeways, and marine environmental structures. Its specialty services include salvage, demolition, diving, surveying, towing and underwater inspection, excavation and repair. The company was founded in 1994 and is headquartered in Houston, TX.
