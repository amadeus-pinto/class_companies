Chico&#x27;s FAS, Inc. engages in the retail of private label women&#x27;s apparel, accessories, and other non-clothing items. Its product portfolio consists of the following brands: Chico&#x27;s, Soma, and White House Black Market. It also operates boutiques, retail stores, and websites to sell the products. The company was founded by Helene B. Gralnick and Marvin L. Gralnick in 1983 and is headquartered in Fort Myers, FL.
