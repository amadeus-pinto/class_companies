Carolina Trust BancShares, Inc. is a bank holding company, which engages to provide financial services. The firm offers banking products and services such as savings accounts, personal and business loans, debit and credit cards, home equity line of credit, cash management, online banking, and mortgages. It operates through the following segments: commercial real estate, commercial, residential mortgage, home equity lines, and other consumer loans. The company was founded on February 29, 2016 and is headquartered in Lincolnton, NC.
