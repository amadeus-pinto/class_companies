Supreme Industries, Inc. manufactures, sells, and repairs truck bodies and related equipment. It manufactures commercial vehicles including truck bodies and specialty vehicles. Its products are the following: Signature Van Bodies, Iner-City Cutaway Van Bodies, Spartan Service Bodies, Sparta Cargo Vans, Kold King Insulated Van Bodies, Stake Bodies, Armored SUV&#x27;s, and Armored Trucks and Specialty Vehicles. It operates through Specialized Commercial Vehicles and Fiberglass Products. The company was founded by Tom Cantwell in 1974 and is headquartered in Goshen, IN.
