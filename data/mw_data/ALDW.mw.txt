Alon USA Partners LP owns and operates oil refinery and petroleum products in the south central and southwestern regions of the United States. It refines crude oil into petroleum products, including ultra-low sulfur gasoline, ultra-low sulfur diesel, jet fuel, petrochemicals, petrochemical feedstocks, asphalts, and other petroleum products, which it markets primarily in West and Central Texas, Oklahoma, New Mexico and Arizona. The company was founded in August 2012 and is headquartered in Dallas, TX.
