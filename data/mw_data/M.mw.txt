Macy&#x27;s, Inc. engages in the operation of stores, websites, and mobile applications. It operates its business under the brand names Macy&#x27;s, Bloomingdale&#x27;s, and Bluemercury, which sells merchandise, including apparel and accessories, cosmetics, home furnishings, and other consumer goods. It also provides various support functions to the company&#x27;s retail operations through its subsidiaries namely, FDS Bank; Macy&#x27;s Systems and Technology, Inc; Macy&#x27;s Merchandising Group, Inc; and Macy&#x27;s Logistics and Operations. The company was founded by Rowland Hussey Macy Sr. on March 6, 1929 and is headquartered in Cincinnati, OH.
