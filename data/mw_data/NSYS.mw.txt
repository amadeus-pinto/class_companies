Nortech Systems, Inc. is a manufacturer and service provider of electro-mechanical products. The company engages in providing cable assemblies, printed circuit, board assemblies, and complete box build assemblies for a wide range of industries. It offers value added services and technical support, including design, testing, prototyping and supply chain management to customers mainly in the aerospace and defense, medical and industrial equipment markets. The company was founded in December 1990 and is headquartered in Maple Grove, MN.
