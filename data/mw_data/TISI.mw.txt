Team, Inc. provides industrial services. Its services includes inspection and assessment, required in maintaining high temperature and high pressure piping systems and vessels that are utilized in the refining, petrochemical, power, pipeline and other heavy industries. The company operates through the following segments: Inspection and Heat Treating Services, Mechanical Services and Quest Integrity Group. The Inspection and Heat Treating Services segment offers inspection services and heat treating services. The Mechanical Services segment offers on-stream services and turnaround and project related services. The Quest Integrity Group segment provides management solutions to the energy industry. The company was founded in 1973 and is headquartered in Sugar Land, TX.
