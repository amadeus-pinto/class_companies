Horizon Bancorp operates as a bank holding company. The company through its subsidiary bank, provides commercial and retail banking services, corporate and individual trust and agency services and other services incident to banking. The bank through its subsidiaries, manages the investment portfolio of the bank and manages the real estate investment trust. It offers general services, personal banking, business banking, investment &amp; trust, and mortgage services. Horizon Bancorp was founded on April 26, 1983 and is headquartered in Michigan City, IN.
