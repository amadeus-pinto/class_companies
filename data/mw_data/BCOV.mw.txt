Brightcove Inc. engages in providing cloud services for video which enable customers to publish and distribute video to Internet-connected devices. It offers a family of products that revolutionize the way organizations deliver video experiences. The company also provides online video platform for publishing and distributing video across PCs, smartphones, tablets and Connected TVs. Brightcove was founded by Jeremy J. Allaire and Robert Mason on August 24, 2004 and is headquartered in Boston, MA.
