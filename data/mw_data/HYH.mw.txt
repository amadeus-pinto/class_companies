Halyard Health, Inc. is a medical technology company focused on preventing infection, eliminating pain and speeding recovery for healthcare providers and their patients. It operates through the following business segments: Surgical &amp; Infection Prevention and Medical Devices. The Surgical &amp; Infection Prevention segment provides healthcare supplies and solutions that target the prevention of healthcare-associated infections. This segment has recognized brands across its portfolio of product offerings, including sterilization wrap, surgical drapes and gowns, facial protection, protective apparel and medical exam gloves. It products sold under HALYARD or KIMGUARD ONE-STEP, QUICK CHECK, SMART-FOLD, POWERGUARD, MICROCOOL, FLUIDSHIELD, PURPLE NITRILE, LAVENDER, STERLING and other brand names. The Medical Devices segment provides a portfolio of innovative product offerings focused on pain management and respiratory and digestive health to improve patient outcomes and reduce the cost of care. These products include post-operative pain management solutions, minimally invasive interventional pain therapies, closed airway suction systems and eternal feeding tubes. It products sold under the ON-Q, COOLIEF, MICROCUFF, MIC-KEY, QUIKBLOC, HOMEPUMP and other brand names. Halyard Health was founded on February 25, 2014 and is headquartered in Alpharetta, GA.
