Goldman Sachs BDC, Inc., a traded business development company, seeks investment opportunities in middle market companies located in the US. The fund focuses on companies with an annual EBITDA of $5 - $75 million. It targets institutional and individual investors with investment and advisory solutions with strategies spanning asset classes, industries and geographies. It provides financing in the form of senior secured debt, unitranche debt, junior secured &amp; mezzanine debt and to a lesser extent investments in equities for acquisitions, refinancing capital for growth, leveraged buyouts, dividend recapitalization and restructuring capital requirements. Its investment size ranges from $10 to $75 million.
