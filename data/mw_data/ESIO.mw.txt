Electro Scientific Industries, Inc. supplies laser-based manufacturing solutions for the microtechnology industry. It operates through the Component Processing and Michromachining business segments. The Component Processing segment offers interconnect products, semiconductor products and component products. The Michromachining segment products are sold primarily to manufacturers of end devices across multiple industries and are used primarily to drill, cut or mark features on a variety of materials, generally on the casing or external surface of the end device. The company was founded in 1944 and is headquartered in Portland, OR.
