Atlantic American Corp. is an insurance holding company, which engages in the life, health, and property and casualty insurance industries through its subsidiary companies. It operates through the following segments: American Southern, Bankers Fidelity, and Corporate and Other. The American Southern segment involves in the property and casualty insurance market. The Bankers Fidelity segment operates in the life and health insurance market. The company was founded in 1968 and is headquartered in Atlanta, GA.
