QC Holdings, Inc. is a financial service provider. It provides financial services through its retail branches and internet lending operations. The company operates its business through three segments: Branch Lending, Centralized Lending and E-Lending. The Branch Lending segment includes its retail branches that offer payday loans, installment loans, credit services, open-end credit, check cashing services, title loans, debit cards, money transfers and money orders. The Centralized Lending segment covers long-term installment loans that are centrally underwritten. The E-Lending segment is in charge of the Internet lending operations in the United States and Canada. QC Holdings was founded by Don Early and Mary Lou Andersen Early in 1984 and is headquartered in Overland Park, KS.
