Fidelity &amp; Guaranty Life is a holding company, which engages in the provision of fixed annuity and life insurance products. Through its subsidiaries, Fidelity &amp; Guaranty Life Insurance Co. and Fidelity &amp; Guaranty Life Insurance Co. of New York, offers products distributed by independent agents through network of independent marketing organizations. The company was founded on August 3, 2010 and is headquartered in Des Moines, IA.
