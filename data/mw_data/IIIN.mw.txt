Insteel Industries, Inc. manufactures and markets steel wire reinforcing products for concrete construction applications. It also manufactures and markets prestressed concrete strand and welded wire reinforcement, including concrete pipe reinforcement and engineered structural mesh. The company&#x27;s products are sold to manufacturers of concrete products that are used in nonresidential construction. Insteel Industries was founded by Howard Osler Woltz, Jr. in 1953 and is headquartered in Mount Airy, NC.
