Mid-Con Energy Partners LP owns, operates, acquires, exploits and develops oil and natural gas properties in North America, with a focus on enhanced oil recovery. Its core areas of operation are located in Southern Oklahoma, Northeastern Oklahoma, the Gulf Coast, the Hugoton, and the Permian. The company was founded by Charles R. Olmstead, S. Craig George, and Jeffrey R. Olmstead in July 2011 and is headquartered in Tulsa, OK.
