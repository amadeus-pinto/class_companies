Acorn Energy, Inc. is a holding company, which engages in the provision of  technology driven solutions for energy infrastructure asset management through its subsidiaries. It operates through the following segments: Energy and Security Sonar Solutions; Smart Grid Distribution Automation, and Machine-to-Machine Critical Asset Monitoring and Control. The Energy and Security Sonar Solutions segment involves in providing sonar and acoustic related solutions for energy, defense, and commercial markets. The Smart Grid Distribution Automation segment offers developing, marketing, and selling remote monitoring and control systems to electric utilities and industrial facilities. The Machine-to-Machine Critical Asset Monitoring and Control segment includes remote diagnostics and prognostics that give real-time visibility of the equipment and assets. The company was founded by George Morgenstern in 1986 and is headquartered in Greenville, DE.
