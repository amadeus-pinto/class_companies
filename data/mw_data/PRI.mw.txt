Primerica, Inc. engages in the distribution of financial products to middle income households. It operates through the Term Life Insurance; and Investment and Savings Products Segments. The Term Life Insurance segment includes underwriting profits on its in-force book of term life insurance policies, net of reinsurance, which are underwritten by its life insurance company subsidiaries. The Investment and Savings Products segment includes mutual funds and variable annuities distributed through licensed broker-dealer subsidiaries and includes segregated funds, an individual annuity savings product. The company was founded by Arthur L. Williams, Jr. on February 10, 1977 and is headquartered in Duluth, GA.
