Capital Southwest Corp. is a traded fund that invests in companies with a minimum EBITDA of $3 million located in the US. The fund targets companies operating in the fields of industrial manufacturing &amp; services, value-added distribution, healthcare products &amp; services, business services, specialty chemicals, tech-enabled services &amp; SaaS models, and food &amp; beverage sectors. It provides debt and equity capital for new platform acquisitions, dividend recapitalizations, refinancings, bolt-on acquisitions, and growth financings with an investment size ranging from $5 to $20 million. It considers co-investments and it also makes non-controlling investments.
