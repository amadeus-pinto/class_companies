Tribune Media Co. is a diversified media and entertainment company. It operates through Television &amp; Entertainment segment, which provides news, entertainment and sports programming. The Television &amp; Entertainment segment comprises Tribune Broadcasting, which owns and operates local television stations; and Tribune Studios, which consists of digital applications and websites. This segment also includes Chicago&#x27;s WGN-AM and the national multicast networks Antenna TV as well as THIS TV. Tribune Media owns &amp; manages real estate properties. The company was founded in 1847 and is headquartered in Chicago, IL.
