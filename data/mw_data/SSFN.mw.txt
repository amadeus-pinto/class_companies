Stewardship Financial Corp. operates as a bank holding company. Its primary business is the ownership and supervision of the Atlantic Stewardship Bank. The company conducts a traditional commercial banking business and offers services including personal and business checking accounts and time deposits, money market accounts and regular savings accounts. It also engages in lending activities and offers commercial, consumer, residential real estate, home equity and personal loans. The company was founded in January 1995 and is headquartered in Midland Park, NJ.
