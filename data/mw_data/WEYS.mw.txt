Weyco Group, Inc. engages in the design, production, and trade of footwear for men, women, and children. It operates through the following segments: North American Wholesale, North American Retail, and Other. The North American Wholesale segment includes selling products to department stores and specialty shops primarily in the United States and Canada. The North American Retail segment operates online shops and company-owned stores in cities. The Other segment consists of the wholesale and retail operations in Australia, South Africa, Asia Pacific, and Europe. It distributes its products under the following brands: Florsheim, Nunn Bush, Stacy Adams, Bogs, Rafters, and Umi. The company was founded in 1906 and is headquartered in Glendale, WI.
