National Security Group, Inc. operates as an insurance holding company, which offers life, accident and health, supplemental hospital and cancer insurance products. The company operates through the following segments: Property &amp; Casualty Insurance and Life Insurance. The Property and Casualty Insurance segment engages in writing personal lines dwelling coverage including dwelling fire and windstorm, homeowners and mobile homeowners lines of insurance through its subsidiaries, National Security Fire and Casualty Co. and Omega One Insurance Co. The Life Insurance segment offers a basic line of life and health and accident insurance products through its subsidiary. The company was founded in 1947 and is headquartered in Elba, AL.
