Scholastic Corp. engages in the publication and market of children&#x27;s books, magazines, and teaching materials. It operates through the following segments: Children&#x27;s Book Publishing and Distribution; Educational; and International. The Children&#x27;s Book Publishing and Distribution segment produces and trades books, electronic books, media, and interactive products. The Education segment publishes and distributes books, magazines, and supplemental classroom materials to schools and libraries. The International segment refers to the firm&#x27;s international operations. The company was founded by Maurice R. Robinson in 1920 and is headquartered in New York, NY.
