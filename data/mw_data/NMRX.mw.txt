Numerex Corp. engages in providing business services, technology, and products that are used in the development and support of machine-to-machine solutions for the enterprise and government markets worldwide. It operates through the following segments: U.S. Canada, and Others. The firm offers Numerex DNA, it includes hardware and smart devices, cellular and satellite network services, and software applications that are delivered through Numerex Foundation Application Software Technology. The company was founded by Stratton J. Nicolaides in 1992 and is headquartered in Atlanta, GA.
