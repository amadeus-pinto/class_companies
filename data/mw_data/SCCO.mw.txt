Southern Copper Corp. engages in the development and exploration of mineral properties. It operates through the segments: Mexican Open-Pit; Mexican IMMSA Unit; Peruvian Operations; and Corporate and Other. The Mexican Open-Pit segment manages mines and processes anodes and copper. The Mexican IMMSA Unit segment operates underground coal mines. The Peruvian Operations segment includes open-pit mines in Peru. The Corporate and Other segment consists of the firm&#x27;s administrative activities. The company was founded on December 12, 1952 and is headquartered in Phoenix, AZ.
