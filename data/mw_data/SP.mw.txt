SP Plus Corp. engages in the provision of parking management, ground transportation, and other ancillary services to commercial, institutional, and municipal clients. Its services include a set of on-site parking management and ground transportation services, which consist of training, scheduling, and supervising all service personnel as well as customer service, marketing, maintenance, security and accounting, and revenue control functions. The company was founded in March 1998 and is headquartered in Chicago, IL.
