Global Brass &amp; Copper Holdings, Inc. is a converter, fabricator, distributor and processor of specialized copper and brass products in North America. It engages in metal melting and casting, rolling, drawing, extruding and stamping to fabricate finished, and semi finished alloy products from processed scrap, copper cathode and other refined metals. The company operates through three segments: Olin Brass, Chase Brass and A.J. Oster. The Olin Brass segment fabricates, manufactures and converts copper and brass sheet, strip, foil, tube and fabricated components in North America. It also engages in processing copper and copper-alloys, and also rerolls and forms other metals, such as stainless and carbon steel. The Chase Brass segment manufactures brass rod. This segment produces brass rod used in production applications, which can be grouped into four end markets, including building and housing, transportation, electronics/electrical components and industrial machinery and equipment. The A.J. Oster segment is a copper-alloy distributor and processor. Its operations include precision slitting, hot tinning, traverse winding, cutting and packaging. The company was founded on October 10, 2007 and is headquartered in Schaumburg, IL.
