Cheniere Energy Partners LP owns and, through its wholly owned subsidiary, SPLNG, engages in the operation of the regasification facilities at the Sabine Pass LNG terminal. It operates through a single segment, LNG Terminal Business. The LNG Terminal Business segment consists of the operational regasification and pipeline facilities at the Sabine Pass LNG terminal and the adjacent Liquefaction Project The company was founded by Charif Souki on October 20, 2003 and is headquartered in Houston, TX.
