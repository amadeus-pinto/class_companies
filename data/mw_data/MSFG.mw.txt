MainSource Financial Group, Inc. operates as financial holding company. Through its subsidiaries, it offers the following financial services: time and transaction deposits; making consumer, commercial, agribusiness and real estate mortgage loans; renting safe deposit facilities; providing personal and corporate trust services; and providing other corporate services, such as letters of credit and repurchase agreements. The company was founded by Robert E. Hoptry in 1982 and is headquartered in Greensburg, IN.
