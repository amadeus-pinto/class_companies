Ocean Bio-Chem, Inc. is a consumer products company. It manufactures, markets and distributes appearance, performance and maintenance products under the Star brite and Star Tron brand names. The company also manufactures and distributes disinfectant, sanitizing and deodorizing products under the Performacide and Star brite brand names. It serves the marine, automotive, power sports, recreational vehicle and outdoor power equipment markets. The company was founded by Peter G. Dornau and Arthur Spector on November 13, 1973 and is headquartered in Fort Lauderdale, FL.
