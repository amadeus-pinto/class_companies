Western Refining Logistics LP owns, operates, develops and acquires terminals, storage tanks, pipelines and other logistics assets. It has pipeline and gathering assets, and terminalling, transportation and storage assets. The company generates revenue primarily by charging fees and tariffs for transporting crude oil and refined and other products through its terminals and pipelines. It operates through two business segments: logistics and wholesale. The logistics assets consist of pipeline and gathering infrastructure and terminalling, transportation and storage assets in the Southwestern portion of the U.S. The wholesale segment operates a fleet of crude oil trucks which are utilized to support crude oil supply options for Western&#x27;s El Paso and Gallup Refineries. Western Refining Logistics was founded on July 17, 2013 and is headquartered in El Paso, TX.
