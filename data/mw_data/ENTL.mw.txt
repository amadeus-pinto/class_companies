Entellus Medical, Inc. is a medical technology company focused on the design, development and commercialization of products for the treatment of chronic sinusitis. Its XprESS family of products is used by ear, nose and throat, physicians to open narrowed or obstructed sinus drainage pathways using balloon sinus dilation to treat patients with symptomatic inflammation of the nasal sinuses. Entellus Medical was founded by Thomas V. Ressemann and Peter Keith in April 2006 and is headquartered in Plymouth, MN.
