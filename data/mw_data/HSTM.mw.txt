HealthStream, Inc. engages in the provision of healthcare industry. It involves in the research and development of healthcare products and services. It operates through the HealthStream Workforce Development Solutions; and HealthStream Research and Patient Experience Solutions segments. The HealthStream Workforce Development Solutions segment offers training, assessment, talent development, and talent management solutions together with the administrative and management tools which helps modifying training materials, documenting training completion and facilitating competency, and performance appraisals. The HealthStream Research Experience Solutions segment provides hospital-based customers with patient insights, employee insights, physician insights, and community insights surveys, data analyses of survey results, and other research-based measurement tools. The company was founded by Robert A. Frist, Jr. and Jeffery L. Mclaren in 1990 and is headquartered in Nashville, TN.
