Old National Bancorp is a financial holding company engages in the provision of financial and banking solutions. It operates through the Banking and Insurance segments. The Banking segment provides a range of services including lending and depository activities, as well as private banking brokerage, trust and investment advisory services, and credit life, property, and casualty insurance services. The Insurance segment provides full-service insurance brokerage services including commercial property, and casualty, surety, loss control services, employee benefits, consulting and administration, and personal insurance. The company was founded in 1982 and is headquartered in Evansville, IN.
