Patriot National, Inc. engages in the provision of outsourcing solutions within the workers compensation marketplace for insurance companies, employers, local governments, and reinsurance captives. It includes front-end, such as brokerage, underwriting, policyholder, and back-end services, such as claims adjudication and administration. The company was founded by Steven Michael Mariano on November 15, 2013 and is headquartered in Fort Lauderdale, FL.
