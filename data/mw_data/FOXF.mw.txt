Fox Factory Holding Corp. engages in designing, engineering, manufacturing, and marketing performance ride dynamics products. Its categories include bike and powered vehicles, including side-by-sides, on-road vehicles with off-road capabilities, off-road vehicles and trucks, all-terrain vehicles, snowmobiles, specialty vehicles, applications, and motorcycles. The company was founded on December 28, 2007 and is headquartered in Scotts Valley, CA.
