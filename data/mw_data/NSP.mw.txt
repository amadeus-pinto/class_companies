Insperity, Inc. provides an array of human resources and business solutions designed to help improve business performance. The company&#x27;s HR business provides through its professional employer organization services, known as workforce optimization, which encompasses of HR functions, including payroll and employment administration, employee benefits, workers compensation, government compliance, performance management, and training and development services. It offers midmarket solutions, performance management, expense management, time and attendance, organizational planning, recruiting services, employment screening, retirement services and business insurance services. Insperity was founded by Paul J. Sarvadi in April 1986 and is headquartered in Kingwood, TX.
