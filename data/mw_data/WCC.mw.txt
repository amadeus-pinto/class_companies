WESCO International, Inc. engages in the provision of electrical, industrial, and communications maintenance, repair &amp; operating, and original equipment manufacturers products, construction materials, and advanced supply chain management and logistics services. Its products include: general electrical and industrial supplies, wire, cable and conduit, data and broadband communications, power distribution equipment, lighting and lighting control systems, control and automation, motors and safety. The company was founded in 1993 and is headquartered in Pittsburgh, PA.
