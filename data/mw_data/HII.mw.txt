Huntington Ingalls Industries, Inc. engages in the shipbuilding business. It operates through the following segments: Ingalls, Newport News, and Other. The Ingalls segment develops and constructs non-nuclear ships, assault ships, and surface combatants. The Newport News segment designs, builds, and maintains nuclear-powered ships which include aircraft carriers and submarines. The Other segment pertains to the firm&#x27;s non-shipbuilding commercial activities. The company was founded on August 4, 2010 and is headquartered in Newport News, VA.
