Atlanticus Holdings Corp. is a financial holding company. It offers financial products and services through its subsidiaries. The company operates its business through two reportable segments: Credit Cards and Other Investments; and Auto Finance. The Credit Cards and Other Investments segment includes its continuing activities relating to investments in and servicing of its various credit card receivables portfolios, as well as other investments and credit products that generally use much of the same infrastructure as its credit card operations. The Auto Finance segment purchase and/or service loans secured by automobiles from or for a pre-qualified network of independent automotive dealers and automotive finance companies in the buy-here, pay-here, used car business. Atlanticus Holdings was founded by David G. Hanna in August 1996 and is headquartered in Atlanta, GA.
