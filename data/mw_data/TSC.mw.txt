TriState Capital Holdings, Inc. engages in the provision of commercial banking, private banking and investment management services. It operates through the Bank and Investment Management segments. The Bank segment involves in providing commercial banking and private banking services to middle-market businesses and high-net-worth individuals through the TriState Capital Bank subsidiary. The Investment Management segment offers advisory and sub-advisory investment management services to institutional plan sponsors through the Chartwell Investment Partners, LLC subsidiary. The company was founded on May 25, 2006 and is headquartered in Pittsburgh, PA.
