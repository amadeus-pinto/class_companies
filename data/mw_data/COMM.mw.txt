CommScope Holding Co., Inc. engages in the provision of infrastructure solution for the core, access, and edge layers of communication networks. It operates through CommScope Connectivity Solutions (CCS), and CommScope Mobility Solutions (CMS) segments. The CCS segment provides connectivity, and network intelligence for indoor and outdoor network applications. The CMS segment refers to the provision of merchant radio frequency wireless network solutions, as well as metro cell, distributed antenna systems and small cell solutions. The company was founded by Frank M. Drendel in 1976 and is headquartered in Hickory, NC.
