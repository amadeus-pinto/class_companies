M/I Homes, Inc. engages in the construction and development of residential properties. It operates through the Homebuilding and Financial Services segments. The Homebuilding segment includes Midwest, Southern, and Mid-Atlantic homebuilding activities. The Financial Services segment sells and collects fees from mortgages, title insurances, and closing services. The company was founded by Irving Schottenstein and Melvin Schottenstein in 1973 and is headquartered in Columbus, OH.
