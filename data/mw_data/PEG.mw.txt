Public Service Enterprise Group, Inc. is a holding company, which engages  in the provision of electric and gas services. It operates through the Public Service Electric &amp; Gas Co. (PSE&amp;G) and Power segments. The PSE&amp;G segment engages in the transmission of electricity and distribution of electricity and natural gas. The Power segment wholesales energy, fuel supply, and energy transacting functions. The company&#x27;s products and services include energy, capacity, ancillary services, and emissions allowances and congestion credits. Public Service Enterprise Group was founded in 1903 and is headquartered in Newark, NJ.
