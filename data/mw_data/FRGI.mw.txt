Fiesta Restaurant Group, Inc. engages in the fast-casual restaurant industry, which involves the acquisition, operation, and franchise of restaurants. It operates through the Pollo Tropical and Taco Cabana segments. The Pollo Tropical segment offers a variety of freshly prepared Caribbean inspired food. The Taco Cabana segment serves a selection of freshly prepared Mexican inspired food. The company was founded in April 2011 and is headquartered in Dallas, TX.
