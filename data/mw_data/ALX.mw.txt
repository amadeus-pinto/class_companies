Alexander&#x27;s, Inc. is a real estate investment trust, which engages in leasing, managing, developing, and redeveloping of properties. Its portfolio includes properties in New York metropolitan area consisting of: 731 Lexington Avenue, Rego Park I, Rego Park II, The Alexander Apartment Tower, Paramus, Flushing, and Rego Park III. The company was founded on May 16, 1955 and is headquartered in Paramus, NJ.
