Actua Corp. engages in the owning and operating cloud-based software and services businesses with recurring revenue streams that improve the productivity and efficiency of business customers. The company operates through two segments: Vertical Cloud and Vertical Cloud. The Vertical Cloud segment includes those businesses in which the company&#x27;s management takes a very active role in providing strategic direction and operational support and towards which it expect to devote relatively large proportions of the company&#x27;s personnel, financial capital and other resources. The Venture cloud segment includes companies in which it take a less active role in terms of strategic direction and operational support, and accordingly, towards which the company devote relatively small amounts of personnel, financial capital and other resources. The company was founded by Walter W. Buckley, III on March 4, 1996 and is headquartered in Wayne, PA.
