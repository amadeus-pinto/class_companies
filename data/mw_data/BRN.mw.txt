Barnwell Industries, Inc. engages in the oil and natural gas exploration, land investment, real estate, and contract drilling. The company operates its business four segments: Oil &amp; Natural Gas, Land Investment, Contract Drilling and Residential Real Estate. The Oil &amp; Natural Gas segment engages in oil and natural gas exploration, development, production and sales in Canada. The Land Investment segment invests in leasehold interests in real estate in Hawaii. The Contract Drilling segment provides well drilling services and water pumping system installation and repairs in Hawaii. The Residential Real Estate segment develops homes for sale in Hawaii. The company was founded by Morton H. Kinzler in 1956 and is headquartered in Honolulu, HI.
