IES Holdings, Inc. operates as a holding company. It operates through the following segments: Communications; Residential; Commercial and Industrial; and Infrastructure Solutions. The Communications segment provides products and solutions for data centers and telecommunications. The Residential segment deals with electrical installation services. The Commercial and Industrial segment designs, constructs, and maintains power infrastructure and generation services. The Infrastructure Solutions segment includes electrical, mechanical, industrial, and rail services. The company was founded in June 1997 and is headquartered in Houston, TX.
