WhiteHorse Finance, Inc is a business development company, which seeks investment opportunities in small-cap companies located in the United States. It targets companies operating across a  sectors such as business equipment &amp; services, financial intermediaries, healthcare, equipment leasing, chemicals &amp; plastics, building &amp; development, insurance and retailers. The company primarily invests in the form of senior secured loans and also makes mezzanine loans, leveraged buyouts, and equity interests. It provides financing for acquisitions, refinancing and growth capital requirements.
