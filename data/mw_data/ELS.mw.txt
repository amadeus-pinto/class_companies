Equity LifeStyle Properties, Inc. owns and operates portfolio of resort communities and lifestyle oriented properties. The company leases individual developed areas with access to utilities for placement of factory built homes, cottages, cabins or recreational vehicles. It operates through the following segments: Property Operations, and Home Sales and Rentals Operations. The Property Operations segment owns and operates land lease Properties. The Home Sales and Rentals Operations segment purchases, sells and leases homes at the Properties. The company was founded in December 1992 and is headquartered in Chicago, IL.segment
