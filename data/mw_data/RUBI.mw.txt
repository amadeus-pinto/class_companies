The Rubicon Project, Inc. aims to automate buying and selling of advertising. Its advertising automation cloud is a highly scalable software platform that powers and optimizes a marketplace for the real time trading of digital advertising between buyers and sellers. The company was founded by Frank Addante, Duc Chau, Craig Roah, Julie Mattern and Brian D. Baumgart on April 20, 2007 and is headquartered in Los Angeles, CA.
