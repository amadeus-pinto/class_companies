Spirit Airlines, Inc. is an ultra low-cost, low-fare airline that offers affordable travel to price-conscious customers. The company&#x27;s ultra low-cost carrier, business model allows it to compete principally by offering customers its Bare Fares, which are unbundled base fares that remove components traditionally included in the price of an airline ticket. It gives customers Frill Control, which provides customers the freedom to save by paying only for the options they choose such as bags, advance seat assignments and refreshments. The company, with its all-Airbus Fit Fleet, operates more than 420 daily flights to 59 destinations in the United States, Caribbean and Latin America. Spirit Airlines was founded by Ned Homfeld in 1964 and is headquartered in Miramar, FL.
