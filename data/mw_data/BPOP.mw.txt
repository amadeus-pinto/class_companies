Popular, Inc. is a financial holding company, which engages in the provision of banking and financial services. It operates through the Banco Popular de Puerto Rico (BPPR) and Banco Popular North America (BPNA) segments. The BPPR segment includes mortgage loans; auto and equipment leasing; investment banking; and insurance products. The BPNA segment offers community banking services. The company was founded on October 5, 1893 and is headquartered in San Juan, Puerto Rico.
