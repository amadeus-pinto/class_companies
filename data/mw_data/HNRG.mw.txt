Hallador Energy Co. through its subsidiaries engages in the mining, production, and sale of steam coal for the electric power generation industry. The company holds interest in the following properties: the Carlisle underground coal mine; Ace in the Hole mine; Oaktown 1 and Oaktown 2 mines; and Bulldog underground mines. It also involves in oil and gas exploration activities. The company was founded in 1951 and is headquartered in Denver, CO.
