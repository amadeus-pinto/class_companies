Cabela&#x27;s, Inc. engages in the retail business of hunting, fishing, camping, shooting sports, and related outdoor merchandise. It operates through Merchandising, and Financial Services segments. The Merchandising segment is comprised of the new retail stores, purchases of inventory, investments in the management information systems and infrastructure of the company, and general working capital needs. The Financial Services segment relates to the financing of credit card loans. The company was founded by Richard N. Cabela and James W. Cabela in 1961 and is headquartered in Sidney, NE.
