Arotech Corp. provides defense and security products for the military, law enforcement and homeland security markets, including advanced zinc-air and lithium batteries and chargers, and multimedia interactive simulators and trainers. The company operates its business through two Divisions: Training &amp; Simulation Division and Power Systems Division. The Training and Simulation Division develops manufactures and market advanced high-tech multimedia and interactive digital solutions for use-of-force training and driver training of military, law enforcement, security and other personnel. The Battery and Power Systems Division manufacture and sell lithium and zinc-air batteries for defense and security products and other military applications. Arotech was founded by Arnold J. Goldman in 1990 and is headquartered in Ann Arbor, MI.
