Financial Engines, Inc. engages in the provision of technology-enabled portfolio management services, investment advice, and retirement income services. Its services include professional management, online advice, retirement evaluation, and Expand Beyond Workplace Defined Contribution Plans. The company was founded by William F. Sharpe, Joseph A. Grundfest, and Craig W. Johnson on May 13, 1996 and is headquartered in Sunnyvale, CA.
