Internap Corp. engages in the provision of performance-optimized information technology infrastructure services. It operates through the Data Center Services and Internet Protocol (IP) Services segments. The Data Center Services segment includes colocation, hosting, and cloud services. The IP Services segment covers the patented Performance IP service, content delivery network services, IP routing hardware and software platform, and Managed Internet Route Optimizer Controller. The company was founded in May 1996 and is headquartered in Atlanta, GA.
