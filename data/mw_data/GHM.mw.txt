Graham Corp. designs, manufactures and sells equipment for the energy, defense and chemical/petrochemical industries. It designs and manufactures custom engineered ejectors, vacuum pumping systems, surface condensers and vacuum systems as well as supplies and components for use inside the reactor vessel and outside the containment vessel of nuclear power facilities. The company&#x27;s equipment is used to produce synthetic fibers, chemicals, petroleum products, electric power, processed food, pharmaceutical products, paper, steel and fertilizers. Graham was founded on March 7, 1983 and is headquartered in Batavia, NY.
