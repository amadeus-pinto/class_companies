Ennis, Inc. operates as a holding company, which engages in the global production and sale of printed business products and apparel. It operates through the following segments: Print and Apparel. The Print segment engages in manufacturing, designing and selling business forms and other printed business products primarily to distributors located in the U.S. This segment products include snap sets, continuous forms, laser cut sheets, tags, labels, envelopes, integrated products, jumbo rolls, and pressure sensitive products under the brand names of Ennis, Royal Business Forms, Block Graphics, Specialized Printed Forms, 360 Custom Labels, Enfusion, Uncompromised Check Solutions, VersaSeal, Witt Printing, B&amp;D Litho, Genforms, PrintGraphics, Calibrated Forms, PrintXcel, and Printegra. The Apparel segment manufactures and sells knitted active wear, including shirts, T-shirts, tank tops, and fleece under the AAA and Murina brand names. This segment also produces hats, shorts, pants, and other active wear apparel products. The company was founded in 1909 and is headquartered in Midlothian, TX.
