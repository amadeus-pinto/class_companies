Canterbury Park Holding Corp. hosts and manages pari-mutuel wagering activities. It operates through the following segments: Horse Racing, Card Casino, and Concessions. The Horse Racing segment includes simulcast and live horse racing operations. The Card Casino segment holds unbanked card games. The Concessions segment sells food and beverages in gaming events. The Company was founded by Curtis A. Samson, Randall D. Sampson, and Dale H. Schenian on March 24, 1994 and is headquartered in Shakopee, MN.
