A.H. Belo Corp. is a newspaper publishing and local news and information company. It owns and operates four daily newspapers which include The Dallas Morning News, The Providence Journal, The Press Enterprise and The Denton Record-Chronicle. The company also owns and operates commercial printing, distribution and direct mail service businesses. It operates through following business segments: Publishing &amp; Marketing and Event Marketing &amp; Other Services. The Publishing and Marketing segment includes the Company&#x27;s core print operations associated with its newspapers, niche publications and related websites. The Event Marketing and Other Services segment is comprised of the Company&#x27;s marketing, event marketing and other businesses. A H Belo was founded in 1842 and is headquartered in Dallas, TX.
