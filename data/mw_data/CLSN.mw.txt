Celsion Corp. is an oncology drug development company. It focuses on developing a portfolio of innovative cancer treatments, including directed chemotherapies, DNA-mediated immunotherapy and RNA- based therapies. The company&#x27;s products include ThermoDox and GEN-1. Celsion was founded by Yim-Pan Cheung in 1982 and is headquartered in Lawrenceville, NJ.
