Healthcare Trust of America, Inc. operates as an integrated, self-administered and self-managed real estate investment trust. It acquires, owns and operates medical office buildings and other facilities that serve the healthcare industry. The company invests in a diversified portfolio of real estate properties, medical office buildings, healthcare-related facilities, commercial office properties and other real estate- related assets that produce recurring income. Healthcare Trust of America was founded by Scott D. Peters on April 20, 2006 and is headquartered in Scottsdale, AZ.
