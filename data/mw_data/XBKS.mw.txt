Hampton Roads Bankshares, Inc. operates as a bank holding company for Bank of Hampton Roads and Shore Bank. It engages in general community and commercial banking businesses, targeting the banking needs of individuals and small-to medium-sized businesses in primary service areas, which include the Hampton Roads region of southeastern Virginia, the Northeastern and Research Triangle regions of North Carolina, the Eastern Shore of Virginia and Maryland, and Richmond, Virginia. The company&#x27;s primary products are traditional loan and deposit banking services. It operates its business through two segments: Mortgage and Corporate. The Mortgage segment provides mortgage banking services such as originating and processing mortgage loans for sale to the secondary market. The Corporate segment which provide securities, brokerage, and investment advisory services. Hampton Roads Bankshares was founded on February 28, 2001 and is headquartered in Norfolk, VA.
