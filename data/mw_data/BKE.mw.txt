The Buckle, Inc. engages in the manufacture and trade of apparel, footwear, and accessories. It manages and operates retail stores. It markets its products under the following brands: BKE, BKE Boutique, BKE Vintage, BKE Lounge, Buckle Black, Gimmicks by BKE, BKE Sport, BKE Red, BKE Sole, Reclaim, and Daytrip. The company was founded by David Hirschfeld in 1948 and is headquartered in Kearney, NE.
