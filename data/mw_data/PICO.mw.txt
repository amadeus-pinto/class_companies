PICO Holdings, Inc. engages in the provision of evaluating, building, and developing undervalued assets and businesses. It operates through the following segments: Water Resource and Water Storage Operations; Real Estate Operations; and Corporate. The Water Resource and Water Storage Operations segment develops and sells existing water rights. The Real Estate Operations segment involves financing additional acquisitions, development, and construction costs from its existing cash; proceeds of the sale of existing lots and homes; and or through external financing. The Corporate segment includes oil and gas production. The company was founded in 1981 and is headquartered in La Jolla, CA.
