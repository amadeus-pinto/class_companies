Concurrent Computer Corp engages in the provision of global software and solutions. It develops advanced applications on a core foundation of high performance Linux and storage technologies. . It operates through two reporting segments: Product and Services. Its video solutions consist of software, hardware, and services for intelligently streaming video and collecting and analyzing media data. The company&#x27;s Real Time products consist of real-time Linux operating system versions, development tools and other system software combined, in most cases, with computer platforms and services. The company was founded in 1966 and is headquartered in Duluth, GA.
