P&amp;F Industries, Inc. engages in manufacturing and importing of air-powered tools. It operates through the following business segments: Tools &amp; Other Products Hardware &amp; Accessories. The Tools &amp; Other Products segment engages in the importation and sale of pneumatic hand tools and compressor air filters. The Hardware &amp; Accessories segment imports and manufactures door, window and fencing hardware, and accessories including rollers, hinges, window operators, sash locks, custom zinc castings and door closers. The company was founded on April 19, 1963 and is headquartered in Melville, NY.
