Tandy Leather Factory, Inc. engages in retailing and wholesaling leather and related products. It operates through the following segments: Wholesale Leathercraft, Retail Leathercraft, and International Leathercraft. The Wholesale Leathercraft segment involves in the distribution of leather and leathercraft-related products in the United States and Canada through wholesale stores operating under the name, The Leather Factory. The Retail Leathercraft segment consists of chain of retail stores operating under the name, Tandy Leather. The International Leathercraft offers company-owned stores located outside of North America. The company was founded by J. Wray Thompson, Sr. and Ronald C. Morgan in 1980 and is headquartered in Fort Worth, TX.
