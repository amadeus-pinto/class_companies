Anika Therapeutics, Inc. is global medical technology company, which develops, manufactures and commercializes therapeutic products for pain management, tissue regeneration and wound healing. The company&#x27;s products are based on hyaluronic acid, a natural chemical occurring, biocompatible polymer found throughout the body. It offers therapeutic products which include orthobiologics, dermal, ophthalmic, surgical and veterinary. Anika Therapeutics was founded in 1992 and is headquartered in Bedford, MA.
