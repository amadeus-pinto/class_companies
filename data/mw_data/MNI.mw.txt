The McClatchy Co. operates as an Internet and newspaper publishing company, which engages in the provision of newspapers, community newspapers, mobile news, advertising, niche publications, direct marketing, and direct mail services. It also involves in publishing brands such as Miami Herald, The Kansas City Star, The Sacramento Bee, The Charlotte Observer, The (Raleigh) News and Observer, and the (Fort Worth) Star-Telegram. The company was founded in 1857 and is headquartered in Sacramento, CA.
