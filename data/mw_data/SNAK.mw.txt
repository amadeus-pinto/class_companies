Inventure Foods, Inc. engages in the manufacturing and marketing healthy and natural snack food brands products. It operates through Snack Products and Frozen Products segments. The Snack Products segment offers producing potato chips, potato crisps, potato skins, pellet snacks, kettle chips and extruded product for sale primarily to snack food distributors and retailers. The Frozen Products segment includes frozen fruit products, such as berries and smoothies, for sale primarily to club stores, groceries and mass merchandisers. The company was founded in 1995 and is headquartered in Phoenix, AZ.
