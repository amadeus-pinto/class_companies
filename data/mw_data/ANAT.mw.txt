American National Insurance Co. is a diversified insurance and financial services company. It offers a spectrum of life, annuity, health and property &amp; casualty insurance products. The company&#x27;s family of companies includes six life insurance companies, eight property and casualty insurance companies and numerous non-insurance subsidiaries. The company operates in five business segments: Life, Annuity, Health, Property and Casualty and Corporate &amp; Other. The Life segment provides individual and group life insurance products, including whole life, term life, variable universal life &amp; universal life and credit life insurance. The Annuity segment offers fixed, indexed and variable annuity products. The Health segment provides medicare supplement, supplemental insurance, medical expense, stop-loss and credit disability products. The Property &amp; Casualty segment provides personal lines, credit-related property insurance products. The Corporate and Other segment consists of primarily invested assets that are not used to support its insurance activities. The company was founded by William L. Moody Jr. in 1905 and is headquartered in Galveston, TX.
