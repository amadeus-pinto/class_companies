Thermon Group Holdings, Inc. engages in the development, manufacture, and trade of engineered thermal solutions for process industries. It operates through the following geographical segments: United States, Canada, Europe, and Asia. Its products include industrial electric heat and steam tracing, instrument tubing bundles, commercial electric heat tracing, controls and monitoring, analyzer umbilicals, and continuous emissions monitoring systems. The company was founded in 2010 and is headquartered in San Marcos, TX.
