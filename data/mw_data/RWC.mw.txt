RELM Wireless Corp. engaged in the manufacturing of wireless communications products principally two-way land mobile radios. Its products include KNG2, KNG Tier 2, KNG Tier 3, KNG S, DPHX5102X, GPHX, GPH command, RP4200 and RP6500. The company was founded on January 30, 1998 and is headquartered in West Melbourne, FL.
