Digital Ally, Inc. engages in global design, manufacture, and innovation of digital technology and it produces digital video imaging, and storage products for use in law enforcement, fire, emergency medical services, security, and commercial fleet industries. Its products include in-car audio/video recording equipment, body-worn cameras, cloud and local server software and storage solutions. The company was founded on December 13, 2000 and is headquartered in Lenexa, KS.
