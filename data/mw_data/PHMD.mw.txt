PhotoMedex, Inc. is a global health products and services company, which provides integrated disease management and aesthetic solutions to dermatologists, professional aestheticians and consumers. It provides proprietary products and services that address skin diseases and conditions including psoriasis, vitiligo, acne and photo damage. The company operates through three segments: Consumer, Physician Recurring and Professional. PhotoMedex was founded on December 30, 2010 and is headquartered in Willow Grove, PA.
