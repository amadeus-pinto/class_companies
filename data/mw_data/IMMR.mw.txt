Immersion Corp. is an intellectual property and software licensing company, which engages on creation, design, development and licensing of patented haptic innovations and software that allow people to use their sense of touch more fully when operating a wide variety of digital devices. It focuses on the following target application areas: mobile devices, wearable, consumer, mobile entertainment and other content, console gaming, automotive, medical, and commercial. The company was founded in 1993 and is headquartered in San Jose, CA.
