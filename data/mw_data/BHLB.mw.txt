Berkshire Hills Bancorp, Inc. operates as a bank holding company, which engages in the provision of community banking services. The firm&#x27;s loan portfolio comprises of the following: commercial real estate; commercial and industrial; residential mortgage; and consumer. It also offers savings and checking accounts, deposits, lending, mortgages loans, insurance, and wealth management products. The company was founded in June 2000 and is headquartered in Pittsfield, MA.
