Destination Maternity Corp. is engaged in the designing and retailing maternity apparel and related accessories. The company operates stores under three brands: Motherhood Maternity, A Pea in the Pod and Oh Baby by Motherhood. The Motherhood Maternity brand serves the moderate priced portion of the maternity apparel business, which is used in special occasion, offering quality merchandise at affordable value. The A Pea in the Pod brand is a contemporary, fashion-forward assortment including a curated selection of exclusive designer labels at premium pricing, offering the mom2be fashionable maternity pieces that reflect her uncompromising sense of style in both casual and career apparel. The Oh Baby by Motherhood brand features a modern assortment of quality fashions, with most items having initial prices and the collection is available at Kohl&#x27;s stores. The company was founded in 1982 and is headquartered in Moorestown, NJ.
