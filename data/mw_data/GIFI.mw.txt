Gulf Island Fabrication, Inc. engages in the fabrication of complex steel structures and marine vessels used in energy extraction and production, petrochemical and industrial facilities, power generation and alternative energy projects, and shipping and marine transportation operations. It operates in the following segments: Fabrication, Shipyards, and Services. The Fabrication segment primarily fabricates structures such as offshore drilling and production platforms and other steel  structure for customers in the oil and gas industries. The Shipyards segment manufactures new build and repairs various steel marine vessels in the United States. The Services segment provides interconnect piping services on offshore platforms and inshore structures. The company was founded by Alden J. Laborde in 1985 and is headquartered in Houston, TX.
