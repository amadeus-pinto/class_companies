American Vanguard Corp. is a holding company which engages in manufacturing of agricultural chemicals. The company develops and markets products for crop protection, turf and ornamental markets, and public health applications. Its subsidiary, AMVAC Chemical Corp. manufactures insecticides, fungicides, herbicides, molluscicides, growth regulators, and soil fumigants, are marketed in liquid, powder, and granular forms. The Company was founded by Herbert A. Kraft and Glenn A. Wintemute in January 1969 and is headquartered in Newport Beach, CA.
