Vince Holding Corp. engages in the design and production of luxury apparel and accessories brand. It offers a wide range of women&#x27;s, men&#x27;s and children&#x27;s apparel, women&#x27;s and men&#x27;s footwear, and handbags. It operates its business through the Wholesale and Direct-to-Consumer segments. The Wholesale segment is comprised of sales to premier department stores and specialty stores in the U.S. and in select international markets. The Direct-to-Consumer segment includes its retail and outlet stores and its e-commerce business. The company was founded by Rea Laccone and Christopher LaPolice in 2002 and is headquartered in New York, NY.
