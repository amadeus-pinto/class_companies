Capitala Finance Corp. is a business development company that invests in lower middle-market companies located in the US. The fund focuses on companies with revenues of more than $15 million, EBITDA of more than $5 million, positive cash flow, strong margins, an established &amp; defensible market position and proven management teams. It targets companies operating in the fields of business services, general industrial, consumer products, restaurant &amp; food, healthcare, information technology, and energy services. It provides financing in the form of mezzanine, subordinated debt, one-stop &amp; uni-tranche structures, first &amp; second-lien loans and equity co-investments for leveraged buyouts, management buyouts, recapitalizations and growth capital financings with an investment size ranging from $5 - $30 million per transaction.
