RadiSys Corp. provides embedded wireless infrastructure solutions for telecom, aerospace, defense and public safety applications. The company operates through two operating segments: Software-Systems and Embedded Products and Hardware Services. The Software-Systems is comprised of FlowEngine, MediaEngine products, CellEngine software. The Embedded Products and Hardware Services are predominantly hardware based products that enable the control and movement of data in both 3G and LTE telecom networks. The products enable network elements for applications such as Deep Packet Inspection, policy management and intelligent gateways (security, femto and LTE gateways). RadiSys was founded by Glenford J. Myers, David L. Budde and Robert Arthur Patterson on March 18, 1987 and is headquartered in Hillsboro, OR.
