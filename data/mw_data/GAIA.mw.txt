Gaia, Inc. provides information, media, products, and services to customers who value yoga, fitness, wellness, personal development, and responsible media. It operates through the following Business segments: Gaiam Brand and Gaia. The Gaiam Brand segment includes all yoga, fitness, and wellness product and media distributed through there website, apps, retail network, and catalogs, and also includes travel business. The Gaia segment includes global digital video subscription service. The company was founded by Jirka Rysavy on July 7, 1988 and is headquartered in Louisville, CO.
