Spark Networks, Inc. engages in the operation of online platform designed to build community. It helps individuals form relationships with others that share their interests and values. It operates through the following segments: Jewish Networks, Christian Networks, Other Networks, and Offline &amp; Other Businesses. The Jewish Networks segment consists of JDate, JDate.co.uk, JDate.fr, JDate.co.il, Cupid.co.il, and JSwipe. The Christian Networks segment comprises of ChristianMingle, CrossPaths, ChristianMingle.co.uk, ChristianMingle.com.au, Believe.com, ChristianCards.net, DailyBibleVerse.com and Faith.com. The Other Networks segment includes Spark.com and related other general market websites as well as other properties which are primarily composed of sites targeted towards various religious, ethnic, geographic, and special interest groups. The Offline &amp; Other Businesses segment composes of revenue generated from offline activities. The company was founded in 1997 and is headquartered in Los Angeles, CA.
