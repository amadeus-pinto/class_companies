The Western Union Co. is a holding company, which engages in the provision of money transfer and payment services. It operates through the following segments: Consumer-to-Consumer; Consumer-to-Business; Business Solutions; and Other. The Consumer-to-Consumer segment offers multi-currency, real-time, and online money transfer transactions. The Consumer-to-Business segment includes bill payments from clients to businesses such as utilities, auto finance companies, mortgages, and government agencies. The Business Solutions segment consists of foreign exchange services, cross-border transactions, and cross-currency payments. The Other segment includes the firm&#x27;s corporate operations. The company was founded in 1851 and is headquartered in Englewood, CO.
