USA Truck, Inc. operates as an international truckload carrier, which provides transportation of general commodities throughout the U.S., Mexico, and Canada. It operates through the following segments: Trucking and USAT Logistics. This segment  transports customer freight over irregular routes utilizing equipment owned by either the company or independent contractors as a medium- to long-haul common carrier. The Trucking segment comprises the truckload and freight service offerings. The Strategic Capacity Solutions segment consists of freight brokerage and rail intermodal service offerings. The company was founded in 1983 and is headquartered in Van Buren, AR.
