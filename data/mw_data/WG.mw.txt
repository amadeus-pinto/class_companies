Willbros Group, Inc. provides engineering and construction services to the oil, gas, refinery, petrochemical and power industries. It operates its business through following segments: Oil &amp; Gas, Utility T&amp;D and Canada. The Oil &amp; Gas segment provides engineering, procurement and construction, project management, maintenance and lifecycle extension services to the upstream, midstream and downstream markets. The Utility T&amp;D segment provides a range of services in electric and natural gas transmission and distribution, including comprehensive maintenance and construction, repair and restoration of utility infrastructure. The Canada segment provides an integrated suite of services to customers located in the oil sands region in Alberta. The company was founded by Miller Williams and David Williams in 1908 and is headquartered in Houston, TX.
