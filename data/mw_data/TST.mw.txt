TheStreet, Inc. is a digital financial media company, which engages in the provision of content and tools through online, social media, tablet, and mobile channels. It operates through the following segments: Deal and BoardEx, RateWatch, and Business to Consumer. The Deal and BoardEx segment offers proprietary and commercial systems developed for consumption by institutional clients. The RateWatch segment involves in database of deposit, loan, and fee rate data from financial institutions using proprietary software and commercial software both internally and within the Amazon web services environment. The Business to Consumer segment includes free content and houses premium subscription products that target varying segments of the retail investing public. The company was founded by James J. Cramer and Martin Peretz in 1996 and is headquartered in New York, NY.
