Psychemedics Corp. engages in the provision of drug testing services through the analysis of hair samples. Its testing methods utilize a technology that digests the hair and releases drugs trapped in the hair without destroying the drugs. The company performs custom-designed enzyme immunoassay on the liquid supernatant, with confirmation testing by mass spectrometry. It provides screening and confirmation by mass spectrometry for cocaine; marijuana; phencyclidine; amphetamines including ecstasy; and opiates such as including heroin, hydrocodone, hydromorphone, oxycodone and codeine. Psychemedics was founded by Werner Baumgartner and Annette Baumgartner on September 24, 1986 and is headquartered in Acton, MA.
