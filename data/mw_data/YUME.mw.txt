YuMe, Inc. engages in the provision of digital video brand advertising solutions. It provides multi-screen programmatic video advertising technology, which connects advertisers, digital media property owners, and consumers of video content across a range of internet-connected devices for branding solutions. The company was founded by Jayant Kadambi and Ayyappan Sankaran on December 16, 2004 and is headquartered in Redwood, CA.
