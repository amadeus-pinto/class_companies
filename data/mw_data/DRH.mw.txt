DiamondRock Hospitality Co. is a real estate investment trust with interests in hotels and resorts portfolio. Its activities include owning  and management of lodging properties. It operates through the following segments: Chicago, Los Angeles, Boston, US Virgin Islands, New York, Minneapolis, Denver, and Other. The company was founded by Mark W. Brugger, William W. McCarten and John L. Williams in July 2004 and is headquartered in Bethesda, MD.
