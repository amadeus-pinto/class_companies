Apollo Commercial Real Estate Finance, Inc. is a real estate investment trust that primarily originates, invests in, acquires and manages performing commercial first mortgage loans, subordinate financings, commercial mortgage backed securities and other commercial real estate-related debt investments. It offers financing across a broad spectrum of commercial property types and geographies and at all points within a property&#x27;s capital structure. The company was founded on on June 29, 2009 and is headquartered in New York, NY.
