Ecology &amp; Environment, Inc. engages in the provision of environmental consulting services. Its customers include federal, state and local governments, domestic private clients, and private and governmental international clients. It operates through the following segments: EEI and its Subsidiaries located in the U.S.; Subsidiaries located in South America; and Other Foreign Subsidiaries. The company was founded by Gerald A. Strobel, Frank B. Silvestro, and Ronald L. Frank on February 1, 1970 and is headquartered in Lancaster, NY.
