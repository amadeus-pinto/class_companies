BioTelemetry, Inc. provides monitoring services and digital population health management in a healthcare setting, medical device manufacturing and centralized core laboratory services for clinical research. The company also develops proprietary integrated patient management platform that incorporates wireless data transmission network. It operates through operates through three segments: Healthcare, Technology, and Research. The Healthcare segment focuses on the diagnosis and monitoring of cardiac arrhythmias or heart rhythm disorders. The Technology segment engages in the development, manufacturing, testing, and marketing of medical devices to medical companies, clinics, and hospitals. The Research segment engages in the central core laboratory services that provides cardiac monitoring, scientific consulting, and data management services for drug and medical device trials. The company was founded on April 22, 2013 and is headquartered in Malvern, PA.
