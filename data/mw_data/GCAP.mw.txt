GAIN Capital Holdings, Inc. provides online trading services, specializing in global over-the-counter markets, including spot foreign exchange and precious metals, as well as contracts-for-difference, which are investment products with returns linked to the performance of an underlying commodity, index or security. It operates through the following segments: Retail, Institutional, Futures, Corporate and Other. The company offers services to retail investors through FOREX.com brand and institutional investors through GAIN GTX offering. GAIN Capital provides execution, clearing, custody and technology products and services to an institutional client base, including asset managers, broker, dealers and other financial services firms. GAIN Capital Holdings was founded on October, 1999 and is headquartered in Bedminster, NJ.
