Spartan Motors, Inc. designs, engineers and manufactures custom chassis and vehicles for the emergency response, recreational vehicle and specialty vehicle markets. It operates through the following segments: Emergency Response Vehicles, Delivery and Service Vehicles, and Specialty Chassis and Vehicles. The Emergency Response Vehicles segment engineers and manufactures emergency response chassis and bodies, and aerial equipment. The Delivery and Service Vehicles segment designs and manufactures walk-in vans for the delivery and service market and the production of commercial truck bodies. The Specialty Chassis and Vehicles segment manufactures motor home chassis, defense vehicles and other specialty chassis and distribute related aftermarket parts and assemblies. The company was founded by William Foster, George Sztykiel, Gerald Geary and John Knox on September 18, 1975 and is headquartered in Charlotte, MI.
