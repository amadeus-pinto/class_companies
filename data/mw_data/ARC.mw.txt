ARC Document Solutions, Inc. is a document solutions company, which services the businesses of all types, with an emphasis on the non-residential segment of the architecture, engineering, and construction industries. The firm operates through the Unites States and Foreign Countries geographical segments. It provides onsite managed print services; archiving and information management; construction document and information management; as well as sells technology applications to support these services. The company was founded by Sathiyamurthy Chandramohan and Kumarakulasingam Suriyakumar in 1988 and is headquartered in Walnut Creek, CA.
