Tredegar Corp. is engaged in the manufacture of polyethylene plastic films, polyester films and aluminum extrusions. The company operates through following business segments: PE Films, Flexible Packaging Films and Aluminum Extrusions. The PE Films segment manufactures plastic films, elastics and laminate materials utilized in personal care materials, surface protection films and specialty and optical lighting applications. The Flexible Packaging Films segment produces polyester-based films used in packaging applications. The Aluminum Extrusions segment manufactures mill, anodized, painted and fabricated aluminum extrusions for sale directly to fabricators and distributors, it competes on the basis of product quality, service and price. The company was founded in 1988 and is headquartered in Richmond, VA.
