Boston Beer Co., Inc. engages in the business of producing and selling alcoholic beverages. It produces malt beverages and hard cider products at company owned breweries and under contract arrangements at other brewery locations. The company operates through the following segments: Boston Beer Company and A&amp;S Brewing Collaborative. The Boston Beer Company segment is comprised of the Samuel Adams, Twisted Tea and Angry Orchard brands. The A&amp;S Brewing Collaborative operating segment includes Angel City Brewing Company and Concrete Beach Brewing Company. Boston Beer was founded by C. James Koch in 1984 and is headquartered in Boston, MA.
