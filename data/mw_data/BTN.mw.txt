Ballantyne Strong, Inc. is a holding company, which engages in the design, integration, and installation of a broad range of applications including digital projection and digital signage. It operates through the Cinema and Digital Media segments. The Cinema segment provides a full range of products and services to the theatre exhibition industry from the design and installation. The Digital Media segment is comprised of the sale of digital signage and cinema equipments and provision of solutions and services across digital out-of-home and cinema. The company was founded by Robert Scott Ballantyne in 1932 and is headquartered in Omaha, NE.
