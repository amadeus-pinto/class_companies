Landauer, Inc. provides radiation science and services. It offers technical and analytical services to determine occupational and environmental radiation exposure, the domestic provider of outsourced medical physics services, as well as a provider of medical accessories used in radiology, radiation therapy, and image guided surgery procedures. The firm&#x27;s  services include the manufacture of various types of radiation detection monitors, the distribution and collection of the monitors to and from customers, and the analysis and reporting of exposure findings. The company operates in three reportable segments: Radiation Measurement, Medical Physics and Medical Products. The Radiation Measurement segment provides analytical services to determine occupational and environmental radiation exposure. This segment includes the manufacture of various types of radiation detection monitors, the distribution and collection of the monitors to and from customers, and the analysis and reporting of exposure findings. The Medical Physics segment provides therapeutic and imaging physics services to domestic hospitals and radiation therapy centers. Its service offerings include clinical physics support, equipment commissioning, accreditation support and imaging equipment testing. The Medical Products segment provides medical consumable accessories used in radiology, radiation therapy, and image guided surgery procedures. Landauer was founded by Robert S. Landauer Jr. in 1954 and is headquartered in Glenwood, IL.
