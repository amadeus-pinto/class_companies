CIT Group, Inc. is a financial holding company, which provides financing, leasing and advisory services. It operates through the following segments: Transportation and International Finance; North America Banking; Legacy Consumer Mortgages; Non-Strategic Portfolios; and Corporate and Other. The Transportation and International Finance segment provides leasing and financing solution to operators and suppliers in the aviation and railcar industries. The North America Banking segment consists of the following divisions: Commercial Banking, Commercial Real Estate, Commercial Services, Equipment Finance, and Consumer Banking. The Legacy Consumer Mortgages segment includes portfolios of mortgage loans and reverse mortgage loans. The Non-Strategic Portfolio segment involves in portfolios that no longer considered strategic. The Corporate and Other segment offers investments and other unallocated items, such as certain amortization of intangible assets. The company was founded by Henry Ittelson in 1908 and is headquartered in New York, NY.
