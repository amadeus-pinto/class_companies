TimkenSteel Corp. engages in the manufacturing of steel large bars and seamless mechanical tubing. Its portfolio includes special bar quality bars, seamless mechanical tubing and precision steel components. It operates its business through the following segments: Industrial and Mobile and Energy and Distribution. The Industrial and Mobile segment provides high quality air-melted alloy steel bars, tubes, precision components and value-added services. The Energy and Distribution segment provides high quality air-melted alloy steel bars, seamless tubes and value-added services such as thermal treatment and machining. The company was founded on October 24, 2013 and is headquartered in Canton, OH.
