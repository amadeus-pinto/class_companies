Rightside Group Ltd. provides domain name services that enable businesses and consumers to find, establish, and maintain their digital address-the starting point for connecting with their online audience. It is an end-to-end provider of domain name services, offering domain name registration services, wholesale and retail distribution, and aftermarket domain services and other related services to resellers and domain name registrants. The company markets its wholesale registration services under eNom brand, and retail registration services under Name.com brand. Rightside Group was founded on July 11, 2013 and is headquartered in Kirkland, WA.
