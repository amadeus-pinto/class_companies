U.S. Concrete, Inc. engages in the business of producing ready-mixed concrete. It operates through the Ready-Mixed Concrete and Aggregate Products segments. The Ready-Mixed Concrete segment focuses on the formulation, preparation, and delivery of ready-mixed concrete to the job sites of customers. The Aggregate Products segment produces crushed stone, sand and gravel from aggregates facilities located in New Jersey and Texas. The company was founded in July 1997 and is headquartered in Euless, TX.
