Full House Resorts, Inc. is a casino gaming company that operates, develops, manages and invests in casinos and related hospitality and entertainment facilities. Its current properties are Silver Slipper Casino, Rising Star Casino Resort, Stockman&#x27;s Casino and Grand Lodge Casino. Silver Slipper located in Bay St. Louis, Mississippi and Stockman&#x27;s Casino located in Fallon, Nevada. Its operations are composed of two primary business segments: Casino Operations and Development/Management. The Casino Operations segment includes the Grand Victoria Hotel and Casino&#x27;s operation in Rising Sun, Indiana and Stockman&#x27;s Casino operation in Fallon, Nevada. The Development/Management segment includes costs associated with tribal casino development and management projects and the Michigan and Delaware joint ventures. Full House Resorts was founded on January 5, 1987 and is headquartered in Las Vegas, NV.
