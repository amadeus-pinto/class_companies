Natus Medical, Inc. engages in the development, manufacture, and trade of medical devices. Its healthcare products caters hearing diagnostics and screening, neometrics data management, jaundice management, thermoregulation, neurology and sleep diagnostics, newborn care, obstetric and vascular dopplers, and neurophysiology research. The firm&#x27;s brands include Bio-logic, BrainZ, Dantec, Grass, Medix, Nicolet, Embla and Xltek. The company was founded by Maurizio Liverani, John Robert Camber Porter, William New, Jr., Brian Prinn, and William W. Moore on May 26, 1987 and is headquartered in Pleasanton, CA.
