Weingarten Realty Investors is a real estate investment trust which owns, manages and develops commercial real estate. Its business activities include the long-term ownership, management, acquisition, development and redevelopment of strategically located neighborhood and community shopping centers and select industrial properties. Its primary business is leasing space to tenants in the shopping and industrial centers which the company owns. The company was founded in 1948 and is headquartered in Houston, TX.
