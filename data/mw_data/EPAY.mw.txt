Bottomline Technologies, Inc. provides trusted and easy-to-use set of cloud-based digital banking, fraud prevention, payment, financial document, insurance and healthcare solutions. It operates through the following business segments: Payments &amp; Transactional Documents, Digital Banking and Hosted Solutions. The Payments &amp; Transactional Documents segment provides financial business process management solutions, including making and collecting payments, sending and receiving invoices, and generating and storing business documents. The Digital Banking segment provides solutions that are specifically designed for banking and financial institution customers. The Hosted Solutions segment provides customers with outsourced and hosted solution offerings that facilitate invoice receipt and presentment and spend management. The company was founded by Daniel M. McGurl and James L. Loomis in May 1989 and is headquartered in Portsmouth, NH.
