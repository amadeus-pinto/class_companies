Carter&#x27;s, Inc. engages in the provision of apparel for babies and young children. The firm operates through the following segments: Carter&#x27;s Wholesale, Carter&#x27;s Retail, OshKosh Retail, OshKosh Wholesale, and International. It offers products under the Carter&#x27;s and OshKosh brands. The company was founded by William Carter in 1865 and is headquartered in Atlanta, GA.
