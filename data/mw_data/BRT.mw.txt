BRT Apartments Corp. is a real estate investment trust, which engages in the ownership, operation, and development of multi-family properties; the ownership, operation and development of commercial, mixed use and other real estate assets; and real estate lending. It operates through the Multi-family Real Estate and Other Real Estate segments. The Multi-family Real Estate segment includes the ownership, operation, and development of the multi-family properties. The Other Real Estate segments involves in the ownership, development, operation and disposition of the real estate assets. The company was founded in June 1972 and is headquartered in Great Neck, NY.
