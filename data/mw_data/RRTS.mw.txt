Roadrunner Transportation Systems, Inc. engages in the provision of logistics services. It operates through the following segments: Truckload Logistics, Less-than-Truckload, and Global Solutions. The Truckload Logistics segment manages pick-ups, deliveries, consolidation, and inventory of intermodal, ground, and air freight services. The Less-than-Truckload segment includes pick-up, line haul, deconsolidation, and delivery of shipments. The Global Solutions segment offers customs brokerage, contract management, carrier selection, freight tracking, bills payment, cost reporting, and dispatch. The company was founded in February 2005 and is headquartered in Cudahy, WI.
