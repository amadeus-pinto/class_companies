iPass, Inc. provides Wi-Fi roaming services for enterprise employees and service provider subscribers. The company operates through the Mobility Services segment. The Mobility Services segment offers open mobile enterprise and open mobile exchange services. This segment also helps enterprises manage the networks, connections and devices used by their mobile workforce. Its open mobile platform is a cloud-based mobility management that manages network connectivity and subscribers of computing &amp; mobile devices and networks provider. It also allows enterprise to configure and manage its mobility by providing reporting and analytics on mobile usage. iPass was founded in July 1996 and is headquartered in Redwood Shores, CA.
