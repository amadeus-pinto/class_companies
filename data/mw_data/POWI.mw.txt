Power Integrations, Inc. designs, develops and markets analog and mixed-signal integrated circuits and other electronic components and circuitry used in highvoltage power conversion. Its products are used in power converters that convert electricity from a high-voltage source to the type of power required for a specified downstream use. The company&#x27;s integrated circuits and diodes enable compact, energy-efficient AC-DC power supplies for a range of electronic products including mobile devices, TVs, PCs, appliances, smart utility meters and LED lights. Power Integrations was founded on March 25, 1988 and is headquartered in San Jose, CA.
