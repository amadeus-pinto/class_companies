Methes Energies International Ltd. is a biodiesel company, which offers an array of products and services to a network of biodiesel fuel producers. It has developed biodiesel processing equipment to produce biodiesel from recycled oils. It&#x27;s operates through the United States and Canada geographic segments. Its projects include Mississauga and Sombra. The company was founded by John Loewen and Han Swoong Ng on June 27, 2007 and is headquartered in Las Vegas, NV.
