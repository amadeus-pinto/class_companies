Delek US Holdings, Inc. is an energy company, which engages in the petroleum refining; wholesale distribution of refined products; and convenience store retailing. It operates through two segments: Refining and Logistics. The Refining segment includes biodiesel facilities it owns and operates that are involved in the production of biodiesel fuels and related activities, located in Crossett, Arkansas and Cleburne, Texas. The Logistics segment gathers, transports and stores crude oil and markets, distributes transports and stores refined products in select regions of the southeastern United States and west Texas. The company was founded in April 2001 and is headquartered in Brentwood, TN.
