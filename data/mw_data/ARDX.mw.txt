Ardelyx, Inc. operates as a clinical-stage biopharmaceutical company that engages in the discovery, development, and commercialization of minimally-systemic, small molecule therapeutics that work exclusively in the gastrointestinal, tract to treat gastrointestinal and cardio-renal diseases. The company&#x27;s products include Tenapanor, RDX002, RDX009 and RDX013. Ardelyx was founded by Dominique Charmot, Peter G. Schultz and Jean M. Frechet on October 17, 2007 and is headquartered in Fremont, CA.
