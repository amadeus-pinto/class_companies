Live Ventures, Inc. provides specialized online marketing solutions for small and medium business to acquiring profitable companies in various industries that have demonstrated a strong history of earnings power. It operates through three segments: Legacy Merchant&#x27;s Services, Online Marketplace Platform and Manufacturing. The Legacy Merchant&#x27;s Services segment consists of LEC business and velocity local. The Online Marketplace Platform segment provides marketing solutions to a growing base of restaurants to boost customer awareness and merchant visibility on the Internet. The Manufacturing segment manufactures innovative carpets, yarn products, as well as a reseller of hard surface flooring products. The company was founded in 1996 and is headquartered in Las Vegas, NV.
