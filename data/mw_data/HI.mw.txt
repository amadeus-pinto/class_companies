Hillenbrand, Inc. engages in the development, manufacture, and sale of engineered industrial equipment. It operates through the Process Equipment Group and Batesville segments. The Process Equipment Group segment offers machines used for compounding, extrusion, material handling, size reduction, screening, and flow control. The Batesville segment produces and trades burial and cremation caskets, urns, room display fixtures, and provides web-based applications. The company founded on November 1, 2007 and is headquartered in Batesville, IN.
