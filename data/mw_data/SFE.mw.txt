: Safeguard Scientifics, Inc. engages in the provision of operational and management support solutions. It operates through the Healthcare and Technology business segments. The Healthcare segment focuses on medical technology such as diagnostics and devices; and healthcare technology. The Technology segment comprises digital media; financial technology; and enterprise software such as mobile technology, cloud, Internet of Things, and big data. The company was founded by Warren V. Musser and Frank A. Diamond in 1953 and is headquartered in Radnor, PA.
