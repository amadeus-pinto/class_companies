DuPont Fabros Technology, Inc. operates as a real estate investment trust. It owns, develops, operates and manages carrier neutral and multi-tenant wholesale data centers. The company&#x27;s facilities are designed to offer efficient and safe computing environments. DuPont Fabros Technology was founded by Lammot J. du Pont and Hossein Fateh on March 2, 2007 and is headquartered in Washington, DC.
