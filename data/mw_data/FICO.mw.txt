Fair Isaac Corp. engages in decision management solutions. The firm operates through the following segments: Applications, Scores, and Decision Management Software. The Applications segment includes decision management applications designed for a type of business problem or process - such as marketing, account origination, customer management, fraud, collections, and insurance claims management. The Scores segment includes scoring solutions and services. Decision Management Software segment is composed of analytic and decision management software tools. The company was founded by Bill Fair and Earl Isaac in 1956 and is headquartered in San Jose, CA.
