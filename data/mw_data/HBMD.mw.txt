Howard Bancorp, Inc. operates as a holding company for Howard Bank, which engages in community banking. It provides commercial banking, insurance, investments, mortgage banking, consumer finance, and real estate loans through banking branches, the internet, and other distribution channels to businesses, business owners, professionals and other consumers located primarily in Howard County and Maryland. The company was founded by Mary Ann Scully in April 2005 and is headquartered in Ellicott City, MD.
