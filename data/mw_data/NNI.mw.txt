Nelnet, Inc. engages in the provision of educational services in loan servicing, payment processing, education planning, and asset management. It operates through the following segments: Student Loan and Guaranty Servicing; Tuition Payment Processing and Campus Commerce; Asset Generation and Management; and Telecommunications. The Student Loan and Guaranty Servicing segment focuses on student loan servicing, student loan servicing-related technology solutions, and outsourcing services for guaranty agencies. The Tuition Payment Processing and Campus Commerce segment concerns the tuition payment plans and online payment processing. The Asset Generation and Management segment includes the acquisition and management of the company&#x27;s student loan assets. The Telecommunications segment involves pure fiber optic service directly to homes and businesses for internet, television, and telephone services. The company was founded by Michael S. Dunlap and Stephen F. Butterfield in 1977 and is headquartered in Lincoln, NE.
