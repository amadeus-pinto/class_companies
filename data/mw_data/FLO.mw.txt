Flowers Foods, Inc. engages in the manufacture and sale of bakery products. It operates through the Direct-Store-Delivery (DSD) and Warehouse Delivery segments. The DSD segment produces fresh breads, buns, rolls, tortillas, and cakes sold primarily by a network of independent distributors to retail and foodservice customers. It sells its products under the Nature&#x27;s Own, Wonder, Cobblestone Bread Company, Tastykake, and Dave&#x27;s Killer Bread brands. The Warehouse Delivery segment offers snack cakes and frozen breads and rolls under the Mrs. Freshley&#x27;s, Alpine Valley Bread, and European Bakers brands. It delivers its products fresh or frozen to customer&#x27;s warehouses via contract carriers. The company was founded in 1919 and is headquartered in Thomasville, GA.
