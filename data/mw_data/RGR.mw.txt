Sturm, Ruger &amp; Co., Inc. engages in the design, manufacture, and sale of firearms. It operates through two segments: Firearms and Castings. The Firearms segment focuses on the manufacture and sale of rifles, pistols, and revolvers. The Castings segment deals with the manufacture and sale of steel investment castings and metal injection molding parts. The company was founded by William B. Ruger in 1949 and is headquartered in Southport, CT.
