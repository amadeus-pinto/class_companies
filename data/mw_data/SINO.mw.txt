Sino-Global Shipping America Ltd. operates as a global shipping and freight logistic integrated solution company. It provides shipping, chartering, logistics and related services; and also offers inland transportation management services and freight logistic services. The company has offices in Los Angeles, China, Australia, Canada and Hong Kong. Sino-Global Shipping America was founded by Cao Lei on February 2, 2001 and is headquartered in Roslyn, NY.
