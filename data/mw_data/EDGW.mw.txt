Edgewater Technology, Inc. engages in the provision of business and technology services and channel-based solutions. It operates through the following segments: Enterprise Performance Management (EPM), Enterprise Resource Planning (ERP), and Classic Consulting. The EPM segment provides business analytics solutions leveraging Oracle EPM, BI and Big Data technologies. The ERP segment relates with Dynamics AX ERP, Business Intelligence and CRM solutions primarily in the manufacturing space. The Classic Consulting segment is specializes in business advisory, process improvement, organizational change management, merge and acquisitions due diligence as well as domain expertise together with technical services such as digital transformation, technical roadmaps, data and analytics services, custom development and system integration. The company was founded by Shirley Singleton and David Clancey in 1992 and is headquartered in Wakefield, MA.
