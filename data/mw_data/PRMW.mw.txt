Primo Water Corp. is provider of multi-gallon purified bottled water, self-service refill water and water dispensers sold. It operates through two segments: Primo Water and Primo Dispensers. The Primo Water segment consists of its exchange and refill products, which are offered through retailers in each of the United States and Canada. Its products are offered through point of purchase display racks and self-service filtered water displays and recycling centers. The Primo Dispensers segment sells water dispensers that are designed to dispense Primo and other dispenser-compatible bottled water. The company was founded by Billy D. Prim on October 20, 2004 and is headquartered in Winston-Salem, NC.
