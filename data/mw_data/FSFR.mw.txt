Fifth Street Senior Floating Rate Corp. is a closed-end and non-diversified management investment company. Its investment objective is to maximize portfolio total return by generating current income from debt investments while seeking to preserve capital. The company invests primarily in senior secured loans, including first lien, unitranche and second lien debt instruments and lends to small &amp; mid-sized companies in connection with an investment by private equity sponsors. It also invests in senior unsecured loans issued by private middle market companies; subordinated loans issued by private middle market companies; and senior &amp; subordinated loans issued by public companies. The company was founded in May 2013 and is headquartered in Greenwich, CT.
