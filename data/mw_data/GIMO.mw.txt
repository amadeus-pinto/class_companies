Gigamon, Inc. designs, develops and sells products and services that together provide customers with visibility and control of network traffic. The firm serves global enterprises and service providers that seek to maintain and improve the reliability, performance and security of their network infrastructure. It operates through the following geographical segments: United States; Rest of Americas; Europe, Middle East, and Africa; and Asia Pacific. The company was founded by Denny K. Miu, Ted C. Ho, Patrick P. Leong, Thomas K. Y. Cheung, Thomas L. Gallatin and King L. Won in 2004 and is headquartered in Santa Clara, CA.
