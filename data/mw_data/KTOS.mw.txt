Kratos Defense &amp; Security Solutions, Inc. provides mission critical products, services and solutions for United States national security priorities. It operates through the following segments: Kratos Government Solutions, Unmanned Systems, and Public Safety &amp; Security. The Kratos Government Solutions segment includes microwave electronic products, satellite communications, modular systems and rocket support operating. The Unmanned Systems segment consists of unmanned aerial system and unmanned ground and seaborne system businesses. The Public Safety &amp; Security segment provides independent integrated solutions for advanced homeland security, public safety, critical infrastructure, and security and surveillance systems for government and commercial applications. The company was founded on December 19, 1994 and is headquartered in San Diego, CA.
