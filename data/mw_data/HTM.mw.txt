U.S. Geothermal, Inc. is a renewable energy company which engages in the construction, management, and operation of power plants which utilizes geothermal resources to produce electricity. It operates through the Operating Plants, and Corporate and Development segments. The Operating Plant segment relates to the sale of electricity from the power plants. The Corporate and Development segment relates to other generating projects. The company was founded by Daniel J. Kunz and Douglas J. Glaspey on March 10, 2000 and is headquartered in Boise, ID.
