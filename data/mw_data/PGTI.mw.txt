PGT Innovations, Inc. manufactures and sells windows and doors. The company&#x27;s impact-resistant products, which are marketed under the WinGuard, PremierVue and PGT Architectural Systems brand names, combine heavy-duty aluminum and vinyl frames with laminated glass to provide protection from hurricane-force winds and wind-borne debris. It also manufactures non-impact resistant products in both aluminum and vinyl frames, including its SpectraGuard line of products. PGT Innovations was founded on December 16, 2003 and is headquartered in North Venice, FL.
