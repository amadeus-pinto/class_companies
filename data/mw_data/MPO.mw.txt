Midstates Petroleum Co., Inc. is an exploration and production company, which focuses on the application of modern drilling and completion techniques in oil and liquids-rich basins in the onshore United States. The company operations are currently focuses on oilfields in the Mississippian Lime play in Oklahoma and the Anadarko Basin in Texas and Oklahoma. It operations also include Upper Gulf Coast Tertiary trend onshore in central Louisiana. Midstates Petroleum was founded in1993 and is headquartered in Houston, TX.
