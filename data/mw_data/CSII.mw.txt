Cardiovascular Systems, Inc. is a medical device company, which develops and sells devices for treating cardiovascular diseases. It focuses on developing and commercializing minimally invasive treatment solutions for vascular diseases. The company&#x27;s products such as diamondback 360&#xB0; PAD system and the stealth 360&#xB0; PAD system are catheter based platforms capable of treating a broad range of plaque types in leg arteries both above and below the knee. The company was founded in 2000 and is headquartered in St. Paul, MN.
