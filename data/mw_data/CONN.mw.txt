Conn&#x27;s, Inc. operates as a retail company, which offers durable consumer goods and related services in addition to a proprietary credit solution for its core credit constrained consumers. It operates through the Retail and Credit segments. The Retail segment sells home appliances to the retail market, and owns and operates retail stores that offer furniture, home appliances, consumer electronics, and home office accessories. The Credit segment offers in-house consumer credit programs that provide standardized credit decisions, including down payment, limit amounts, and credit terms, based on customer risk and income level. The company was founded by Edward Eastham in 1890 and is headquartered in The Woodlands, TX.
