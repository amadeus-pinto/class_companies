Bojangles&#x27;, Inc. operates as a holding company with interests in franchising and operating quick service restaurants. The firm operates restaurants in North Carolina, South Carolina, Georgia, Virginia, Tennessee, Alabama, Florida, Maryland, Kentucky and Pennsylvania, and the District of Columbia. It offers biscuits, sandwiches, salads, combos, boxes, family meals, boneless chicken, fixins, drinks, sweets, and kids&#x27; meals. The company was founded in 1977 and is headquartered in Charlotte, NC.
