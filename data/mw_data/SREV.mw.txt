ServiceSource International, Inc. engages in the provision of business to business companies with technology-enabled solutions. It operates through the Managed Services, and Cloud and Business Intelligence segments. The Managed Services segment consists of end-to-end management and optimization of the recurring revenue process, including quoting, selling, and business intelligence. The Cloud and Business Intelligence segment involves in the subscription sales and professional services to deploy the solutions of the company. The company was founded by Michael A. Smerklo in November 2002 and is headquartered in San Francisco, CA.
