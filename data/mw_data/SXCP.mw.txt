SunCoke Energy Partners LP manufactures metallurgical coke used in the blast furnace production of steel. The company operates its business through two segments: Domestic Coke and Coal Logistics. The Domestic Coke segment consists of its Haverhill and Middletown coke making and heat recovery operations located in Franklin Furnace, Ohio and Middletown, Ohio, respectively. The Coal Logistics segment consists of its coal handling and blending service operations in East Chicago, Indiana; Ceredo, West Virginia; Belle, West Virginia; and Catlettsburg, Kentucky. SunCoke Energy Partners was founded in July 2012 and is headquartered in Lisle, IL.
