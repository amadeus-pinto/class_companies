Kaman Corp. engages in the design, manufacture and distribution of aircrafts, aircraft parts and components. It operates through the following segments: Distribution, Aerospace, and Corporate. The Distribution segment offers power transmission, automation, assembly, fabrication, and repair services. The Aerospace segment produces and sells aircraft bearings, composite aero structures, and arming solutions for missile and bomb systems. The Corporate segment includes cash surrender value of life insurance policies, income tax assets, and fixed assets. The company was founded by Charles H. Kaman in 1945 and is headquartered in Bloomfield, CT.
