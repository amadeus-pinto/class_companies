Ampco-Pittsburgh Corp. manufactures and sells custom designed engineering products. It operates through Forged &amp; Cast Engineered Products and Air &amp; Liquid Processing segments. The Forged &amp; Cast Engineered Products segment produces forged hardened steel rolls used in cold rolling by producers of steel, aluminum, and other metals throughout the world. The Air and Liquid Processing segment produces finned tube and plate finned heat exchange coils for the commercial and industrial construction, process, and utility industries. The firm also produces large custom air handling systems used in commercial, institutional and industrial buildings. The company was founded in 1929 and is headquartered in Carnegie, PA.
