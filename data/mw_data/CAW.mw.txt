CCA Industries Inc. manufactures and markets health and beauty aids, each under its individual brand name. Its products include Plus&#x2B;White toothpastes &amp; teeth whiteners, Bikini Zone medicated topical &amp; shave gels, Nutra Nail nail care, Scar Zone scar treatment products, Sudden Change anti-aging skin care products, Hair Off hair removal &amp; depilatory products and Solar Sense sun protection products. The company&#x27;s products are marketed and sold to major drug, food chains, mass merchandisers and wholesale beauty aids distributors. The company was founded by David Edell and Ira W. Berman on March 25, 1983 and is headquartered in Bergen, NJ.
