Ingles Markets, Inc. is engaged in the operation of a supermarket chain in the Southeast of United States, including Georgia, North Carolina, Tennessee, Virginia, and Alabama. Its products are categorized into the following: grocery, non-foods, perishables and other. The grocery products refer to grocery, dairy and frozen foods. The non-foods products include alcoholic beverages, tobacco, pharmacy, health and video. The perishables products include meat, produce, deli and bakery. The company was founded by Robert P. Ingle in 1963 and is headquartered in Black Mountain, NC.
