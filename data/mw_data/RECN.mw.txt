Resources Connection, Inc. operates as a multinational professional services firm. It provides consulting and business initiative support services to its global client base in the areas of accounting; finance; corporate governance, risk and compliance management; corporate advisory, strategic communications and restructuring; information management; human capital; supply chain management; healthcare solutions; and legal and regulatory services. The company was founded by Donald Brian Murray in June 1996 and is headquartered in Irvine, CA.
