Snyder&#x27;s-Lance, Inc. engages in the manufacture, marketing, and sale of snack food products. The company offers pretzels, crackers, kettle cooked chips, pretzel crackers, cookies, potato chips, tortilla chips, and nuts. It brands include Snyder&#x27;s of Hanover, Lance, Kettle, Cape Cod, Snack Factory Pretzel Crisps, Late July, Tom&#x27;s, Archway, Jays, Stella, EatSmart, Pop Secret, Krunchers, and O-Ke-Doke. The company was founded on December 6, 2010 and is headquartered in Charlotte, NC.
