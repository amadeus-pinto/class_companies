Southwestern Energy Co. is an independent energy company, which engages in the  exploration, development, and production of natural gas and oil. The company operates its business through the Exploration and Production; and Midstream Services segments. The Exploration and Production segment explores and produces natural gas as well as oil. The Midstream Services segment engages in natural gas gathering activities. The company was founded on July 2, 1929 and is headquartered in Spring, TX.
