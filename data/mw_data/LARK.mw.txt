Landmark Bancorp, Inc. operates as a bank holding company of Landmark National Bank, which is a community bank that provides personal and business banking. The company mainly engages in attracting deposits from the general public and using such deposits, together with borrowings and other funds, to originate one-to-four family residential real estate, construction and land, commercial real estate, commercial, agriculture, municipal and consumer loans. Landmark Bancorp was founded on October 9, 2001 and is headquartered in Manhattan, KS.
