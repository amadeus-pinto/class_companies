Miller Industries, Inc. (Tennessee) engages in the manufacture of vehicle towing and recovery equipment. The firm&#x27;s products include wrecker, car carrier, and trailer bodies. Its brands include Century, Vulcan, Challenger, Holmes, Champion, Chevron, Eagle, Titan, Jige, and Boniface. The company was founded by William G. Miller in April 1994 and is headquartered in Ooltewah, TN.
