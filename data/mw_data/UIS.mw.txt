Unisys Corp. is an information technology company, which engages in the provision of integrated solutions to clients in the government, financial services, and commercial markets. It operates through the Services and Technology segments. The Services segment involves outsourcing, systems integration and consulting, infrastructure services, and core maintenance. The Technology segment includes designing and developing servers and related products to help clients reduce costs and improve the efficiency of data center environments. The company was founded in 1986 and is headquartered in Blue Bell, PA.
