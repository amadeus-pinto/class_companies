Medovex Corp. engages in development of medical devices. Its activities include obtaining, development, and commercialization of various intellectual property rights such as patents, patent applications, and knowhow in the field of medical technology. Its pipeline, DenerveX device, provides relief from pain associated with the facet joint syndrome. The company was founded by Steven Gorlin on July 30, 2013 and is headquartered in Altanta, GA.
