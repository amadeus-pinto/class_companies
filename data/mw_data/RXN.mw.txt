Rexnord Corp. is engaged in the manufacturing of engineered power transmission, aerospace, and other precision motion technology products. It operates through two segments: Process &amp; Motion Control and Water Management. The Process and Motion Control segment designs, manufactures, and markets engineered mechanical components such as gears, couplings, industrial and aerospace bearings, and seals which are used within complex systems. The Water Management segment designs, procures, manufactures, and markets products for water quality, safety and flow control enhancements, and conservation. Rexnord was founded in 1892 and is headquartered in Milwaukee, WI.
