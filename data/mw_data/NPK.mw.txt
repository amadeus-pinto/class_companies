National Presto Industries, Inc. engages in the manufacturing of housewares, small appliances, defense and absorbent products. It operates through the following segments: Housewares/Small Appliance, Defense, and Absorbent Products. The Housewares/Small Appliance segment involves in designing, marketing, and distributing house wares and small electrical appliances, including pressure cookers and canners, kitchen electrics, and comfort appliances. The Defense segment offers manufacturing ammunition, precision mechanical, and electro-mechanical products. The Absorbent Products segment includes manufacturing and selling private label adult incontinence products and diapers. The company was founded in 1905 and is headquartered in Eau Claire, WI.
