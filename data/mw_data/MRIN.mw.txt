Marin Software, Inc. is a cloud-based digital advertising management company that enables digital marketers to improve performance of their online advertising campaigns, realize efficiencies and time savings, and make better business decisions. The company&#x27;s integrated platform is a software-as-a-service analytics, workflow and optimization solution for marketing professionals, allowing them to manage their digital advertising spend across search, display, social and mobile advertising channels. Marin Software was founded by Christopher A. Lien, Joseph Chang and Wister Walcott in April 2006 and is headquartered in San Francisco, CA.
