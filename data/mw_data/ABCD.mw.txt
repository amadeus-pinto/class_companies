Cambium Learning Group, Inc. engages in the provision of educational solutions and services. It operates through the following business segments: Learning A-Z, Voyager Sopris Learning, ExplorLearning, and Other. The Learning A-Z segment is a PreK-6 education technology which provides digital resources and tools which supports instruction and student growth in reading, writing, and science. The Voyager Sopris Learning segment refers to the Voyager Sopris Learning and Kurzweil Education brands which offers technology, materials, and professional development for educators. The ExplorLearning segment is the development of STEM-based curriculum tools in the field of math and science. The company was founded in June 2009 and is headquartered in Dallas, TX.
