CNB Financial Corp. operates as a bank holding company, which through its subsidiaries engages in the provision of banking and financial solutions. The company&#x27;s subsidiaries include CNB Bank, CNB Securities Corp., County Reinsurance Co., CNB Insurance Agency and Holiday Financial Services Corp. Its solutions include checking, savings, and time deposit accounts, real estate, commercial, industrial, residential and consumer loans, and a variety of other specialized financial services. The company also offers credit and debit cards. CNB Financial was founded in 1983 and is headquartered in Clearfield, PA.
