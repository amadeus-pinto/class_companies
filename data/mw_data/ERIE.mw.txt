Erie Indemnity Co. engages in the business of insurance and operates as an attorney-in-fact. It perform certain services for the Erie Insurance Exchange relating to the sales, underwriting, and issuance of policies. It manages affairs at the Erie Insurance Exchange for the benefit of the policyholders. It covers policies in auto insurance, business insurance, home insurance, life insurance and retirement. and property and liability insurance. The company was founded Henry Orth Hirt and Ollie G. Crawford in April 4, 1925 and is headquartered in Erie, PA.
