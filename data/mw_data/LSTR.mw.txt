Landstar System, Inc. is an asset-light provider of transportation management solutions. It offers services, with the ability to arrange for individual shipments of freight to enterprise-wide solutions to manage all of a customer&#x27;s transportation needs. It operates through Transportation Logistics and Insurance segment. The Transportation Logistics segment engages in transportation management solutions. The Insurance segment includes risk and claims management services. The company was founded in January 1991 and is headquartered in Jacksonville, FL.
