Salisbury Bancorp, Inc. operates as a bank holding company, which engages in the provision of financial and commercial banking services for Salisbury Bank and Trust Co. Its products include money market and health savings accounts, mortgage loans, checking and savings accounts, retirement plans, business loans, investment management, trust and estate planning, online and mobile banking, and direct deposits. The company was founded on August 24, 1998 and is headquartered in Lakeville, CT.
