ProAssurance Corp. is a holding company for property and casualty insurance companies. The company operates through four segments: Specialty Property and Casualty, Workers&#x27; Compensation, Lloyd&#x27;s Syndicate and Corporate. The Specialty Property and Casualty segment includes professional liability business and medical technology and life sciences business. The Workers&#x27; Compensation segment includes the workers&#x27; compensation business which the company provides for employers, groups and associations. The Lloyd&#x27;s Syndicate segment includes operating results from participation in Lloyd&#x27;s Syndicate 1729. The Corporate segment includes investing operations managed at the corporate level, non-premium revenues generated outside of insurance entities, and corporate expenses, including interest and U.S. income taxes. ProAssurance was founded in June 2001 and is headquartered in Birmingham, AL.
