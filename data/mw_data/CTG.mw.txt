Computer Task Group, Inc. operates as an information solutions and staffing services company with operations in North America and Europe. Its services include information technology solutions and information technology staffing. The company&#x27;s services encompass the information technology business solution life cycle, including phases for planning, developing, implementing, managing, and ultimately maintaining the information technology solution. It develops information technology solutions to address the business needs and challenges of companies in several growth industries, including healthcare, financial services, energy, and technology services. The company provides various other IT solutions which include helping clients assess their business needs and identifying the right IT solutions to meet these needs. It also supports larger companies and organizations that need to augment their own IT staff on a flexible basis. Computer Task Group was founded by Randolph A. Marks and G. David Baer on March 11, 1966 and is headquartered in Buffalo, NY.
