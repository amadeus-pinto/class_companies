Cheesecake Factory, Inc. engages in the operation of chain restaurants. The firm operates through the Cheesecake Factory Restaurants and Other segments. The Cheesecake Factory segment operates as a restaurant. The Others segment comprises of Grand Lux Cafe; RockSugar Pan Asian Kitchen; bakery; and international licensing. Its menu offering includes appetizers, pizza, seafood, steaks, chicken, burgers, small plates, pastas, salads, sandwiches, omelettes, and desserts. The company was founded by David M. Overton, Oscar Overton, and Evelyn Overton in 1972 and is headquartered in Calabasas Hills, CA.
