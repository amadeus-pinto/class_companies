Preformed Line Products Co. designs and manufactures products and systems employed in the construction and maintenance of overhead and underground networks for the energy, telecommunication, cable operators, data communication and other similar industries. Its products include formed wire and related products, protective closures, plastic and other. The company also provides solar hardware systems and mounting hardware for a variety of solar power applications. Preformed Line Products was founded by Thomas F. Peterson in 1947 and is headquartered in Mayfield Village, OH.
