NuStar GP Holdings LLC owns general partner and limited partner interests in NuStar Energy LP. It operates through two segments: Pipeline, Storage and Fuels Marketing. The Pipeline segment owns refined product pipelines, which consist of Central West System refined product pipelines, the East Pipeline and the North Pipeline. The Storage segment owns terminal and storage facilities in the U.S., Canada, Mexico, the Netherlands, including St. Eustatius in the Caribbean, and the United Kingdom. The company was founded in June 2000 and is headquartered in San Antonio, TX.
