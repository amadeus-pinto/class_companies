Qumu Corp. provides the tools that businesses need to create, manage, secure, deliver and measure the success of their videos. It operates through one segment: Enterprise Video Content Management Software. The Enterprise Video Content Management Software segment allow organizations to capture, organize and distribute content across the extended enterprise to a wide variety of end points, including mobile devices. Its video platform supports both live and on-demand streaming, and also incorporates secure download capabilities. The company was founded in 1978 and is headquartered in Minneapolis, MN.
