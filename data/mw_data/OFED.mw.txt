Oconee Federal Financial Corp. is holding company of Oconee Federal Savings and Loan Association, which engages in the attraction of retail deposits from the general public in its market area and investing it, together with funds generated from operations, in one-to-four-family residential mortgage loans and, to a lesser extent, nonresidential mortgage, construction and land, agricultural, and other loans. It also offers personal banking, business banking, and mortgage lending. The company was founded in January 2011 and is headquartered in Seneca, SC.
