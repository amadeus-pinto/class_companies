Owens &amp; Minor, Inc. is a healthcare logistics company which engages in provision of services to the manufacturers of healthcare products, supplies and devices. It operates through the Domestic and International segments. The Domestic segment provides all services in the United States relating to medical supply and logistics company serving healthcare providers and manufacturers. The International segment is comprised of the Movianto Group, which provides third-party logistics for the pharmaceutical, biotechnology, and medical device services in the European market. The company was founded by Otho O. Owens and G. Gilmer Minor in 1882 and is headquartered in Mechanicsville, VA.
