Blue Bird Corp. designs, manufactures, and sells school buses. It operates through two segments: Bus and Aftermarket Parts. The Bus segment is involved in the design, manufacture, engineering and sale of school buses and extended warranties across the United States, Canada and international markets. The Aftermarket Parts segment engages in the sale of replacement bus parts within the company&#x27;s network. Blue Bird was founded by Albert Laurence Luce in 1927 and is headquartered in Fort Valley, GA.
