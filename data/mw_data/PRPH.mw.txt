ProPhase Labs, Inc. engages in the research, development, manufacturing, distribution and marketing of consumer products, natural base health products and other supplements. Its products include Cold-EEZE, Tk supplement, natural allergy relief, cold and flu relief and Organix Rx Complete. The company was founded by Guy J. Quigley and Charles A. Phillips in July 1989 and is headquartered in Doylestown, PA.
