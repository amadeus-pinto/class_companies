Halc&#xF3;n Resources Corp. is an energy company, which engages in the acquisition, production, exploration and development of onshore liquids-rich oil and natural gas assets in the U.S. Its operations include Delaware Basin, located in Southern Delaware Basin; Bakken and Three Forks, located in North Dakota and Non-Core Acreage, located in Ohio and Pennsylvania. Halc&#xF3;n Resources was founded in 1987 and is headquartered in Houston, TX.
