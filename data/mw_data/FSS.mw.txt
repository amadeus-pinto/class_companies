Federal Signal Corp. engages in the design, and manufacture of products and integrated solutions for municipal, governmental, industrial, and commercial customers. It operates through Environmental Solutions, and Safety and Security Systems segments. The Environment Solutions segment engages in the manufacture and supply of full range of street sweeper vehicles, sewer cleaner and vacuum loader trucks, hydro-excavation trucks and water blasting equipment. The Safety and Security Systems segment provides comprehensive systems and products which law enforcement, fire rescue, emergency medical services, campuses, military facilities, and industrial sites use to protect people and property. The company was founded in 1901 and is headquartered in Oak Brook, IL.
