PDC Energy, Inc. is a natural gas and crude oil company. It operates through the Oil and Gas Exploration, and Production; and Gas Marketing segments. The Oil and Gas Exploration and Production segment engages in the production and sale of natural gas, natural gas liquids and crude oil. The Gas Marketing segment consists of Riley Natural Gas co.&#x27;s activities. The company was founded in 1969 and is headquartered in Denver, CO.
