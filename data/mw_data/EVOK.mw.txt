Evoke Pharma, Inc. is a pharmaceutical company, which engages in the development of drugs for the treatment of gastrointestinal disorders and diseases. It develops EVK-001 for the relief of symptoms associated with acute and recurrent diabetic gastroparesis in women. The company was founded by Matthew J. D&#x27;Onofrio, Cam L. Garner, Scott L. Glenn and David A. Gonyer on January 29, 2007 and is headquartered in Solana Beach, CA.
