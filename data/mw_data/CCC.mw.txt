Calgon Carbon Corp. engages in the manufacture, supply, reactivation, and application of activated carbon. The firm also provides ballast water treatment, ultraviolet light disinfection, and advanced ion-exchange technologies. It operates through the following segments: Activated Carbon and Service, Equipment, and Consumer. The Activated Carbon and Service segment manufactures granular and powdered activated carbon for use in applications to remove organic compounds from water, air, and other liquids and gases. The Equipment segment provides solutions to customers&#x27; air, water, and other liquid purification problems through the design, fabrication and operation of equipment systems that utilize the firm&#x27;s enabling technologies. The Consumer segment supplies activated carbon for use in medical, military, and industrial applications. The company was founded in 1967 and is headquartered in Moon Township, PA.
