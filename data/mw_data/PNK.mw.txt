Pinnacle Entertainment, Inc. owns, operates and develops the casinos, related hospitality and entertainment facilities. It also providing guests with their favorite games, restaurants, hotel accommodations, entertainment and other amenities in attractive surroundings with guest service and guest rewards programs. The company operates through three segments: Midwest segment, South segment and West segment. Pinnacle Entertainment was founded on July 23, 2015 and was headquarter in Las Vegas, NV.
