Control4 Corp. engages in the provision of automation and control solutions for the connected home. The firm provides consumers with the ability to integrate music, video, lighting, temperature, security, communications and, other functionalities into a unified home automation solution. Its software services delivers through controller products, cloud services, and user-interface products; as well as sells third-party connected devices through independent dealer channels and online store available on dealer website. The company was founded by W. Eric Smith, William B. West and Mark J. Morgan on March 27, 2003 and is headquartered in Salt Lake City, UT.
