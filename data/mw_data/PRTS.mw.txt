U.S. Auto Parts Network, Inc. engages in the provision of automotive aftermarket parts and repair information through its online platforms. The firm&#x27;s flagship websites include www.autopartswarehouse.com, www.carparts.com, www.jcwhitney.com, www.automd.com, and www.usautoparts.net. Its products include auto parts, including body parts, engine parts, performance parts and accessories. It operates through the Base USAP and AutoMD segments. The Base USAP segment comprises its core auto parts business. The AutoMD segment segment offers online automotive repair source. The company was founded by Sol Khazani and Mehran Nia in 1995 and is headquartered in Carson, CA.
