Almost Family, Inc. engages in the provision of home health nursing, rehabilitation, and personal care services. It operates through the Home Health and Healthcare Innovations division. The Home Health division includes the Visiting Nurse and Personal Care segments. the Visiting Nurse segment provides skilled medical services in patients&#x27; homes largely to enable recipients to reduce or avoid periods of hospitalization and/or nursing home care. The Healthcare Innovation segment offers services paraprofessional staff such as home health aides that are provided in patients&#x27; homes. The Healthcare Innovations division includes activities in the technology, information, population health management, risk-sharing, care coordination and transitions, clinical advancements, enhanced patient engagement, and informed clinical decision. The company was founded in 1976 and is headquartered in Louisville, KY.
