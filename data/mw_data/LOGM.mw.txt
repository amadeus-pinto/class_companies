LogMeIn, Inc. develops and markets a suite of remote access, remote support and collaboration solutions that provide instant, secure connections between internet enabled devices. It provides essential cloud based services for remote access, device management, data management, customer care and collaboration. The company&#x27;s product line includes Gravity, LogMeIn Free, LogMeIn Pro, LogMeIn Central, LogMeIn Rescue, LogMeIn Rescue Mobile, LogMeIn Backup, join.me, Pachube and RemotelyAnywhere. LogMeIn was founded by Michael K. Simon and Marton B. Anka in February 2003 and is headquartered in Woburn, MA.
