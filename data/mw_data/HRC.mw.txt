Hill-Rom Holdings, Inc. is a medical technology company, which focuses on patient care solutions that improve clinical and economic outcomes. It operates through the following segments: North America Patient Support Systems, International Patient Support Systems, Front Line Care, and Surgical Solutions. The North America Patient Support Systems segment sells and rents frames and surfaces and mobility solutions, as well as clinical workflow solutions, in the U.S. and Canada. The International Patient Support Systems segment sells and rents similar products as its North America Patient Support Systems segment in regions outside of the U.S. and Canada. The Front Line Care segment sells and rents respiratory care products, and sells medical diagnostic equipment and a diversified portfolio of devices that assess, diagnose, treat, and manage a variety of illnesses and diseases. The Surgical Solutions segment sells surgical products globally. The company was founded on August 7, 1969 and is headquartered in Batesville, IN.
