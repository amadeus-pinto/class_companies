Universal Stainless &amp; Alloy Products, Inc. engages in the manufacture and market of semi-finished and finished specialty steels, including stainless steel, nickel alloys, tool steel, and certain other alloyed steels. Its products includes specialty bar, forging quality billet, ingots, plate, specialty shapes, and coil products, which are sold to service centers, forgers, re-rollers, original equipment manufactures, and wire re-drawers. The company was founded in 1994 and is headquartered in Bridgeville, PA.
