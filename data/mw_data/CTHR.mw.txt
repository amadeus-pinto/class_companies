Charles &amp; Colvard Ltd. engages in the manufacturing, marketing, distribution and sale of moissanite jewels and finished jewelry featuring moissanite for sale in the worldwide jewelry market. It also sells moissanite jewels to jewel distributors and jewelry manufacturers and finished moissanite jewelry primarily to retailers and end consumers. It operates through the following business segments: Wholesale, Moissanite.com, and Charles and Colvard Direct. The company was founded in 1995 and is headquartered in Morrisville, NC.
