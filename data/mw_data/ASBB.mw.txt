ASB Bancorp, Inc. operates as a holding company for Asheville Savings Bank. Through its subsidiary, it attracts deposits from the general public and use those funds to originate primarily one-to four- loans and commercial real estate loans, and, to a lesser extent, home equity loans and lines of credit, consumer loans, construction and land development loans, and commercial and industrial loans. The company was founded on May 12, 2011 and is headquartered in Asheville, NC.
