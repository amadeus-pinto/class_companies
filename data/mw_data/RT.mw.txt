Ruby Tuesday, Inc. owns and operates the Ruby Tuesday casual dining restaurant chain, which engages in the provision of food and services. It offers franchises for the Ruby Tuesday concepts in domestic and international markets. Its restaurants serve handcrafted burgers, garden bar, steaks, fresh chicken, crab cakes, lobster, salmon, tilapia and fork-tender ribs. It also own, operate, and franchise the Lime Fresh fast casual restaurant concept. The company was founded in 1972 and is headquartered in Maryville, TN.
