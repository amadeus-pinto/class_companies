Waterstone Financial, Inc. operates as a bank holding company. The company through its subsidiary, WaterStone Bank SSB, provides principal lending activities, which includes originating one-to-four family and multi-family residential real estate loans for retention in its portfolio. It offers home equity loans and lines of credit, construction and land loans, commercial real estate and commercial business loans, and consumer loans. Waterstone Financial was founded in June 2013 and is headquartered in Wauwatosa, WI.
