Acuity Brands, Inc. provides lighting and building management solutions for the commercial, institutional, industrial, infrastructure and residential applications throughout North America and selects international markets. Its lighting and system control product lines include Lithonia Lighting, Holophane, Peerless, Mark Architectural Lighting, Hydrel, American Electric Lighting, Gotham, Carandini, RELOC, Antique Street Lamps, Tersen, Winona Lighting, Synergy Lighting Controls, Sensor Switch, Lighting Control &amp; Design, Dark to Light, ROAM, Sunoptics, acculamp, Healthcare Lighting and eldoLED. The company was founded in 2001 and is headquartered in Atlanta, GA.
