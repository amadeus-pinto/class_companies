Clear Channel Outdoor Holdings, Inc. operates as an outdoor advertising company, which provides advertising opportunities through billboards, street furniture displays, transit displays and other out-of-home advertising displays, such as wallscapes and spectaculars, which it owns or operates in key markets worldwide. It operates its business through two segments: Americas Outdoor Advertising and International Outdoor Advertising. The Americas Outdoor Advertising segment&#x27;s assets consist of billboards, street furniture &amp; transit displays, airport displays, mall displays and wallscapes &amp; other spectaculars which operate in the United States, Canada and Latin America. The International Outdoor Advertising segment&#x27;s assets consist of street furniture &amp; transit displays, billboards, mall displays, smartbike schemes, wallscapes and other spectaculars which operate in Asia, Australia and Europe. The company was founded in August 1995 and is headquartered in San Antonio, TX.
