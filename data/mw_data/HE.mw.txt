Hawaiian Electric Industries, Inc. operates as a holding company, which engages in the electric utility and banking businesses through its subsidiaries. It operates through the following segments: Electric Utility, Bank, and Other. The Electric Utility segment involves in the generation, purchase, transmission, distribution, and sale of electric energy. The Bank segment offers banking services to individual and business customers through its branch system. The Other segment includes amounts for the holding companies and subsidiaries. The company was founded by C. Dudley Pratt Jr. in 1981 and is headquartered in Honolulu, HI.
