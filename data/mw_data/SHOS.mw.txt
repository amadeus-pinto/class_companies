Sears Hometown &amp; Outlet Stores, Inc. engages in the sale of home appliances, hardware, tools and lawn and garden equipment. The company operates through Sears Hometown &amp; Hardware, and Sears Outlet segments.  The Sears Hometown &amp; Hardware segment further operates through the following formats: Hometown Stores, Hardware Stores, and Home Appliance Showrooms. The Hometown Stores format provides selection of merchandise categories, including home appliances, lawn and garden equipment, tools, sporting goods, and household goods, with the majority of business driven by big-ticket home appliance and lawn and garden sales. The Hardware Stores format refers to selection of merchandise categories with sales primarily driven by home appliances, lawn and garden equipment, tools, and other home improvement products. The Home Appliance Showrooms format relates to  home appliances and related services in stores primarily located in strip malls and lifestyle centers of metropolitan areas. The  Sears Outlet segment designed to provide in-store and online access to purchase outlet-value products across a broad assortment of merchandise categories, including home appliances, mattresses, apparel, sporting goods, lawn and garden equipment, tools, and other household goods, including furniture. The company was founded on April 23, 2012 and is headquartered in Hoffman Estates, IL.
