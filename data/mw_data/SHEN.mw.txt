Shenandoah Telecommunications Co. (Virginia) is a holding company, which engages in the provision of regulated and unregulated telecommunication services to end-user customers and other telecommunications providers. It offers voice, video and data communications services. It operates through the following segments: Wireless, Cable, and Wireline. The Wireless segment provides digital wireless telephone and data network services. The Cable segment offers video, voice, and data services. The Wireline segment consists of  regulated and unregulated voice services, Digital Subscriber Line Internet access, cable modem, and long distance access service. The company was founded in 1902 and is headquartered in Edinburg, VA.
