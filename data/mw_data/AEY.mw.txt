ADDvantage Technologies Group, Inc. distributes and supplies electronics and hardware for the cable television industry. It operates through the Cable Television and Telecommunications business segments. The Cable TV segment sells new, surplus, and re-manufactured cable television equipment to cable operators or other resellers that sell to these customers throughout North America, Central America, and South America. The Telecommunications segment provides telecommunication networking equipment to its world-wide customer base of telecommunications providers and resellers by utilizing its inventory from a broad range of manufacturers as well as other supply channels. The company was founded in September 1989 and is headquartered in Broken Arrow, OK.
