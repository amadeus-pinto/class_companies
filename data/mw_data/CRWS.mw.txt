Crown Crafts, Inc. engages in the design, market, and distribution of infant, toddler, and juvenile consumer products. It operates under NoJo brand. Its products include: Infant and Toddler bedding and blankets; Accessories for Babies on the Go; Room Decor; and Nursery Basics. The company was founded by Philip Bernstein in 1957 and is headquartered in Gonzales, LA.
