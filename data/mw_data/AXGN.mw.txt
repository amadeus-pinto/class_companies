AxoGen, Inc. offers surgical solutions for peripheral nerve injuries. It focuses specifically on the science, development and commercialization of technologies for peripheral nerve regeneration and repair. Its products include Avance Nerve Graft, AxoGuard Nerve Protector and AxoGuard Nerve Connector. The company&#x27;s portfolio of products is available in the United States, Canada, the United Kingdom, and several other European and international countries. AxoGen was founded in 1977 and is headquartered in Alachua, FL.
