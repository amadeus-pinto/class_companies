Century Communities, Inc. engages in the provision of homebuilding services. Its activities include land acquisition and development, design, construction, marketing, sale, and management of residential projects. It operates through the following geographical segments: Atlanta, Central Texas, Colorado, Houston and Nevada. It builds single-family attached and detached homes in metropolitan areas in Colorado, Austin &amp; San Antonio, Texas, Houston, Texas, Las Vegas, Nevada &amp; Atlanta, Georgia. The company was founded by Dale Francescon and Robert J. Francescon in 2000 and is headquartered in Greenwood Village, CO.
