Famous Dave&#x27;s of America, Inc. owns, operates and franchises barbeque restaurants. It offers catering services in corporate events, weddings, graduation, personal celebrations. The company has three kinds of menus, such as dine in menu, to go menu and catering. It also offers online order. The company runs it operation in United States. Famous Dave&#x27;s of America was founded by David W. Anderson on March 14, 1994 and is headquartered in Minnetonka, MN.
