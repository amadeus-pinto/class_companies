Energy Recovery, Inc. engages in the development, manufacture, and sale of energy recovery devices. It operates through the Water; and Oil &amp; Gas segments. The Water segment deals with reverse osmosis water desalination, circulation pumps, pressure exchanger, and turbochargers. The Oil &amp; Gas segments manages hydraulic fracturing and gas processing plants. The company was founded in April 1992 and is headquartered in San Leandro, CA.
