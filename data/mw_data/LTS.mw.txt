Ladenburg Thalmann Financial Services, Inc. is a holding company, which engages in investment banking and research, institutional sales and trading, asset management services, wholesale life insurance brokerage and trust services through its principal subsidiaries. It operates through the following segments: Independent Brokerage and Advisory Services, Ladenburg, and Insurance Brokerage. The Independent Brokerage and Advisory segment is the broker-dealer and investment advisory services provided by the independent contractor financial advisors of the company and the wealth management services provided by Premier Trust. The Ladenburg Segment is comprised of the investment banking, sales and trading, asset management services and investment activities conducted by Ladenburg and Ladenburg Thalmann Asset Management Inc. The Insurance Brokerage segment is the wholesale insurance brokerage activities provided by Highland, which delivers life insurance, fixed and equity indexed annuities and long-term care solutions to investment insurance providers. The company was founded in February 1996 and is headquartered in Miami, FL.
