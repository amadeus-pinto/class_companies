MTGE Investment Corp. is a mortgage real estate investment trust. It is engaged in the investment and management of leveraged portfolios of agency, non-agency and other mortgage-related investments. The company&#x27;s investment objective is to provide returns to its stockholders over the long-term through a combination of dividends and net book value appreciation. MTGE Investment was founded on March 15, 2011 and is headquartered in Bethesda, MD.
