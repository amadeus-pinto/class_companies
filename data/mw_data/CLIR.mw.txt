ClearSign Combustion Corp. engages on the design and development of technologies for the purpose of improving key performance characteristics of combustion systems, including emission and operational performance, energy efficiency, and overall cost-effectiveness. Its product, Duplex Plug &amp; Play, provides a simple, pre-engineered, and standardized direct burner replacement solution for traditional refinery heaters where Duplex technology can be applied. The company was founded by Richard F. Rutkowski and David B. Goodson and Geoffrey D. Osler on January 23, 2008 and is headquartered in Seattle, WA.
