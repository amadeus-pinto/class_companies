Monolithic Power Systems, Inc. is a power solutions company. Its activities include design, development, marketing and sale of high-performance power solutions used in the field of communications, storage, and computing, consumer and industrial. It offers power conversion, lighting and illumination, E-fuse and switches, as well as battery management. The company was founded by Michael R. Hsing and James C. Moyer on August 22, 1997 and is headquartered in San Jose, CA.
