Xenia Hotels &amp; Resorts, Inc. is a real estate investment trust company, which engages in the investment in premium full services, lifestyle, and urban upscale hotels. It also owns a diversified portfolio of lodging properties operated by Marriott, Kimpton, Hyatt, Aston, Fairmong, and Loews. The company was founded in 2007 and is headquartered in Orlando, FL.
