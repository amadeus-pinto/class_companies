Unifi, Inc. engages in the manufacturing, processing and selling of multi-filament polyester and nylon textured yarns. It operates through the following segments: Polyester, Nylon, and International. The Polyester segment produces chip, partially-oriented, textured, dyed, twisted, beamed, and draw wound yarns. The Nylon segment offers textured nylon and covered spandex yarns. The International segment includes textured polyester and various types of resale yarns and staple fibers. The company was founded by George Allen Mebane IV in 1969 and is headquartered in Greensboro, NC.
