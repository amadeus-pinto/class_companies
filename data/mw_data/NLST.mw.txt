Netlist, Inc. designs, manufactures and sells memory subsystems for the datacenter server, high performance computing, and communications markets. Its memory subsystems consist of combinations of dynamic random access memory integrated circuits, NAND flash memory, application-specific integrated circuits and other components assembled on printed circuit boards. It primarily markets and sells products to original equipment manufacturer customers. The company was founded by Jayesh Bhakta, Chun Ki Hong, and Christopher Lopes in June 2000 and is headquartered in Irvine, CA.
