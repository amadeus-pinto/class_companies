GSE Systems, Inc. provides real-time high-fidelity simulation. It provides simulation, educational, and engineering solutions and services to the nuclear and fossil electric utility industry and the chemical and petrochemical industries. The company operates through the following segments: Performance Improvement Solutions and Nuclear Industry Training &amp; Consulting. The Performance Improvement Solutions segment includes simulation products, engineering services, and operation training systems delivered across the industries. This segment primarily nuclear and fossil fuel power generation, and the process industries. The Nuclear Industry Training &amp; Consulting segment provides nuclear operations instructors and other consultants to the nuclear power industry. GSE Systems was founded on March 30, 1994 and is headquartered in Sykesville, MD.
