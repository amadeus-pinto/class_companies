Customers Bancorp, Inc. operates as a bank holding company. Through its subsidiary, Customers bank, it offers financial services to small and medium-sized businesses, professionals, individuals and families through branch locations in Pennsylvania, New York and New Jersey. It provides financial products and services to small and middle market businesses, not-for-profits, and consumers through its branches. It also provides liquidity to the mortgage market nationwide through the operation of its loans to mortgage banking companies. The company was founded on April 7, 2010 and is headquartered in Wyomissing, PA.
