TherapeuticsMD, Inc. is a healthcare company, which focuses on developing and commercializing products for women. Its technology, SYMBODA, develops advanced hormone therapy pharmaceutical products to enable delivery of bio-identical hormones through a variety of dosage forms and administration routes. The company also manufactures and distributes branded and generic prescription prenatal vitamins, as well as over-the-counter vitamins under the vitaMedMD and BocaGreenMD brands. TherapeuticsMD was founded by Robert G. Finizio and Brian A. Bernick in 2008 and is headquartered in Boca Raton, FL.
