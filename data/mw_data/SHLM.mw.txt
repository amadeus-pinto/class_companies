A. Schulman, Inc. engages in the supply of plastic compounds and resins. Its principal product lines consist of proprietary and custom-formulated engineered plastic compounds, color concentrates and additives. The company serves to the packaging, mobility, building &amp; construction, electronics &amp; electrical, agriculture, personal care &amp; hygiene, sports, leisure &amp; home, and custom services markets. A. Schulman also offers custom performance colors, masterbatch solutions, engineered plastics, specialty powders, and distribution services. The company was founded by Alex Schulman in 1928 and is headquartered in Fairlawm, OH.
