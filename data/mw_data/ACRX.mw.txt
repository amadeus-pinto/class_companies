AcelRx Pharmaceuticals, Inc. is a specialty pharmaceutical company, which engages in the development and commercialization of innovative therapies for the treatment of moderate-to-severe acute pain. Its product candidates is comprised of DSUVIA, and ZALVISO, which both contains sufentanil as a therapeutic ingredient. The company was founded by Thomas A. Schreck and Pamela P. Palmer on July 13, 2005 and is headquartered in Redwood City, CA.
