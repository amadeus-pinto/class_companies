Old Line Bancshares, Inc. is a bank holding company, which engages in the provision of commercial banking services. It offers various deposit products, including demand, negotiable order of withdrawal, money market, time, and savings accounts, as well as certificates of deposit. Its loan portfolio includes short to medium term commercial business, real estate, real estate construction, mortgage, home equity, and consumer installment loans. The company was founded in April 11, 2003 and is headquartered in Bowie, MD.
