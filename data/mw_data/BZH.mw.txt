Beazer Homes USA, Inc. engages in the sale and production of new homes. It designs and constructs single-family and multi-family homes. The firm operates through the following geographical segments: West, East, and Southeast. The West segment includes Arizona, California, Nevada, and Texas regions. The East segment includes Indiana, Maryland and Delaware, New Jersey, Pennsylvania, New York, Tennessee, and Virginia regions. The Southeast segment includes Florida, Georgia, North Carolina, and South Carolina regions. The company was founded in November 1993 and is headquartered in Atlanta, GA.
