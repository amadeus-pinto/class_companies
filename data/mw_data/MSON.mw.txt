Misonix, Inc. is a medical device company that designs, develops, manufactures and markets ultrasonic surgical device products. Its products include the bonescalpel cutting system, which is used for surgical procedures of the spine and on maxillofacial procedures; the sonastar surgical aspirator, which is used to emulsify and remove soft and hard tumors; the sonicone wound cleansing and debridement system, which offers tissue specific debridement and cleansing of wounds for effective removal of devitalized tissue and fibrin deposits while sparing viable cells. The company was founded by Howard Alliger in 1959 and is headquartered in Farmingdale, NY.
