Lightbridge Corp. develops and commercializes next generation nuclear fuel technology for reactors. The company operates its business through two segments: Nuclear Fuel Technology and Nuclear Energy Consulting. The Nuclear Fuel Technology segment develops next generation nuclear fuel technology that increases the power output of commercial reactors and reduces the cost of generating electricity. The Nuclear Energy Consulting segment provides nuclear power consulting and strategic advisory services to the commercial and governmental entities worldwide. Lightbridge was founded on January 8, 1992 and is headquartered in Reston, VA.
