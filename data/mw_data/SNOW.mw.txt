Intrawest Resorts Holdings, Inc. is a mountain resort, adventure, and real estate company which delivers distinctive vacation and travel experiences to guests for over three decades. It operates its business through the following business segments: Mountain, Adventure and Real Estate. The Mountain segment includes mountain resort and lodging operations at steamboat ski &amp; resort, winter park resort, mont tremblant resort, stratton mountain resort and snowshoe mountain resort as well as interest in blue mountain ski resort. The Adventure segment includes Canadian Mountain Holidays and aviation businesses that support CMH and provide services to third parties. The Real Estate segment includes real estate development activities, as well as real estate management, marketing and sales businesses, including intrawest resort club group, intrawest hospitality management and playground. Intrawest Resorts Holdings was founded on August 30, 2013 and is headquartered in Denver, CO.
