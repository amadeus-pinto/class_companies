CoreCivic, Inc. is a government solutions company, which solves tough government challenges. It provides solutions to government partners that serve the public through corrections and detention management, government real estate solutions and a growing network of residential reentry centers to help address America&#x27;s recidivism crisis. The company offers CoreCivic Safety, CoreCivic Properties, and CoreCivic Community. CoreCivic Safety covers quality corrections and detention management. CoreCivic Properties engages in the provision of government real estate solutions. CoreCivic Community comprises of growing network of residential reentry centers. CoreCivic was founded by Thomas W. Beasley, T. Don Hutto, and Robert Crants on January 28, 1983 and is headquartered in Nashville, TN.
