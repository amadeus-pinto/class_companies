Axalta Coating Systems Ltd. Manufactures, markets and distributes coatings systems. The company operates its business through the following segments: Performance Coatings and Transportation Coatings. The Performance Coatings segment provides liquid and powder coatings solutions to a fragmented and local customer base. The Transportation Coatings segment provides advanced coatings technologies to original equipment manufacturers of light and commercial vehicles. Axalta Coating Systems was founded on 1866 and is headquartered in Philadelphia, PA.
