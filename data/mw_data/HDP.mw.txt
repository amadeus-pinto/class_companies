Hortonworks, Inc. provides and contributes Apache Hadoop data platform. Its Data Platform provides an open platform that deeply integrates with existing IT investments and upon which enterprises can build and deploy Hadoop-based applications. The company was founded by Robert Bearden, Alan F. Gates, Arun C. Murthy, Devaraj Das, Eric Baldeschwieler, Mahadev Konar, Owen O&#x27;Malley, Sanjay R. Radia &amp; Suresh Srinivas in April 2011 and is headquartered in Santa Clara, CA.
