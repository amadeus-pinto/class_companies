Optical Cable Corp. specializes in fiber optic and copper communication cabling and connectivity products and solutions. Its activities include design and manufacture of copper cable, fiber optic cables, copper connectivity such as field terminable plugs, and test fixture assemblies; fiber optic connectivity such as fiber optic connectors and tools, enclosures, and others. It also provide cable management, freestanding racks and cabinets, rack accessories, shelving, and wall-mount racks and cabinets. The company was founded in 1983 and is headquartered in Roanoke, VA.
