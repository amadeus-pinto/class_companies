Enanta Pharmaceuticals, Inc. engages in the research and development of small molecule drugs for the treatment of viral infections and liver diseases. It focuses on the following disease targets: Hepatitis C virus, Hepatitis B virus, Non-alcoholic Steatohepatitis, and Respiratory syncytial virus. The company was founded by Peter O. Kliem and Gregory L. Verdine in 1995 and is headquartered in Watertown, MA.
