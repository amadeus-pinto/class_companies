Monotype Imaging Holdings, Inc. engages in the development, marketing, and licensing of technologies and fonts for creative professionals and consumer device manufacturers. The firm offers font subscription, embedded solutions, Swyft mobile engagement platform, and Olapic earned content. It operates through the following geographical segments: United States, United Kingdom, Germany, Japan, and Other Asia. The company was founded by Robert M. Givens on November 5, 2004 and is headquartered in Woburn, MA.
