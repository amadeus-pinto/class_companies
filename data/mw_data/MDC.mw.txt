M.D.C. Holdings, Inc. builds and sells primarily single-family detached homes that are designed and built to meet local customer preferences. It operates its business through the following segments: West, Mountain, and East. The West segment includes Arizona, California, Nevada, and Washington. The Mountain segment consists of Colorado and Utah. The East segment contains Virginia, Florida, Maryland, Pennsylvania, and New Jersey. The company was founded by Larry A. Mizel in 1972 and is headquartered in Denver, CO.
