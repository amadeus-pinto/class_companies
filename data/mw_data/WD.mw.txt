Walker &amp; Dunlop, Inc. is a holding company, which engages in the provision of financing and investment sales to owners of multifamily and commercial properties. It originates, sell, and service a range of multifamily and other commercial real estate financing products, including multifamily finance, FHA finance, capital markets, and proprietary capital. The company was founded by Oliver Walker and Laird Dunlop in 1937 and is headquartered in Bethesda, MD.
