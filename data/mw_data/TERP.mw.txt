TerraForm Power, Inc. owns and operates contracted clean power generation assets. The company&#x27;s objective is to acquire assets with contracted cash flows primarily from owning solar and wind generation assets serving utility, commercial and residential customers. It intends to acquire other clean power generation assets, including wind, natural gas, geothermal and hydro electricity as well as hybrid energy solutions that enable the company to provide contracted power on a 24/7 basis. TerraForm Power was founded on January 15, 2014 and is headquartered in Beltsville, MD.
