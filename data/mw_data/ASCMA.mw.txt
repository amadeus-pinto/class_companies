Ascent Capital Group, Inc. operates as a holding company to Monitronics International, Inc., which provides home security alarm monitoring services. It operates through the following business segments: Monitronics, LiveWatch, and Other Activities. The Monitronics segment provides security alarm monitoring services: monitoring signals arising from burglaries, fires, medical alerts, and other events through security systems. The LiveWatch segment is a is a do-it-yourself (&quot;DIY&quot;) home security provider, which offers monitored security services through a direct-to-consumer sales channel. The Other Activities segment consists of Ascent Capital&#x27;s corporate costs, including administrative and other activities. The company was founded on May 29, 2008 and is headquartered Greenwood Village, CO.
