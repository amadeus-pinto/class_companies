Apartment Investment &amp; Management Co. is a real estate investment trust that engages in the acquisition, ownership, management and redevelopment of apartment properties. It operates through two segments: Conventional Real Estate Operations and Affordable Real Estate Operations. The Conventional Real Estate Operations segment is engaged in diversified portfolio of market rate apartment communities, store properties, redevelopment properties and other properties. The Affordable Real Estate Operations segment is engaged in portfolio of properties with rents that are generally paid in whole or in part, by a government agency. The company was founded by Terry Considine in 1975 and is headquartered in Denver, CO.
