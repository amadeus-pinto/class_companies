SigmaTron International, Inc. operates as an independent provider of electronic manufacturing services. It manufactures printed circuit board assemblies and assembled electronic products. The firm also provides automatic and manual assembly and testing of products, material sourcing and procurement, manufacturing and test engineering support, design services, warehousing and distribution service, and assistance in obtaining product approval from governmental and other regulatory bodies. The company was founded in November 16, 1993 and is headquartered in Elk Grove Village, IL.
