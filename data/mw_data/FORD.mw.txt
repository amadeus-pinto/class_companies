Forward Industries, Inc. manufactures and distributes soft-sided carrying cases, bags, clips, decorative faceplates and other accessories. Its original equipment manufacturer products include carrying cases and other accessories for blood glucose monitoring kits and portable electronic &amp; non-electronic products, such as sporting and recreational products, bar code scanners, smart phones, GPS &amp; location devices, tablets, firearms and other products. The company designs and distributes specialty and promotional products for hand held electronic devices made of leather, nylon, vinyl, plastic, PVC and other synthetic materials. Forward Industries was founded in 1961 and is headquartered in West Palm Beach, FL.
