Alliqua BioMedical, Inc. is a regenerative technologies company, which commercializes differentiated regenerative medical products which assist the body in the repair or replacement of soft tissue. The firm builds a suite of technological solutions that allow clinicians to utilize the latest advances in regenerative therapies to bring improved patient outcomes to their practices. Its commercial wound care portfolio consists of the following product categories: wound bed preparation, human biologics, and antimicrobial protection. The company was founded on October 27, 1997 and is headquartered in Yardley, PA.
