Park City Group, Inc. is a software-as-a-service provider. It designs, develops, markets, and supports proprietary software products. Its business suite comprises scan based trading, food safety, distribution center procurement, store ordering, fresh market manager, DSD visibility and strategic analysis, operational insights, ActionManager, vendor managed inventory, store level replenishment, and enterprise supply chain planning. The company was founded by Randall K. Fields in May 1990 and is headquartered in Salt Lake City, UT.
