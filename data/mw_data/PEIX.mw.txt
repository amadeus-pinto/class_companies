Pacific Ethanol, Inc. produces and markets carbon renewable fuels in the Western United States. It operates through the Ethanol Production and Marketing and Distribution segments. The Ethanol Production segment includes the production and sale of ethanol and co-products. The Marketing and Distribution segment includes marketing and merchant trading for company-produced ethanol and co-products and third-party ethanol. The company was founded by William L. Jones and Neil M. Koehler on February 28, 2005 and is headquartered in Sacramento, CA.
