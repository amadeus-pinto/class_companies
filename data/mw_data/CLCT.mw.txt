Collectors Universe, Inc. engages in the provision of third-party authentication, grading and related services for rare and high-value collectibles consisting of coins, trading cards, stamps, sports memorabilia and autographs. It operates through three segments: Coins, Trading Cards and Autographs, and Other High-end Collectibles. The company through these segments provides authentication, grading, publication and web-based advertising, subscription-based revenues, and product sales. The company was founded on February 5, 1999 and is headquartered in Santa Ana, CA.
