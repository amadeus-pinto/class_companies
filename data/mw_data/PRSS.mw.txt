CafePress, Inc. engages in the operation of retail business. It operates an e-commerce platform which enables consumers and business partners to shop, create, and sell products. The store offers a range of products such as gifts ad accessories including t-shirts and apparel, mugs, and drinkware, and home goods such as custom curtains and bed coverings. The company was founded by Maheesh Jain and Fred E. Durham III in August 1999 and is headquartered in Louisville, KY.
