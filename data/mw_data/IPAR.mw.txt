Inter Parfums, Inc. engages in the business of manufacturing, marketing and distributing wide array of fragrances and fragrance related products. Its brands include Prestige, Agent Provocateur, Anna Sui, Balmain, Boucheron, Jimmy Choo, Karl Lagefeld, Lanvin, Mont Blank, Oscar de la Renta, Paul Smith, Repetto, Shanghai Tang, S.T. Dupont and Van Cleef and Arpels, Banana Republic, Bebe and Gap. The company was founded by Jean Madar and Philippe Benacin in May 1985 and is headquartered in New York, NY.
