Cutera, Inc. engages in the design, development, manufacture, marketing and servicing of laser and other energy based aesthetics systems for practitioners. The company engages in sale of aesthetic medical equipment and services, and distribution of cosmeceutical and dermal filler products to qualified medical practitioners. Its products include Enlighten, Excel HR, Excel V, Xeo, and TruSculpt. The company was founded in August 1998 and is headquartered in Brisbane, CA.
