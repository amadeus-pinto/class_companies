Gladstone Investment Corp. is a business development company, invests in small &amp; medium sized companies located in the US. The company objective is to invest in debt securities of established businesses. The fund targets companies operating in the fields of electronics, aerospace &amp; defense, oil &amp; gas, automobile, machinery, beverage, media &amp; communications. The firm makes debt investments in the form of senior debt &amp; senior/junior subordinated debt and equity investments in the form of common equity, preferred equity &amp; warrants for buyouts/change of control, acquisition, growth and recapitalization transactions with an debt investments between $5 million to $30 million and equity investments between $10 million to $40 million. It makes co-investments and takes a seat on the board. It takes minority &amp; majority stakes and holds controlling interests.
