Fenix Parts, Inc. engages in the recycling and resale of original equipment manufacturer automotive products in the United States and Canada. Its primary business is auto recycling, which is the recovery and resale of insurance quality OEM parts, components and systems reclaimed from damaged, totaled or low value vehicles. The company sells recycled OEM products to collision repair shops, and mechanical repair shops, auto dealerships and individual retail customers through centralized distribution facilities. Its recycled OEM products include mechanical parts used to repair worn or damaged components, such as engines and transmissions, and collision repair parts used to repair vehicles typically involved in a collision, such as door assemblies, trunk lids, lights, fenders and other products. Fenix Parts was founded in January 2014 and is headquartered in Westchester, IL.
