FelCor Lodging Trust, Inc. is a real estate investment trust. Its portfolio primarily consists of upper-upscale, full-service hotels that are located throughout the United States and Canada. The company&#x27;s portfolio consists of upper upscale hotels, which are flagged under global brands such as Embassy Suites Hotels, Doubletree, Hilton, Marriott, Renaissance, Sheraton, Westin and Holiday Inn. FelCor Lodging Trust was founded by Thomas J. Corcoran, Jr. and Hervey A. Feldman in 1991 and is headquartered in Irving, TX.
