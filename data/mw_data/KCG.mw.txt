KCG Holdings, Inc. is an independent securities company, which offers investors a range of services designed to address trading needs across asset classes, product types and time zones. It operates its business through the following segments: Market Making, Global Execution Services and Corporate &amp; Other. The Market Making segment principally consists of market making in global equities and listed domestic options. The Global Execution Services segment offers access via its electronic agency-based platforms to markets and self-directed trading in equities, options, fixed income, foreign exchange and futures. The Corporate &amp; Other segment invests in strategic financial services-oriented opportunities, allocates, deploys and monitors all capital, and maintains corporate overhead expenses and all other income and expenses that are not attributable to the other segments. The Corporate &amp; Other segment houses functions that support the company&#x27;s other segments such as self-clearing services, including stock lending activities. The company was founded in July 2013 and is headquartered in Jersey City, NJ.
