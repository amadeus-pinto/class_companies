22nd Century Group, Inc. is a plant biotechnology company, which engages in the development of technology that will allow to increase or decrease the level of nicotine and nicotinic alkaloids in tobacco plants and levels of cannabinoids in cannabis plants through genetic engineering and plant breeding. Its products include X-22, modified risk cigarettes, spectrum government research cigarettes, magic 0 and magic 2, moonlight, red sun, variable nicotine-level research cigarettes, and verfola. The company was founded on January 25, 2011 and is headquartered in Clarence, NY.
