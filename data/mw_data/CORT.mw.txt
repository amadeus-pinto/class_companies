Corcept Therapeutics, Inc. engages in the development of drugs for the treatment of severe psychiatric, neurological, and metabolic disorders. It focuses on the development of drugs for disorders that are associated with a steroid hormone called cortisol. Its products include korlym and korlym support. The company was founded by David B. Singer and Joseph K. Belanoff On May 13, 1998 by and is headquartered in Menlo Park, CA.
