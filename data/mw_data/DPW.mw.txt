Digital Power Corp. engages in the design, development, manufacture, and sale of customized and flexible power system solutions. It offers products with diverse application in the field of telecom, industrial military, and medical field including power conversion, power distribution equipment, and direct current inverters. These include adapters, open frame, capacitor chargers, compact PCI and front end. The company was founded in 1969 and is headquartered in Fremont, CA.
