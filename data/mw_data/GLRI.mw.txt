Glori Energy Inc. engages in oil and gas exploration activities. It uses technology to increase oil production in fields that it acquires and redevelops. The company is a technology focused energy company that deploys its proprietary biotechnology to facilitate the secondary production of oil. Glori Energy engages in three segments: Oil and Gas, AERO Services and Corporate segment. The company was founded on April 15, 2014 and is headquartered in Houston, Texas.
