Michaels Cos., Inc. is an arts and crafts specialty retailer, which engages in the provision of materials, project ideas, and education for creative activities. It operates through the following segments: Michaels-U.S., Michaels-Canada, Aaron Brothers, Pat Catan&#x27;s, and Darice. The company was founded by Michael Dupey in 1973 and is headquartered in Irving, TX.
