Information Services Group, Inc. provides advisory sourcing services. It supports private and public sector clients in transforming and optimizing their operational environments through strategic consulting, benchmarking and analytics, managed services and research with a focus on information technology, business process transformation and enterprise resource planning. The company provides innovative solutions for leveraging technology, the deepest data source in the industry. Information Services Group was founded by Michael P. Connors on July 20, 2006 and is headquartered in Stamford, CT.
