Cousins Properties, Inc. is an integrated, self-administered and self-managed real estate investment trust. It invests in top-tier urban office assets and opportunistic mixed-use developments in Sunbelt markets, with a focus on Georgia, Texas and North Carolina. It operates through the following segments: Office, Mixed-Use, and Other. The company was founded by Thomas G. Cousins in 1958 and is headquartered in Atlanta, GA.
