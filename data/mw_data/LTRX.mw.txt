Lantronix, Inc. engages in the provision of machine to machine and Internet of Things (IoT) solutions. Its products delivers secure connectivity, device management, and mobility. Its product and services includes IoT Gateways, IoR Modules, IT Infrastructure management, Mobile Printing, Software, and Professional Services. The company was founded by Bernhard Bruscha in June 1989 and is headquartered in Irvine, Canada.
