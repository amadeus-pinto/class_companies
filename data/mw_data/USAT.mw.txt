USA Technologies, Inc. provides technology-enabled solutions and value-added services that facilitate electronic payment transactions within the unattended point of sale market. Its provides in the small ticket, beverage and food vending industry and is expanding its solutions to other unattended market segments, such as amusement, commercial laundry, kiosk and others. The company was founded by George Raymond Jensen Jr. in January 1992 and is headquartered in Malvern, PA.
