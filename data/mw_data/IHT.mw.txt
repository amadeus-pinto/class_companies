InnSuites Hospitality Trust is a real estate investment trust company, which engages in owning and managing hotels. It operates through Hotel Operations &amp; Corporate Overhead, and IBC Developments segments. The Hotel Operations &amp; Corporate Overhead segment is consist of hotel operations of Arizona Property. The IBC Developments segment is comprised primarily of general, administrative, sales, and marketing expenses. The company was founded by James F. Wirth in 1971 and is headquartered in Phoenix, AZ.
