Grand Canyon Education, Inc. is a regionally accredited provider of postsecondary education services focused on offering graduate and undergraduate degree programs in its core disciplines of education, healthcare, business, and liberal arts. The company offers programs online, on ground at campus in Phoenix, Arizona, and onsite at facilities it leases and at facilities owned by third party employers. Grand Canyon Education was founded by Christopher C. Richardson and Brent D. Richardson in November 2003 and is headquartered in Phoenix, AZ.
