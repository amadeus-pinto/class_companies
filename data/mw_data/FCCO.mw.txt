First Community Corp. (South Carolina) is a bank holding company, which provides commercial banking services. It operates through the following segments: Commercial and Retail Banking; Mortgage Banking; Investment Advisory and Non-Deposit; and Corporate. The Commercial and Retail Banking segment offers loans and deposits to retail and commercial clients. The Mortgage Banking segment includes real estate loans. The Investment Advisory and Non-Deposit segment provides investment management services. The Corporate segment refers to dividend income, interest, and parent company financial information. The company was founded on November 2, 1994 and is headquartered in Lexington, SC.
