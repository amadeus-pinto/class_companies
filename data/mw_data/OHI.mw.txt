Part of Omega Healthcare Investors, Inc. (Private Equity), Omega Healthcare Investors, Inc. is an American company located in Hunt Valley, MD. The firm functions as real estate investment trust. It was founded in 1992. C. Taylor Pickett is the current CEO, occupying that position since 2001.
