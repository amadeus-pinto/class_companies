Central Garden &amp; Pet Co. produces and markets products for the lawn, garden and pet supplies markets. It operates through two segments: Pet and Garden. The Pet segment consists of Four Paws Products, TFH Publications, Kaytee, Aquatics, Interpet, Pets International, Breeder&#x27;s Choice, and Life Sciences. The Garden segment consists of Pennington Seed, Matthews Four Seasons, Grant&#x27;s, AMBRANDS, Lilly Miller, the Pottery Group, Gulfstream, and GKI/Bethlehem Lighting. The company product offerings fall under the product categories of pet supplies, garden controls and fertilizer products, wild bird feed, other garden supplies, and grass seed. Central Garden &amp; Pet was founded by William E. Brown in 1980 and is headquartered in Walnut Creek, CA.
