US Global Investors, Inc. is an investment management company specializing in gold, natural resources, emerging markets and global infrastructure opportunities around the world. The company operates through three segments: Investment Management Services, Investment Management Services-Canada and Corporate Investments. The Investment Management Services segment offers a broad range of investment management products and services to meet the needs of individual and institutional investors. The Investment Management Services-Canada segment is a privately held Toronto-based asset management firm which offers investment management products and services in Canada. The Corporate Investments segment invests for its own account in an effort to add growth and value to its cash position. U.S. Global Investors was founded in 1968 and is headquartered in San Antonio, TX.
