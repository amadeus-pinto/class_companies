NeoPharm, Inc. engages in the research, development, and commercialization of drugs for the treatment of various cancers and other diseases. The company has built its drug development program around two proprietary technology platforms, a tumor-targeting toxin platform which includes development for a new indication of Pulmonary Fibrosis, and a novel NeoLipid liposomal drug delivery system. It has advanced five drug product candidates in various stages of clinical and pre clinical development for the treatment of cancer and one drug product candidate in pre-clinical development for treatment of interstitial pulmonary fibrosis and asthma. NeoPharm was founded in May 15, 1900and is headquartered in Lake Bluff, IL.
