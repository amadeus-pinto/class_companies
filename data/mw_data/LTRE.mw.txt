Learning Tree International, Inc. provides instructor-led, hands-on courses and certification programs for information technology professionals and managers in business and government organizations. It develops, markets, and delivers a broad, proprietary library of instructor-led courses focused on: web development, IT security, project management, operating systems, databases, networking, software development, leadership, and management and business skills. The firm&#x27;s courses are offered at learning tree education centers and training locations around the world, on-site at client facilities, or via the Internet with Learning Tree AnyWare, the company&#x27;s web-based, remote-attendance platform. The company was founded by Eric R. Garen and David C. Collins in 1974 and is headquartered in Herndon, VA.
