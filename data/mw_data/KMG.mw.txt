KMG Chemicals, Inc. manufactures and distributes specialty chemicals. It operates through the following segments: Electronic Chemicals, Wood Treating Chemicals and Industrial Lubricants. The Electronic Chemicals segment sells wet process chemicals primarily to the semiconductor industry. These are used to clean and etch silicon wafers in the production of semiconductors. The Wood Treating Chemicals segment produces and supplies pentachlorophenol, a specialty chemical for industrial wood treating applications. The Industrial Lubricants segment provide products and services for industrial valve and actuator maintenance. This segment product portfolio includes valve lubricants, cleaners and sealants, as well as related products such as fittings, adapters and application equipment The company was founded in 1992 and is headquartered Fort Worth, TX.
