Jamba, Inc. is a restaurant retailer of beverage and food offerings, which engages in the provision of fruit smoothies, fresh squeezed juices, and juice blends, hot teas, and food items. It includes super blend smoothies&#x2122;, energy bowls&#x2122;, hot oatmeal, breakfast wraps, sandwiches and artisan flatbreads&#x2122;. The company was founded on January 6, 2005 and is headquartered in Frisco, TX.
