Gentex Corp. designs, develops, manufactures, and markets electro-optical products for the automotive, commercial building, and aircraft industries. Its revenue is derived from the production and sales of automotive products in the United States, Germany, and Japan. Its products includes automatic-dimming rearview mirrors, dimmable aircraft windows, and commercial smoke alarms and signaling devices. The company was founded by Fred Bauer in 1974 and is headquartered in Zeeland, MI.
