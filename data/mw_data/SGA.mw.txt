Saga Communications, Inc. operates as a broadcasting company, which engages in the acquisition, development, and operation of radio stations, television stations, and state radio networks. It operates through two segments: Radio and Television . The Radio segment comprises of twenty four markets, which include all ninety one of its radio stations. The Television segment consists of two markets that include four television stations and five low power television stations. The company was founded by Edward K. Christian in 1986 and is headquartered in Grosse Pointe Farms, MI.
