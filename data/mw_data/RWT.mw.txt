Redwood Trust, Inc. is an internally-managed specialty finance company. It is focused on engaging in residential and commercial mortgage banking activities and investing in mortgage- and other real estate-related assets. The company operates through the following segments: Residential Mortgage Banking, Residential Investments and Commercial Mortgage Banking and Investments. The company was founded by George E. Bull III, Douglas B. Hansen and Frederick H. Borden on April 11, 1994 and is headquartered in Mill Valley, CA.
