Atlas Financial Holdings, Inc. operates as a financial holding company with interest in providing insurance underwriting services. The company engages in underwriting commercial automobile insurance policies in the United States. It primarily focuses on insurance for the light commercial automobile sector, including taxi cabs, non-emergency paratransit, limousine, livery and business auto. The company distributes its insurance products through a network of retail independent agents. It operates its business through single segment: property and casualty insurance. Atlas Financial Holdings was founded on December 31, 2010 and is headquartered in Elk Grove Village, IL.
