Northern Technologies International Corp. develops, markets and sells proprietary environmentally beneficial products and technical services. The company operates its business through two business units: Natur-Tec and Zerust. The Natur-Tec business unit manufactures bio-degradable and bio-based plastics for industrial and consumer applications. The Zerust business unit provides corrosion mitigation solutions and services to oil, gas and chemical processing applications. Northern Technologies International was founded in 1970 and is headquartered in Circle Pines, MN.
