EXCO Resources, Inc. is an independent oil and natural gas company. It engages in the exploration, exploitation, acquisition, development, and production of onshore U.S. oil and natural gas properties with a focus on shale resource plays. The company holds interests in various projects located in East Texas, North Louisiana, Appalachia and the Permian Basin in West Texas. EXCO Resources was founded in October 1955 and is headquartered in Dallas, TX.
