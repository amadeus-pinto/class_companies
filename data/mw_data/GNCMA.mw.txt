General Communication, Inc. is a holding company. It provides wireless telephone services, data services, Internet access, video services and local and long-distance voice services, to residential and business customers across the state under its GCI brand. The company operates through the following segments: Wireless and Wireline. The Wireless segment provides wholesale wireless services to wireless carriers. The Wireline segment offers services and products to the following three major customer groups: Consumer, Business Services and Managed Broadband. General Communication was founded by Robert M. Walp and Ronald A. Duncan in 1979 and is headquartered in Anchorage, AK.
