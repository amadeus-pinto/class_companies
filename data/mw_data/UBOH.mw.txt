United Bancshares, Inc. (Ohio) is a bank holding company which operates through its wholly owned subsidiary Union Bank Co. It specializes in banking and financial solutions focuses on commercial banking industry. It offers a range of commercial and retail banking services checking accounts, savings and money market accounts; certificates of deposit; on-line banking and automatic teller machines; commercial, consumer, agricultural, residential mortgage and home equity loans; wealth management services; treasury management services; safe deposit box rentals; and other personalized banking services. The company was founded in 1985 and is headquartered in Columbus Grove, OH.
