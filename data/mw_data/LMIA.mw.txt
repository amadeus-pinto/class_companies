LMI Aerospace, Inc. engages in the development and provision of structural assemblies, kits, and components. It operates through the Aerostructures and Engineering Services segments. The Aerostructures segment involves in producing and selling edge assemblies, wing slats, flap skins, helicopter cabin, wing panels, tail cone assemblies, door components, cockpit window frames, and landing light lens. The Engineering Services segment offers designing and offering wingbox, fixed and moveable edges, fuselage, empennage, weight improvement engineering, and aircraft modification. The company was founded in 1948 and is headquartered in St. Charles, MO.
