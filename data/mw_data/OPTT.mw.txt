Ocean Power Technologies, Inc. is a renewable wave-energy technology company which develops and seeks to commercialize proprietary systems that generate electricity through ocean waves. It offers the PowerBuoy technology which integrates with hydrodynamics, electronics, energy conversion, and computer control systems to extract the natural energy in ocean waves, and the PB3 which harvest energy from the waves. The company was founded by George W. Taylor on April 19, 1984 and is headquartered in Pennington, NJ.
