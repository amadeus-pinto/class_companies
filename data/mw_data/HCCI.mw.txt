Heritage-Crystal Clean, Inc. provides parts cleaning, containerized waste management, used oil collection, vacuum truck services, antifreeze recycling, and field services. It operates through following business segments: Environmental Services and Oil Business. The Environmental Services segment provides parts cleaning, drum waste removal, servicing of parts cleaning machines, containerized waste management, vacuum truck, antifreeze recycling services, other services and sells solvents, machines, absorbents, and accessories. The Oil Business segment provides used oil collection and re-refining, and waste water collection and disposal services and sells re-refined base oils, by-products, and used oils. The company was founded by Joseph Chalhoub in 1999 and is headquartered in Elgin, IL.
