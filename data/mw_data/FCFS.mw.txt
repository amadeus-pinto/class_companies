FirstCash, Inc. engages in service pawn store operation. The company operates through following segments: U.S. pawn operations, U.S. consumer loan operations and Latin America pawn &amp; consumer loan operations. It buys and sells jewelry, consumer electronics, power tools, household appliances, sporting goods, musical instruments and other merchandise, and make small consumer pawn loans secured by pledged personal property. The company was founded on September 2, 2016 and is headquartered in Arlington, TX.
