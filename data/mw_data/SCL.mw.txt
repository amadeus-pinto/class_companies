Stepan Co. engages in the manufacturing and sale of specialty and intermediate chemicals. It operates through the following segments: Surfactants, Polymers, and Specialty Products. The Surfactants segment offers a range of surfactant chemicals such as anionic, cationic, nonionic, and amphoteric surfactants. The Polymers segment produces polyester polyols, specialty polyurethane foam systems, and phthalic anhydride. The Specialty Products segment includes patented, science-based nutritional oils used in the food, nutrition, and pharmaceutical industries. The company was founded by Alfred C. Stepan Jr. in 1932 and is headquartered in Northfield, IL.
