Lincoln Educational Services Corp. provides career education and training. It offers recent high school graduates and working adults degree and diploma programs in five areas of study, including health science, automotive technology, skilled trades, business and information technology, and hospitality services. It operates its business through the Transportation and Skilled Trades, and Transitional segments. The Transportation and Skilled Trades segment offers academic programs mainly in the career-oriented disciplines of transportation and skilled trades, such as automotive, diesel, HVAC, welding and manufacturing. The Transitional segment refers to operations that are being phased out and consists of campuses that are currently being taught out. The company was founded in 1946 and is headquartered in West Orange, NJ.
