Destination XL Group, Inc. engages as a specialty retailer of apparel for big and tall men. It operates under the following trade names: Destination XL, DXL, Casual Male XL, Casual Male XL outlets, DXL outlets, Rochester Clothing, ShoesXL, and LivingXL. The company was founded by Calvin Margolis and Stanley I. Berger in 1976 and is headquartered in Canton, MA.
