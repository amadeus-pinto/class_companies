Continental Materials Corp. engages in heating, ventilation and air conditioning, and construction products. It operates through Heating and Cooling, and Evaporative Cooling segments. The Heating and Cooling produces and sells gas-fired wall furnaces, console heaters and fan coils. The Evaporative Cooling segment produces and sells primarily evaporative coolers. The company was founded in 1954 and is headquartered in Chicago, IL.
