Micronet Enertec Technologies, Inc. engages in the development, manufacture, integration, and marketing of command and control rugged tablets and computer-based systems for mobile resource management, fleet management, and aerospace markets. It operates through the Defense and Aerospace, and MRM segments. The Defense and Aerospace segment designs, develops, produces, and supplies customized military computer-based systems, simulators, automatic test equipment, and electronic instruments. The MRM segment offers mobile computing devices that provide fleet operators and field workforces with mobile computing solutions in challenging work environments. The company was founded on January 31, 2002 and is headquartered in Montvale, NJ.
