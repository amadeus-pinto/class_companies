El Paso Electric Co. engages in the generation, transmission, and distribution of electricity in West Texas and Southern New Mexico. Its energy sources consist of nuclear fuel, natural gas, coal, wind turbines, and purchased power. The company electrical generating facilities include Palo Verde Station, Newman Power Station, Rio Grande Power Station, Four Corners Station, Copper Power Station, and Wind Ranch. It serves residential, commercial, industrial, public authority and wholesale customers in El Paso, Texas and Las Cruces, New Mexico. El Paso Electric was founded on August 30, 1901 and is headquartered in El Paso, TX.
