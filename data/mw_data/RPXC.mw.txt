RPX Corp. provides new patent acquisition services. It also provides patent risk solutions, offers defensive buying, acquisition syndication, patent intelligence and advisory services. The company solutions help clients reduce patent related risk and expense. It offers a subscription based patent risk management solutions that facilitates exchange of value between owners and users of patents. RPX was founded by John A. Amster, Geoffrey T. Barker and Eran Zur on July 15, 2008 and is headquartered in San Francisco, CA.
