Ultralife Corp. engages in energy and battery business. It provides a range of power products and solutions to communications and electronic systems in the field of government, defense and commercial sector. It involves in the design and manufacture of power and communication systems such as rechargeable and non-rechargeable batteries, charging systems, communications and electronics systems as well as accessories and custom engineered system. It operates through the Battery and Energy Products; and Communications Systems segments. The Battery and Energy Products segment refers to batteries using various chemistries including Lithium Manganese Dioxide, Nickel Metal Hydride, Lithium Manganese Oxide, Lithium Polymer, and Lithium Thionyl Chloride among others. The Communications Systems segment refers to the tactical communications solutions that support the warfighter and homeland security such as RF amplifiers, to power supplies, to antennas and accessory products. The company was founded by Arthur M. Liberman in December 1990 and is headquartered in Newark, NY.
