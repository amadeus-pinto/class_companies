Cowen Group, Inc. is a financial services company, which provides alternative investment management, investment banking, research, and sales, and trading services through its subsidiary. The company operates through following business segments: Alternative Investment Management and Broker-Dealer. The alternative investment segment includes hedge funds, private equity structures, registered investment companies and listed investment vehicles. The Broker-Dealer segment offers industry focused investment banking for growth-oriented companies including advisory and global capital markets origination and domain knowledge-driven research and a sales and trading platform for institutional investors, under the Cowen name. Cowen Group was founded in 1918 and is headquartered in New York, NY.
