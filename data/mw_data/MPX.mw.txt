Marine Products Corp. engages in the manufacture and market of fiberglass motorized boats and distributed through its independent dealer network. The company manufactures Chaparral sterndrive pleasure boats including H2O sport and fish &amp; ski boats, SSi and SSX sportboats, Sunesta sportdecks, Signature cruisers, SunCoast sportdeck outboards, Vortex jet boats and Robalo outboard sport fishing boats. The company was founded on February 28, 2001 and is headquartered in Atlanta, GA.
