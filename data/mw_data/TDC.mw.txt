Teradata Corp. provides analytic data platforms, consulting services, marketing, and analytic applications. It operates through the following segments: Americas Data and Analytics; and International Data and Analytics. Its analytic data platforms are comprised of software, hardware, and related business consulting and support services for data warehousing, active intelligence, big data analytics, and data discovery. The company was founded on July 13, 1979 and is headquartered in Dayton, OH.
