Consolidated Communications Holdings, Inc. provides business and broadband communications services. It provides integrated communication services in consumer, commercial and carrier channels in California, Illinois, Iowa, Kansas, Minnesota, Missouri, North Dakota, Pennsylvania, South Dakota, Texas and Wisconsin. The company offers communication solutions including: High-Speed Internet, Data, Digital TV, Phone, managed and cloud services and wireless backhaul over a fiber optic network. Consolidated Communications Holdings was founded by Iverson A. Lumpkin on August 10, 1894 and is headquartered in Mattoon, IL.
