Movado Group, Inc. engages in the design, manufacture and distribution of watches. It operates through the Wholesale and Retail segments. The Wholesale segment includes the design, development, sourcing, marketing and distribution of watches, in addition to after-sales service activities and shipping. The Retail segment includes the company&#x27;s outlet stores. The company was founded by Grinberg Gedalio in 1967 and is headquartered in Paramus, NJ.
