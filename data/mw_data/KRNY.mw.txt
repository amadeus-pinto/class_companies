Kearny Financial Corp. operates as a holding company for Kearny Federal Savings Bank. Its business includes the ownership and operation of the bank. The bank is engaged in the business of attracting deposits from the general public in New Jersey. It uses these deposits, together with other funds, to originate or purchase loans for its portfolio and invest in securities. The bank offers both personal and business services. The company is a unitary savings and loan holding company and conducts no significant business or operations of its own. The company was founded on September 2, 2014 and is headquartered in Fairfield, NJ.
