K2M Group Holdings, Inc. is a global medical device company. It focused on designing, developing and commercializing innovative and proprietary complex spine technologies and techniques. The company&#x27;s complex spine products are used by spine surgeons to treat some of the most difficult and challenging spinal pathologies, such as deformity, trauma and tumor. Its products consist of implants, disposables and instruments which are marketed and sold primarily to hospitals for use by spine surgeons. K2M Group Holdings was founded by John Philip Kostuik and Eric D. Major in June 29, 2010 and is headquartered in Leesburg, VA.
