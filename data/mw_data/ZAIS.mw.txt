ZAIS Group Holdings, Inc. controls ZAIS Group LLC, an investment management company focusing on investments in specialized credit strategies. ZAIS Group LLC invests in corporate and mortgage-related fixed income instruments, CLOs, Non-Agency RMBS, CMBS, residential whole loans, and leveraged loans in the United States and Europe. ZAIS Group Holdings was founded on October 5, 2012 and is headquartered in Red Bank, NJ.
