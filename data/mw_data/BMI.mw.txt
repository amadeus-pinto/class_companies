Badger Meter, Inc. engages in the manufacture and market of flow measurement and control products that serves water and gas utilities, municipalities, and industrial customers worldwide. The firm operates through the following geographical segments: Unites States, Asia, Canada, Europe, Mexico, Middle East, and Other. Its products are used in a wide variety of applications, including water, oil, and lubricants. The company&#x27;s products are classified into the following categories: municipal water, flow instrumentation, and specialty products. Municipal water products comprises of water meters and related technologies to municipal water utilities. Flow instrumentation products involves meters to various industries for water and other fluids. Specialty products offers gas meter radios and concrete vibrators to unique markets. The company was founded in 1905 and is headquartered in Milwaukee, WI.
