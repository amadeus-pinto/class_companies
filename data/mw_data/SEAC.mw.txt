SeaChange International, Inc. engages in the provision of transformative multi-screen video services. Its products and services facilitate the aggregation, licensing, management, and distribution of video and television advertising content to cable television system operators, telecommunications, media companies and individuals. It offers the following solutions: Multiscreen Platform, In-Home, Advertising, and Linear Broadcast. It also provides implementation, management and monitoring, and training services. The company was founded by William C. Styslinger III on July 9, 1993 and is headquartered in Acton, MA.
