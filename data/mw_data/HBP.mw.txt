Huttig Building Products, Inc. engages in the distribution of millwork, building materials, and wood products. The firm offers its services for residential construction as well as in home improvement, remodeling, and repair. Its customer base includes building materials dealers, national buying groups, home centers, and industrial users including makers of manufactured homes. The company was founded by Charles H. Huttig in 1885 and is headquartered in St. Louis, MO.
