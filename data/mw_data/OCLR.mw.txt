Oclaro, Inc. engages in the provision, design, manufacture, and market lasers and optical components, modules, and subsystems for the optical communications, industrial and consumer laser markets. Its portfolio of products for component level includes tunable lasers, pump lasers, external modulators, integrated lasers and modulators, and receivers. It also provides transceivers, transponders, tunable dispersion compensation, amplifiers, wavelength selective switch and controlled subsystems, integrated reconfigurable optical add-drop multiplexer line cards for the module, and subsystem levels. The company was founded on April 27, 2009 and is headquartered in San Jose, CA.
