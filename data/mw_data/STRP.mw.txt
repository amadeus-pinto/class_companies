Straight Path Communications, Inc. engages in the business of leasing and sub-licensing a national portfolio of wireless spectrum and licensing intellectual property. It operates through the following segments: Straight Path Spectrum, Straight Path IP Group, and Straight Path Ventures. The Straight Path Spectrum segment seeks to lease and market fixed wireless spectrum; and offers its customers point-to-point and point-to-multipoint wireless broadband digital telecommunications services. The Straight Path IP segment holds intellectual property primarily related to communications over the internet and the licensing and other businesses related to this intellectual property. The Straight Path Ventures develops next generation wireless technologies primarily for Gigabit Mobility Lab. The company was founded on April 4, 2013 and is headquartered in Glen Allen, VA.
