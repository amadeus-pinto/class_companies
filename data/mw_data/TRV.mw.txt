The Travelers Cos., Inc. operates as a holding company, which engages in the insurance business. It operates through the following segments: Business and International Insurance; Bond &amp; Specialty Insurance; and Personal Insurance. The Business and International Insurance segment offers property and casualty insurance; and insurance related services. The Bond &amp; Specialty Insurance segment provides surety; fidelity; management liability; professional liability; and other property and casualty coverage and related risk management services. The Personal Insurance segment includes property and casualty insurance covering individuals&#x27; personal risks. The company was founded in 1853 and is headquartered in New York, NY.
