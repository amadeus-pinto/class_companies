EnerNOC, Inc. engages the provision of energy intelligence software and demand response solutions It offers software-as-a-service or SaaS which improve how enterprises manage and control energy costs for their organizations. It operates through the Software and Demand Response segments. The Software segment offers a range of solutions which supports enterprise, utility, and energy procurement solutions. The Demand Response segment centralizes demand response event performance and help manage outcomes between the control rooms dispatching the resource and the commercial and industrial end-users providing the resource. The company was founded by David B. Brewster and Timothy G. Healy in December 2001 and is headquartered in Boston, MA.
