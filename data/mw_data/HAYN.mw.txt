Haynes International, Inc. engages in the development, manufacture, and marketing of nickel- and cobalt-based alloys used in corrosion and high-temperature applications. Its products consist of high-temperature resistant and corrosion-resistant alloys. It also offers its products as seamless and welded tubulars, and in slab, bar, billet, and wire forms. The company was founded by Elwood P. Haynes in 1912 and is headquartered in Kokomo, IN.
