Plexus Corp. provides electronic manufacturing services. It provides product realization services to original equipment manufacturers and other technology companies in the wire line/networking, wireless infrastructure, medical, and industrial/commercial and defense/security/aerospace market sectors. It operates through the following business sectors: Networking/Communications, Healthcare/Life Sciences, Industrial/Commercial. Defense/Security/Aerospace. The company was founded by Peter Strandwitz, Shirani Ramin and John L. Nussbaum in 1979 and is headquartered in Neenah, WI.
