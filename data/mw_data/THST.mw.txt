Truett-Hurst, Inc. operates as a holding company, which engages in the production and sale of winery products. It operates through the following segments: Wholesale and Direct to Consumer. The Wholesale segment includes retail brand label model and four fully-owned brands sold and marketed through the three-tier distribution system. The Direct to Consumer segments involves sales occuring through tasting rooms and wine clubs. The company was founded by Phillip L. Hurst, Sylvia M. Hurst, Paul E. Dolan, III, and Heath E. Dolan in November 2007 and is headquartered in Healdsburg, CA.
