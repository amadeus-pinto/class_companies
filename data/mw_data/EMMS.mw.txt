Emmis Communications Corp. is a media company, which focuses on radio broadcasting. The firm owns and operates radio and magazine entities in large and medium sized markets throughout the United States. It operates through the following segments: Radio, Publishing, and Corporate and Emerging Technologies. Its FM and AM radio stations are located in New York, Los Angeles, St. Louis, Austin, Indianapolis, and Terre Haute. The company was founded by Jeffrey H. Smulyan in 1979 and is headquartered in Indianapolis, IN.
