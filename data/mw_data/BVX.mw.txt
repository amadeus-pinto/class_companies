Bovie Medical Corp. is an energy-based medical company, which engages in developing, manufacturing, and marketing a range of electrosurgical products and technologies, as well as related medical products used the offices of doctors, surgery centers, and hospitals worldwide. It operates through the following segments: Core, Original Equipment Manufacture, and J-Plasma. The company was founded by Andrew Makrides in 1978 and is headquartered in Clearwater, FL.
