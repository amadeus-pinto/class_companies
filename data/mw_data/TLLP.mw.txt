Tesoro Logistics LP engages in the ownership, operation, development, and acquisition of crude oil and refined products logistics assets. It operates through the following segments: Gathering, Processing, and Terminalling and Transportation. The Gathering segment consists of crude oil, natural gas and produced water gathering systems in the North Dakota Williston Basin/Bakken Shale area and the Uinta, Vermillion and greater Green River basins. The Processing segment consists of gas processing complexes, including an interest in Green River Processing, LLC, which owns fractionation facility and gas processing complexes. The Terminalling and Transportation segment consists of regulated common carrier products pipeline running from Salt Lake City, Utah to Spokane, Washington and a jet fuel pipeline to the Salt Lake City International Airport. The company was founded on December 3, 2010 and is headquartered in San Antonio, TX.
