Citizens, Inc. engages in the provision of insurance and reinsurance services. It operates through the following segments: Life Insurance, Home Service, and Other Non-Insurance Enterprises. The Life Insurance segment provides interest-bearing cash value accumulation, tax-deferred interest earnings, retirement fund, and financial security. The Home Service segment offers funeral and burial costs. The Other Non-Insurance Enterprises segment provides data processing services and aviation transportation to the firm. The company was founded by Harold Eugene Riley in 1969 and is headquartered in Austin, TX.
