ICF International, Inc. provides management, technology and policy consulting and implementation services to government, commercial and international clients. It helps clients conceive, develop, implement and improve solutions that address complex natural resource, social and national security issues. The company offers services to four key markets: Energy, environment and infrastructure; Health, education and social programs; and safety &amp; security and consumer &amp; financial. The company was founded in 1969 and is headquartered in Fairfax, VA.
