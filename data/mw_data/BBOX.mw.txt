Black Box Corp. engages in the provision of technology solutions. It supports its customers to design, build, manage, and secure their information technology (IT) infrastructure. The company offers its products under products and services platforms. The products platform includes IT infrastructure, specialty networking, multimedia and keyboard/video/mouse switching. The services platform includes unified communications, data infrastructure and managed services. It operates its business through the following segments: North America Products, North America Services, International Products, and International Services. Black Box was founded in 1976 and is headquartered in Lawrence, PA.
