CEL-SCI Corp. is a biotechnology company, which is involved in the research and development of investigational immunotherapy products for the treatment of cancer and infectious diseases. The company&#x27;s lead investigational therapy, Multikine is currently in a pivotal phase 3 clinical trial against advanced primary head and neck cancer. It is also developing its pre-clinical Ligand Epitope Antigen Presentation System technology for the potential treatment of pandemic influenza in hospitalized patients and as a potential vaccine for the treatment of rheumatoid arthritis. CEL-SCI was founded by Maximilian de Clara on March 22, 1983 and is headquartered in Vienna, VA.
