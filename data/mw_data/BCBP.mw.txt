BCB Bancorp, Inc. is a holding company of BCB Community Bank. Its primary business is the ownership and operation of the Bank. The company offers loans, including commercial and multi-family real estate loans, one to four-family mortgage loans, home equity loans, construction loans, consumer loans and commercial business loans. Its retail and commercial banking services includes wire transfers, money orders, traveler&#x27;s checks, safe deposit boxes, a night depository, bond coupon redemption and automated teller services. The company was founded on May 1, 2003 and is headquartered in Bayonne, NJ.
