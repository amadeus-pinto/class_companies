Northwest Natural Gas Co. engages as a natural gas distribution and storage company. It operates through the following segments: Local Gas Distribution, Gas Storage, and Others. The Local Gas Distribution segment focuses on the purchase, sale, and delivery of natural gas and related services to customers in Oregon and southwest Washington. The Gas Storage segment includes natural gas storage services provided to customers primarily from two underground natural gas storage facilities. The Others segment involves the non-utility investments and other business activities. The company was founded by H. C. Leonard and John Green on January 7, 1859 and is headquartered in Portland, OR.
