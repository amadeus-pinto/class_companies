Innodata, Inc. provides business process, technology and consulting services. It operates through the Content Services and Innodata Advanced Data Solutions segments. The Content Services segment provides solutions to digital retailers, information services companies, publishers and enterprises. The Innodata Advanced Data Solutions segment performs advanced data analysis through the Synodex division and the docGenix division. It provides an array of services to help companies both define and implement strategies related to digital data. The company was founded by Todd H. Solomon in 1988 and is headquartered in Hackensack, NJ.
