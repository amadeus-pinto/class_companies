Spark Energy, Inc. engages in the supply and retail of electricity and natural gas. The company operates through two segments: Retail Natural Gas and Retail Electricity. The Retail Natural Gas segment purchases natural gas supply through physical and financial transactions with market counterparts and supply natural gas to residential and commercial consumers pursuant to fixed-price, variable-price and flat rate contracts. The Retail Electricity segment supplies through physical and financial transactions with market counterparts and independent system operators and supply electricity to residential and commercial consumers pursuant to fixed-price and variable price contracts. The firm also provides green energy solutions and offers renewable electricity and carbon neutral natural gas products. Spark Energy was founded in 1999 and is headquartered in Houston, TX.
