National Beverage Corp. develops, manufactures, markets and sells a diverse portfolio of flavored beverage products. Its brands include Shasta, Faygo, Everfresh, LaCroix, Rip It, Asante, Mt. Shasta, ClearFruit, Mr. Pure, Ritz, Crystal Bay, Cascadia Sparkling Clear, Cascadia Only 2 Calories, Ohana, Big Shot and St. Nick&#x27;s. The company was founded by Nick A. Caporella in 1985 and is headquartered in Fort Lauderdale, FL.
