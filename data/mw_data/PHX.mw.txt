Panhandle Oil &amp; Gas, Inc. engages in the acquisition, management, and development of non-operated oil and natural gas properties, including wells located on the firm&#x27;s mineral and leasehold acreage. It explores, produces, and sells oil, gas, and natural gas liquids. Its mineral and leasehold properties are located primarily in Arkansas, New Mexico, North Dakota, Oklahoma, and Texas. The company was founded in 1926 and is headquartered in Oklahoma City, OK.
