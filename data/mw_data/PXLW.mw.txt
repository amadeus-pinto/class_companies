Pixelworks, Inc. engages in the designing, developing, and marketing of video and pixel processing semiconductor, and software and custom application-specific integrated circuit solutions for digital video applications. Its products include mobile, high-resolution displays, projectors, VueMagic&#x2122;, IP cores, and video display processing&#x2122;. The company was founded in 1997 by Robert Y. Greenberg and Allen H. Alley and is headquartered in San Jose, CA.
