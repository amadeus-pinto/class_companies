Hi-Crush Partners LP engages in the production of monocrystalline sand. It owns, operates, and develops sand reserves and related excavation; as well as processing facilities. The company produces a wide range of frac sand and specialized mineral. It also produces Northern White sand with onsite rail capacity to fulfill large quantities of shipment to all major Unites States shale basins. The company was founded on May 8, 2012 and is headquartered in Houston, TX.
