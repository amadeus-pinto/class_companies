MSC Industrial Direct Co., Inc. markets and distributes metalworking and maintenance, repair and operations products to customers throughout the U.S. The company serves primarily domestic markets through its distribution network of customer fulfillment centers. Its products include cutting tools, measuring instruments, tooling components, metalworking, fasteners, flat stock, raw materials, abrasives, machinery hand and power tools, safety and janitorial supplies, plumbing supplies, materials handling products, power transmission components and electrical supplies. MSC Industrial Direct was founded by Sidney Jacobson in 1941 and is headquartered in Melville, NY.
