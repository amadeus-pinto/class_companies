The Brink&#x27;s Co. provides secure transportation and cash management services. Its logistics and security solutions include cash-in-transit; ATM replenishment &amp; maintenance; and cash management &amp; payment services, such as vault outsourcing, money processing, intelligent safe services, and international transportation of valuables. The Brink&#x27;s customers include financial institutions, retailers, government agencies, mints and jewelers. The company was founded by Perry Brink and Fidelia Brink on May 5, 1859 and is headquartered in Richmond, VA.
