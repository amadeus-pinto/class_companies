Uni-Pixel, Inc. develops and markets touch sensor films for the touch screen and flexible electronics market. It markets its technologies for touch panel sensor and hard coat resin for cover glass replacement, and protective cover film applications under the XTouch and Diamond Guard brands. The company also developed a hard coat resin that can be applied using film, spray, or inkjet coating methods for applications as protective cover films, a cover lens replacement, or a conformal hard coat for plastic components. Uni Pixel was founded by Daniel K. Van Ostrand on February 17, 1998 and is headquartered in The Woodlands, TX.
