Zillow Group, Inc. engages in the provision of real estate and home-related information marketplaces on mobile and the web. Its brand portfolio is comprised of Zillow, Trulia, StreetEasy, HotPads, and Naked Apartments. It also houses the business brands Bridge Interactive, Dotloop, Mortech, and Retsly. The company was founded by Richard N. Barton and Lloyd D. Frink on July 25, 2014 and is headquartered in Seattle, WA.
