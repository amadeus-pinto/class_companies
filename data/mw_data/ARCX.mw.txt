Arc Logistics Partners LP engages in the ownership, operation, development, and acquisition of diversified portfolio of complementary energy logistics assets. It engages in the terminalling, storage, throughput and transloading of crude oil and petroleum products. The company focuses on growing the business through the optimization, organic development and acquisition of terminalling, storage, rail, pipeline and other energy logistics assets. the company was founded on July 29, 2013 and is headquartered in New York, NY.
