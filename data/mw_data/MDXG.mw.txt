MiMedx Group, Inc. engages in the designing, manufacturing, and marketing of regenerative biomaterials products and tissue processing services for the wound care, surgical, sports medicine, ophthalmic and dental market categories. Its allograft product families include dHACM family with AmnioFix, EpiFix and EpiBurn brands; Amniotic Fluid family with OrthoFlo brand; Umbilical family with EpiCord&#x2122; and AmnioCord brands; Placental Collagen family with CollaFix and AmnioFill brands; Bone family with Physio brand; and Skin family with AlloBurn brand. The company was founded on July 30, 1985 and is headquartered in Marietta, GA.
