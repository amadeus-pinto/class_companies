Heartland Financial USA, Inc. is a multibank holding company that provides banking, mortgage, wealth management, insurance and consumer finance services to individuals and businesses. The company operates its business through two segments: Community Banking and Retail Mortgage Banking. The Community banking segment involves making loans to and generating deposits from individuals and businesses in the markets where Heartland has banks. The Retail Mortgage Banking segment involves the origination of residential loans and subsequent sale of those loans to investors. Heartland Financial USA was founded in 1981 and is headquartered in Dubuque, IA.
