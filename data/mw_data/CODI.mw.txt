Compass Diversified Holdings, Inc is a traded fund that invests in small and middle market companies located in North America. The fund focuses on companies with a minimum EBITDA of $10 million, highly defensible positions in their markets, strong management teams, and growth potential. It targets companies operating in the fields of industrial &amp; branded consumer industries. It acquires a controlling interest and provides financing in the form of debt &amp; equity for acquisitions with a transaction size of $75 - $500 million.
