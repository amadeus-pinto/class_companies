Bravo Brio Restaurant Group, Inc. owns and operates Italian restaurants in the United States. The company operates full-service Italian restaurants under the BRAVO! Cucina Italiana brand name; Italian chophouse restaurants under the BRIO Tuscan Grille brand name; and full-service American-French bistro restaurant under the Bon Vie brand name. Its restaurants primarily offer Italian food and wine. As of December 25, 2016, it operated 51 BRAVO! restaurants, 65 BRIO restaurants, and 1 Bon Vie restaurant in 33 states. The company was formerly known as Bravo Development, Inc. and changed its name to Bravo Brio Restaurant Group, Inc. in June 2010. Bravo Brio Restaurant Group, Inc. was incorporated in 1987 and is based in Columbus, Ohio.
