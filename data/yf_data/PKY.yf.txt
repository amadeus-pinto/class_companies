Parkway, Inc. operates as a real estate investment trust in the United States. It engages in the ownership, acquisition, development, and leasing of office assets in Houston, Texas submarkets. The company qualifies as a real estate investment trust for federal income tax purposes. It generally would not be subject to federal corporate income taxes if it distributes at least 90% of its taxable income to its stockholders. Parkway, Inc. was incorporated in 2016 and is based in Houston, Texas.
