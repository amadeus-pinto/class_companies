Two River Bancorp operates as the bank holding company for Two River Community Bank that provides banking services to small and medium-sized businesses, professionals, and individual consumers. The company accepts deposit products, such as demand, savings, and time deposits; checking and money market accounts; and certificates of deposit. Its loan products include construction and land development loans; commercial and industrial loans; commercial real estate loans; residential real estate loans, such as single-family detached units, individual condominium units, two-to-four family dwelling units, and townhouses; consumer loans comprising home equity lines of credit, home equity loans, personal loans, automobile loans, and overdraft protection; participation loans; and small business administration loans. The company also offers debit and ATM cards; and remote deposit capture, safe deposit boxes, ACH, money orders, direct deposit, coin counting, night depository, wire transfers, automated teller machines, corporate business, and telephone and Internet banking services. As of February 15, 2017, it operated 15 branches and 2 loan production office in Monmouth, Middlesex, Union, and Ocean counties, New Jersey. The company was formerly known as Community Partners Bancorp and changed its name to Two River Bancorp in June 2013. Two River Bancorp was founded in 2000 and is headquartered in Tinton Falls, New Jersey.
