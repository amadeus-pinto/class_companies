Brinker International, Inc., together with its subsidiaries, owns, develops, operates, and franchises casual dining restaurants worldwide. As of June 29, 2016, it had 1,660 company-owned and franchised restaurants comprising 1,609 restaurants under the Chili&#x27;s Grill &amp; Bar brand name; and 51 restaurants under the Maggiano&#x27;s Little Italy brand name. The company was founded in 1975 and is based in Dallas, Texas.
