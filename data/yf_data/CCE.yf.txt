Coca-Cola European Partners plc, a consumer packaged goods company, produces, distributes, and markets a range of non-alcoholic ready-to-drink beverages in Europe. It serves approximately 300 million consumers across Western Europe, including Andorra, Belgium, continental France, Germany, the Great Britain, Iceland, Luxembourg, Monaco, the Netherlands, Norway, Portugal, Spain, and Sweden. The company was founded in 2015 and is based in Uxbridge, United Kingdom.
