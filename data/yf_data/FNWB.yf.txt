First Northwest Bancorp operates as a bank holding company for the First Federal Savings and Loan Association of Port Angeles that provides various banking products and services in North Olympic Peninsula region of Washington. Its deposit products include checking accounts, money market deposit accounts, savings accounts, transaction accounts, and certificates of deposits.
