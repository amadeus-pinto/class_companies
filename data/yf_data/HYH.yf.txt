Halyard Health, Inc. operates as a medical technology company that focuses on eliminating pain, speeding recovery, and preventing infection for healthcare providers and patients worldwide. The company operates through two segments, Medical Devices and Surgical and Infection Prevention (S&amp;IP). The Medical Devices segment provides a portfolio of products that focuses on pain management, and respiratory and digestive health to enhance patient outcomes. Its products include post-operative pain management solutions, minimally invasive interventional (or chronic) pain therapies, closed airway suction systems, and enteral feeding tubes. This segment sells its products under the ON-Q, COOLIEF, MICROCUFF, MIC-KEY, QUIKBLOC, HOMEPUMP, CORTRAK, and other brand names. The S&amp;IP segment provides sterilization wrap, surgical drapes and gowns, facial protection products, protective apparel, and medical exam gloves for the prevention of healthcare-associated infections. This segment offers its products under the HALYARD ONE-STEP, QUICK CHECK, SMART-FOLD, POWERGUARD, MICROCOOL, AERO BLUE, AERO CHROME, FLUIDSHIELD, PURPLE NITRILE, LAVENDER, STERLING, BLACK-FIRE, and other brand names. The company markets its products directly to hospitals and other healthcare providers, as well as through third-party distribution channels. Halyard Health, Inc. was incorporated in 2014 and is headquartered in Alpharetta, Georgia.
