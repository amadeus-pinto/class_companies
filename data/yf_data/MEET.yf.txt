The Meet Group, Inc. owns and operates a social network for meeting new people on the Web and on mobile platforms in the United States. The company owns and operates MeetMe and Skout mobile applications; and meetme.com and skout.com Websites. It also offers online marketing capabilities, which enable marketers to display their advertisements in various formats and in various locations. The company was formerly known as MeetMe, Inc. and changed its name to The Meet Group, Inc. in April 2017. The Meet Group, Inc. was founded in 1997 and is headquartered in New Hope, Pennsylvania.
