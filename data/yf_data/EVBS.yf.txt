Eastern Virginia Bankshares, Inc. operates as a bank holding company for EVB, a community bank that provides various personal and commercial banking services to individuals and small to medium-sized businesses. It accepts demand deposits, savings and money market accounts, and time deposits. The company also offers lending and credit services, such as mortgage, commercial, consumer, and real estate loans. In addition, the company provides a range of investment, title insurance settlement, and related financial services; and sells insurance products. The company operates through a network of 24 full service branches and 2 drive-in facilities in eastern Virginia, as well as 1 loan production office in Chesterfield County, Virginia. Eastern Virginia Bankshares, Inc. was founded in 1910 and is headquartered in Glen Allen, Virginia.
