S&amp;W Seed Company, an agricultural company, engages in breeding, growing, processing, and selling alfalfa seeds. It primarily offers alfalfa seeds varieties; seed cleaning and processing for other seed manufacturers, as well as conducts a stevia breeding program. The company sells its seeds to distributors and dealers in approximately 30 countries worldwide. S&amp;W Seed Company was founded in 1980 and is headquartered in Hanford, California.
