Digital Power Corporation designs, develops, manufactures, and sells power system solutions for the medical, military, telecom, and industrial markets in North America and Europe. The company provides high-speed switching power rectifiers, including defense and commercial custom power products, server power supplies, front-end, open-frame, enclosed, CompactPCI, MicroTCA, Desktop/Wall-mount Adaptors, Power over Ethernet, and other product solutions. It also offers power conversion, power distribution equipment, direct current/active current inverters, and uninterrupted power supply products; transformer rectifiers for naval use; and value-added services to original equipment manufacturers. The company sells its products directly through its sales force, as well as through a partner network of independent manufacturer representatives and distributors. Digital Power Corporation was founded in 1969 and is headquartered in Fremont, California.
