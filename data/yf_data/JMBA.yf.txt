Jamba, Inc., through its subsidiary, Jamba Juice Company, owns, operates, and franchises Jamba Juice stores. Its restaurants provide specialty food and beverage offerings, such as whole fruit smoothies, fresh squeezed juices and juice blends, and whole fruit and soymilk or fresh Greek yogurt, as well as various food items, including hot oatmeal, hot beverages, breakfast wraps, sandwiches, Artisan Flatbreads, baked goods, and snacks. The company also licenses its Jamba brand name to sell consumer packaged goods through retail channels, such as grocery stores, warehouse clubs, and convenience stores, as well as online. As of October 17, 2016, it operated 68 company stores, as well as 752 franchisee-owned stores in the United States; and 65 franchised stores internationally under the Jamba brand. The company was founded in 1990 and is headquartered in Frisco, Texas.
