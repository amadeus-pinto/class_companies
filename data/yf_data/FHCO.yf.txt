It has operations in the United States, Brazil, Zimbabwe, South Africa, Angola, the Democratic Republic of the Congo, Tanzania, Malaysia, Spain, France, the United Kingdom, and internationally. The company was founded in 1896 and is headquartered in Miami, Florida.
