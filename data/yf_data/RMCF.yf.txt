Rocky Mountain Chocolate Factory, Inc., together with its subsidiaries, operates as a confectionery franchisor, manufacturer, and retail operator. It operates through five segments, Franchising, Manufacturing, Retail Stores, U-Swirl Operations, and Other. The company produces approximately 300 chocolate candies and other confectionery products, including clusters, caramels, creams, mints, and truffles; and offers 15 varieties of caramel apples and other products that are prepared in individual stores, as well as provides ice cream, coffee, and other sundries. As of March 31, 2016, it had 3 company-owned, 96 licensee-owned, and 272 franchised Rocky Mountain Chocolate Factory stores in 40 states, Canada, Japan, South Korea, the Philippines, the Kingdom of Saudi Arabia, and the United Arab Emirates. Rocky Mountain Chocolate Factory, Inc. also operated 8 company-owned cafés and 210 franchised cafés in 38 states, Canada, Turkey, and the United Arab Emirates, as well as operated self-serve frozen yogurt cafés under the U-Swirl, Yogurtini, CherryBerry, Josies Frozen Yogurt, Yogli Mogli Frozen Yogurt, Fuzzy Peach Frozen Yogurt, Lets Yo!, and Aspen Leaf Yogurt brands. Rocky Mountain Chocolate Factory Inc. was founded in 1981 and is headquartered in Durango, Colorado.
