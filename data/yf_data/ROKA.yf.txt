Roka Bioscience, Inc., a molecular diagnostics company, focuses on the development and commercialization of molecular assay technologies for the detection of foodborne pathogens in the United States. It provides Atlas instrument, a fully automated molecular diagnostic testing instrument that eliminates the need for batch processing and automates all aspects of molecular diagnostic testing on a single and integrated platform. The company also offers atlas detection assays that include molecular tests for Salmonella, Listeria, E. coli O157:H7, Shiga toxin- producing E.coli, and Listeria monocytogenes, as well as related consumable supplies. It serves food processors, third-party contract testing laboratories, and government regulatory authorities. The company was founded in 2009 and is headquartered in Warren, New Jersey.
