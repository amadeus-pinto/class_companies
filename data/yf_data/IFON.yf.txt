InfoSonics Corporation, together with its subsidiaries, defines, sources, and sells wireless telecommunication products and accessories to wireless carriers, distributors, retailers, and OEMs primarily in Latin America, the Asia Pacific, Europe, Africa, and rest of the United States. It offers wireless handsets; tablets and accessories; and GSM Android-based smartphones and feature phones, and Android-based tablets. The company offers its products under the verykool brand name. InfoSonics Corporation was founded in 1994 and is headquartered in San Diego, California.
