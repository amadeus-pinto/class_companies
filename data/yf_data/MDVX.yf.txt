Medovex Corporation, through its subsidiaries, focuses on developing medical devices primarily in the in the United States and Europe. It offers DenerveX device for the treatment of conditions resulting from the degeneration of joints in the spine, which cause back pain; and IV suspension system that allows the transportation of IV poles secured to hospital beds, such as that one person could move a patient without separating the IV pole from the patient during transport. The company serves healthcare providers, physicians, and third-party payors. Medovex Corporation is headquartered in Atlanta, Georgia.
