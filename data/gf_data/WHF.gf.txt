Whitefield Limited is an investment company. The Company is engaged in investing in companies and trusts. The Company invests in shares and securities listed on the Australian Stock Exchange. The Company's investment objective is to provide investors with capital growth and franked income from investment in a diversified portfolio of industrial shares listed on the Australian Stock Exchange, and an investment return exceeding the ASX200 Industrials Accumulation Index over the long term. The Company invests in sectors, such as utilities, banks, consumer discretionary, consumer staple, healthcare, information technology, materials, non-bank financials, property, industrials and telecoms. The Company provides shareholders with exposure to a diversified portfolio of listed Australian industrial shares.
