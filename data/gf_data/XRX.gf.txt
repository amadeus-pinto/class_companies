Xerox Corporation is engaged in imaging, business process, analytics, automation and user-centric insights. The Company's segments include Services, Document Technology and Other. The Company's Services segment includes service offerings, such as BPO and Document Outsourcing (DO). It provides business services in markets across various industries and to government agencies. The Company's Document Technology segment includes the sale of products and supplies, as well as the associated technical service and financing of those products. The product groups offered under this segment are entry, mid-range and high-end products. Other segment includes paper sales in its developing market countries, wide-format systems, licensing revenue, global imaging systems network integration solutions and non-allocated corporate items, including other expenses and net. Its DO offers services, such as managed print services (MPS) include workflow automation and centralized print services (CPS).
