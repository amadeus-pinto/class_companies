AOL Inc. (AOL) is a global media and technology company with a worldwide audience and a suite of digital brands, products and services that it offers to consumers, advertisers, publishers and subscribers. The Company has three reportable segments: The Brand Group, The Membership Group and AOL Platforms. AOL Properties include the owned and operated content, products and services in the Membership Group and Brand Group segments. AOL Properties also include co-branded websites owned or operated by third parties for which certain criteria have been met. The Company&#8217;s AOL subscription service is a distribution channel for AOL Properties. Through AOL Platforms, the Company provides third party publishers. Its AOL Properties with products and services, such as premium formats, video advertising and content personalization, makes their websites and applications to sell advertising inventory.
