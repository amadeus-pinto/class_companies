Tatts Group Limited is engaged in the operation of regulated lotteries, conducting wagering and sports betting, conducting game machine monitoring and supply of jackpot, and provision of third party installation and maintenance services for gaming, wagering, lottery and other transactional equipment and systems. The Company's segments include Lotteries, which includes the operation of lottery licenses, the operation of a Lottery Operator Agreement in Queensland, South Australia, and an agreement in Northern Territory to conduct lotteries; Wagering, which includes totalisator and fixed odds betting on thoroughbred, harness, greyhounds and other sporting events; Maxgaming, which includes gaming machine monitoring and services in Queensland, New South Wales and the Northern Territory, and Bytecraft Systems, which includes warehousing, installation, relocation and maintenance of gaming machines, lottery and wagering terminals, and other transaction devices in Australia and New Zealand.
