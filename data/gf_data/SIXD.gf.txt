6D Global Technologies, Inc. is a digital business solutions company. The Company is engaged in serving the digital marketing and technology needs of organizations. It operates in two segments: Content Management Systems (CMS) and Information Technology Staffing (IT Staffing). Its CMS offers Web content management solutions, marketing cloud solutions, mobile applications, analytics, front-end user experience and design, and marketing automation. The IT Staffing segment provides contract and contract-to-hire IT professional staffing services. Its digital technology services include consulting, Web experience and content management, creative interfaces, mobile solutions, managed marketing services, and IT infrastructure staffing. It focuses on providing business-to-business (B2B) and business-to-business-to-consumer (B2B2C) solutions that addresses digital operations. Its portfolio of service offerings include 6D Web Experience, 6D Analytics, 6D Creative and IT Infrastructure Staffing.
