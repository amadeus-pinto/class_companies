Skipton Building Society Charitable Foundation is a charitable foundation. The Charitable Foundation services to registered charities based in the United Kingdom. The Charitable Foundation supports registered charities with donations to enable various tangible items or activities to be purchased rather than donating to general funds. The Charitable Foundation supports charities registered with the Charity Commission with over one year of published accounts covering a 12 month period. It considers donations from various charities, whose charities' objectives are to benefit children, through education and/or welfare, youth schemes and projects, or the elderly and their care.
