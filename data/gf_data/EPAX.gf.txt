Ambassadors Group, Inc. is a provider of educational travel experiences primarily engaged in organizing and promoting worldwide educational travel programs for students through a direct to consumer marketing model. The Company operates student and adult travel programs using the People to People International brand. The Company operates educational travel programs under three categories: Student Ambassador Programs, which provides educational opportunities for students to visit one or more foreign destinations to learn about its history, government, economy and culture; Leadership Ambassador Programs, which provides domestic destination travel programs for students through its People to People Leadership Summit and People to People World Leadership Forum programs, and Citizen Ambassador Programs, which provides professional travel programs for adults to travel abroad to meet and exchange ideas with foreign citizens who have similar backgrounds, interests or professions.
