OTI Greentech AG is a Germany-based provider of engineering services, project management and a wide range of product and technology solutions to the maritime, infrastructure, and oil and gas industries. It focuses on solutions in the area of cleaning, recovery and disposal of tanks, ships and industrial machinery, as well as for oil recovery from sludge and soil remediation. The Company's subsidiaries include VTT Maritime AS, a provider of engineering, technical inspection services and project management services for maritime operations, as well as RADA Engineering & Consulting AS, a provider of engineering services for infrastructure projects. The Company is present in Europe, the United States and Africa.
