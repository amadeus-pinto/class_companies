Kansas City Life Insurance Company offers individual life, annuity and group products and variable life, variable annuities, mutual funds and other investment options. The Company offers interest sensitive products that include an array of universal life, variable universal life, fixed deferred annuities, and variable annuities. The Company offers multiple fixed deferred annuity products. It also offers variable annuity products, which allow the policyholder options that include either single or flexible contracts combined with the advantages of a range of investment options and the advantages of an annuity. It offers traditional immediate annuity products with a range of payout options, including guaranteed specified amounts and life contingencies. The Company offers a range of immediate annuity options, in association with policy and contract holder benefit options, as well as new product sales.
