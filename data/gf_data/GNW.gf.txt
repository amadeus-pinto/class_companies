Genworth Financial, Inc. provides mortgage insurance products that allow people to purchase homes. The Company offers individual and group long-term care insurance products to meet consumer needs for long-term care. Its segments include U.S. Mortgage Insurance, Canada Mortgage Insurance, Australia Mortgage Insurance, U.S. Life Insurance, Runoff, and Corporate and Other. In the United States, the Company offers mortgage insurance products predominantly insuring prime-based, individually underwritten residential mortgage loans (flow mortgage insurance). The Canada Mortgage Insurance segment offers flow mortgage insurance and also provides bulk mortgage insurance. In Australia, the Company offers flow mortgage insurance and provides bulk mortgage insurance. It offers long-term care insurance products. The Runoff segment's non-strategic products include its variable annuity, variable life insurance, corporate-owned life insurance, and other accident and health insurance products.
