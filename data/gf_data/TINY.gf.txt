Sprout Tiny Homes, Inc. is engaged in acquiring businesses, which are engaged in designing, developing and manufacturing affordable housing for individual purchase or tiny/eco home real estate developments. The Company is also engaged in developing its own real estate development projects of sustainable living communities. The Company utilizes a streamlined manufacturing facility and a network of design partners and vendors to furnish and equip the homes with features for sustainable lifestyles and living in smaller homes. The Company designs homes ranging from 135 square feet to over 500 square feet. The Company manufactures two types of homes: Tiny homes ranging from 135 square feet up to 300 square feet, and Eco or modular manufactured homes range from 450 square feet to over 900 square feet. The Company provides consulting and custom design services to customers that engage the Company to build homes in its 14,000 square feet manufacturing facility.
