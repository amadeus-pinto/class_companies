Immobiliere Parisienne de la Perle et des Pierres Precieuses SA is a France-based holding company engaged in real estate operations. As of December 31, 2008, SAIP owns 99% of SNC Croix des Petits Champs, a company which owns a building in Rue Croix des Petits Champs in Paris, and 72% of Villa Pradon SA, a company which owns properties based in Madagascar. As of December 31, 2008, the Company is 96.62%-owned by Electricite et Eaux de Madagascar.
