Daegis Inc. is an enterprise software company that offers its products for information governance, application migration, data management and application development. The Company sells its solutions through three product lines: information governance, development tools and migration. Its information governance products manage the cost of information governance and e-Discovery. Its products include Daegis AXS-One and Daegis Edge, information governance solutions; Team Developer, TD Mobile, NXJ, ACCELL and VISION, application development products; SQLBase, DataServer and Report Builder, database management products, and Composer CipherSoft and Composer Notes, migration solutions. The Company's development tools products include mobile, Web and .NET application development, data management and application modernization software, and application development software. Its migration products offer automated software and services for migrating Lotus Notes and Oracle Forms applications.
