MGC Diagnostics Corporation is a medical technology company, which offers cardiorespiratory health solutions. The Company designs, markets and sells non-invasive cardiorespiratory diagnostic products. It sells its products internationally through distributors and in the United States through a direct sales force targeting specialists located in hospitals, university-based medical centers, medical clinics, physician offices, pharmaceutical companies, medical device manufacturers and clinical research organizations (CROs). Its cardiorespiratory diagnostic products measure flow and respiratory pressures and, in most cases analyze the inhaled and exhaled gases, such as oxygen and carbon dioxide. Its products include pulmonary function (PFT) and gas exchange (GX) testing products. The Company's pulmonary function products are divided into four product categories: Spirometry, Complete Pulmonary Function, Body Plethysmography and Specialty Products.
