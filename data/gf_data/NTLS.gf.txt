NTELOS Holdings Corp. is a regional provider of digital wireless communications services to consumers and businesses primarily in Virginia and West Virginia. The Company offers wireless voice and digital data personal communications services (PCS) products and services to retail and business customers under the NTELOS Wireless and FRAWG Wireless brand names. The Company conducts its business through NTELOS-branded retail operations, which sells its products and services via direct and indirect distribution channels, and provide network access to other telecommunications carriers. The Company conducts all of its business through its subsidiary NTELOS Inc. and its subsidiaries. The Company operates a code-division multiple access (CDMA) digital PCS network. The Company utilizes radio spectrum licensed by the Federal Communications Commission (FCC) to provide wireless broadband mobile services to its customers. The Company has approximately 1,008 cell sites in operation.
