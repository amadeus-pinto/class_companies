Stock Spirits Group PLC is a United Kingdom-based spirits company. The Company, together with its subsidiaries, is engaged in the production and distribution of branded spirits in Central and Eastern Europe. Its operating segments include Poland, Czech Republic, Italy, Other Operational and Corporate. The Other Operational segment consists of the results of operations of the Slovakian, International and Baltic Distillery entities. The Company offers spirits, vodka, vodka-based liqueurs, brandy, whisky, herbal bitters, rum, limoncello and others. It has operations in Poland, Czech Republic, Italy, Slovakia, Croatia, and Bosnia and Herzegovina. It has over 40 brands and exports its products internationally to approximately 40 countries across the world. The Company markets its products primarily under the brands, including Zoladkowa de Luxe, Stock Prestige, 1906, Bozkov, Stock Original, Amundsen, Keglevich, Imperator, Fernet Stock Citrus, Zoladkowa Gorzka, Stock 84 and others.
