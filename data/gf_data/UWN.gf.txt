Nevada Gold & Casinos, Inc. is a gaming company involved in financing, developing, owning and operating gaming properties and projects. The Company's segments include Washington, South Dakota, Nevada and Corporate. The Company operated a portfolio of nine mini-casinos in Washington State (Washington), which include restaurants, bars and approximately 126 table games, at April 30, 2016. The Company owned A.G. Trucano, Son and Grandsons, Inc. (South Dakota), a slot machine route operation in Deadwood, South Dakota consisting of over 705 slot machines in 16 locations, at April 30, 2016. It also owns Club Fortune Casino in Henderson, Nevada. As of April 30, 2016, it had approximately 540 slot machines, seven table games and a poker room, two bars, an entertainment lounge, a William Hill sports book, a cafe, a snack bar, and a gift shop. Its gaming facility operations are located in the United States, specifically in the states of Nevada, Washington and South Dakota.
