Resource America, Inc. is an asset management company that specializes in real estate and credit investments. The Company evaluates, originates, services and manages investment opportunities through its real estate, financial fund management and commercial finance subsidiaries as well as its joint ventures. The Company operates through Real Estate, Financial Fund Management and Commercial Finance segments. The Company's real estate segment is focused on acquiring and managing a portfolio of commercial real estate and real estate related debt, as well as value-added multifamily investments. Its financial fund management segment focuses on the sponsorship and management of collateralized loan obligations (CLOs), and the management of legacy Collateralized Debt Obligations (CDOs). The Company conducts its commercial finance operations through LEAF Commercial Capital, Inc. (LEAF) and LEAF Financial Corporation (LEAF Financial). It maintains an investment in the funds it sponsors.
