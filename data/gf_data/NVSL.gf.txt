Naugatuck Valley Financial Corporation is a stock savings bank holding company. The Company is primarily engaged in the business of planning, directing and coordinating the business activities of its wholly owned subsidiary bank, Naugatuck Valley Savings and Loan (Naugatuck Valley Savings or the Bank). Naugatuck Valley Savings is a federally chartered stock savings association. The Bank operates as a community-oriented financial institution serving the financial services needs of consumers and businesses with a variety of deposit and lending products from its banking offices in the Greater Naugatuck Valley region of southwestern Connecticut. The Bank offers family, multi-family and commercial real estate, construction, commercial business and consumer loans. The Bank's loan portfolio consists of one-to-four family residential mortgage loans, multi-family and commercial real estate loans, construction loans, commercial business loans and consumer loans.
