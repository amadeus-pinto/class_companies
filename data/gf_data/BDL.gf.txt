Flanigan's Enterprises, Inc. operates as a chain of full-service restaurants and package liquor stores. The Company operates through two segments: the restaurant segment and the package liquor store segment. The operation of package stores consists of retail liquor sales and related items. The Company's restaurants offer alcoholic beverages and full food service. The Company operates its package liquor stores and restaurants under two service marks: Big Daddy's Liquors and Flanigan's Seafood Bar and Grill. As of October 1, 2016, it operated 25 units consisting of restaurants, package liquor stores and combination restaurants/package liquor stores, and franchised an additional five units. It also owns an adult entertainment club. As of October 1, 2016, the Company owned and operated 10 restaurants under its service mark, Flanigan's Seafood Bar and Grill. As of October 1, 2016, the Company owned and operated nine package liquor stores in the South Florida area.
