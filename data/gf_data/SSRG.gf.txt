Symmetry Surgical Inc. is a global marketer and distributor of medical devices focused on the general surgery market. The Company offers over 20,000 products and sells to hospitals and surgical centers in the United States and countries around the world. The Company's Symmetry Surgical Branded Products include products with Symmetry Surgical-owned brands sold in the United States and worldwide. This includes surgical instruments, such as retractor instruments/systems, electro-surgery instruments, ligation clips and appliers, containers and sterilization devices and general and specialty instruments. Its Alliance Partners Products include complementary products offered by other manufacturers, which Symmetry Surgical distributes, primarily in markets within the United States. This includes complementary general surgery instruments used in surgical lighting, laparoscopic surgery and accessories.
