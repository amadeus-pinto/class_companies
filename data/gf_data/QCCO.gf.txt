QC Holdings, Inc. is a provider of financial solutions for mainstream consumers. The Company's business is to help customers meet their immediate financial needs between paychecks by providing short-term loans, which is also known as payday advances, deferred deposits and cash advances. In addition to providing payday advances, its locations offer other services, such as check cashing and wire transfers. Its locations also offer debit cards and bill payment services. It offers a range of consumer-directed products and services, including longer term installment loans, automotive sales and financing through its AutoStart USA subsidiary, and small business loans through its QC Capital Solutions unit. The Company, through Direct Credit Holdings, owns Credilogic, a provider of online loans to Canadian consumers. It provides credit solutions and other products and services to consumers and small business owners. The Company also provides loans and other specialty financial services.
