Nighthawk Energy PLC is an exploration and production company. The Company, through its subsidiaries, is focused principally on the exploration and development of oil producing assets located in and around Lincoln County, Colorado, the United States. The Company is engaged in the production of, exploration for and investment in hydrocarbons in a geographical area, the United States. The Company's two primary projects, Smoky Hill and Jolly Ranch, consist of approximately 300,000 gross acres within the southeast flank of the Denver-Julesburg Basin. The Company's subsidiaries include Nighthawk Royalties LLC, Nighthawk Production LLC and OilQuest USA LLC, which are engaged in oil and gas development.
