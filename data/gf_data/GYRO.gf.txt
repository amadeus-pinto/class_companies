Gyro International provides integrated marketing communications and creative services, primarily for business-to-business clients. It offers strategy development, digital advertising, media research and planning, interactive marketing, and public relations services. The company's roster of clients includes such big-name brands as Audi , Hewlett-Packard , John Deere , and Virgin Atlantic . Gyro operates from more than 15 offices in some 10 countries, including France, Germany, the Netherlands, Spain, the UK, and the US. The current iteration of the business was formed in 2009, through the merger of Cincinnati based-HSR Business to Business, Inc and Gyro International, headquartered in London.
