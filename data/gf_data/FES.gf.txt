Flint Energy Services Ltd. (Flint) is engaged in providing upstream and midstream construction, transportation, production and maintenance services to the on-shore oil and gas industry in Canada and the United States through approximately 82 centers in the oil and gas producing regions of Western Canada (Alberta, British Columbia, Manitoba, Newfoundland, the Northwest Territories and Saskatchewan), and in the continental United States, (Oklahoma, Texas, Louisiana, North Dakota, New Mexico, Colorado, Utah, Wyoming, North Dakota, Pennsylvania and West Virginia). The Company operates in four segments: production Services, Facility Infrastructure, Oilfield Services and Maintenance Services. On October 31, 2011, it acquired further 49% interest in Sub-One Technology Inc. On May 12, 2011, the Company sold Entrec Transportation Services Ltd. On May 14, 202, URS Corporation completed the acquisition of Flint, which has become the new Oil & Gas division of URS Corporation.
