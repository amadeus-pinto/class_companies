LookSmart, Ltd. is a digital advertising solutions company that provides relevant solutions for search and display advertising customers. The Company's offerings are organized into five lines if businesses: Clickable, LookSmart AdCenter, Novatech.io, ShopWiki and Web searches. The Company in partnership with Conversion Media Holdings, LLC, also supports the other lines of business through the creation of content sites directed at e-commerce verticals. The Clickable business enable brands and agencies measure marketing return on investment (ROI) through a customer's lifetime by connecting marketing and advertising products and services into one platform that gives customers the ability to analyze, publish, moderate, social media and search marketing. The Company has developed a Web-based advertising auction platform, the AdCenter that allows creating, tracking, analyzing, reporting and optimizing customers' advertising campaigns. ShopWiki is a consumer shopping search engine.
