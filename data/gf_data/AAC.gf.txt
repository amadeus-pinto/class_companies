Australian Agricultural Company Limited is an Australia-based cattle and beef producer. The principal activities of the Company are ownership, operation and development of pastoral properties; production of beef, including breeding, backgrounding, feedlotting and processing of cattle, and sales and marketing of branded beef. The Company specializes in grassfed beef and grainfed beef production. It operates an integrated cattle production system across approximately 20 owned cattle stations, over two leased stations, approximately 10 agisted properties, over two owned feedlots, a beef processing facility and over two owned and an external farms located throughout Queensland, New South Wales, the Northern Territory and Western Australia. The Company runs herd of Wagyu cattle, producing Wagyu beef, which is exported to over 20 countries around the world. Its Northern Beef Processing Facility is located at Livingstone Valley.
