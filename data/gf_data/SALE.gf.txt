RetailMeNot Inc. operates a savings destination connecting consumers with retailers, restaurants and brands, both online and in-store. The Company operates through two segments: Core and Gift Card. The Core segment consists of all other products and services that are related to its marketplace for digital offers. The Gift Card segment consists of its marketplace for gift cards. The Company's Websites offer its consumers digital offers from retailers and brands across multiple product categories. The Company's mobile applications allow consumers to shop when they want, where they want. The Company's e-mail newsletter allows consumers to stay informed about featured digital offers, while its alerts notify consumers when digital offers from their preferred retailers become available. The Company provides paid merchants access to a large marketplace dedicated to digital offers, offering a large and engaged audience of consumers with intent to purchase.
