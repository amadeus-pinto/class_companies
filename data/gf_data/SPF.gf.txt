Sopaf SpA is an Italy-based holding company engaged in investment activities. It specializes in provision of investment strategies in enterprises with growth opportunities. The investment portfolio includes investments in companies active in the industrial, real estate, financial services and insurance markets. The Company applies the business model based of Anglo-Saxon investment companies; it invests capital of its own and of third parties with the aim of generating returns above market average. Sopaf combines revenues from its own capital and the commission flow generated by the asset management subsidiaries in order to ensure stability to the revenue stream. It operates through numerous subsidiaries, including Sopaf Capital Management SGR SpA, Sopaf Asia Sarl, Sopaf Real Estate Opportunity I, SPF Clip Srl and Gnosis Finance Srl, among others.
