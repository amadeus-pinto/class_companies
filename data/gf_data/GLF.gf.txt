Gulf Industrials Limited is an Australia-based resource development company. The Company is engaged in mining and mineral exploration. It is an industrial mineral developer with exploration and development assets in Uganda and Madagascar. The Company operates through two segments: industrial minerals development and mining exploration. Its geographical locations include Australia and Africa. It operates Soalara Limestone Project in Madagascar. The Soalara Limestone Project is located on the coast in southwest Madagascar and consists of approximately two contiguous permits that encompass an area of over 18.75 square kilometers.
