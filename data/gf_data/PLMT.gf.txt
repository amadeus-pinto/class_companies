Palmetto Bancshares, Inc. is a bank holding company. The Company serves as the bank holding company for The Palmetto Bank (the Bank). Through the Company's retail, commercial and wealth management businesses, the Bank specializes in providing financial solutions to consumers and businesses with deposit and cash management products, loans (including consumer, Small Business Administration (SBA), commercial, corporate, mortgage, credit card and automobile), lines of credit, trust, brokerage, private banking, financial planning and insurance throughout its primary market area of nine counties located in northwest South Carolina, which includes the counties of Abbeville, Anderson, Cherokee, Greenville, Greenwood, Laurens, Oconee, Pickens and Spartanburg (commonly referred to as the Upstate). It is engaged in the business of commercial and retail banking through its approximately 25 branches in the Upstate.
