Vectura Group plc is engaged in research, development and commercialization of therapeutic products and drug delivery systems for human use. The Company's products include Ultibro Breezhaler (European Union (EU) and Rest of World (RoW)) - LABA-LAMA; Seebri Breezhaler (EU and RoW) - LAMA; AirFluSal Forspiro (EU and RoW) - ICS-LABA; ADVATE (Global) - Antihaemophilic Factor (Recombinant); Adept (Global) - Icodextrin; Anoro Ellipta (Global) - LAMA-LABA; Relvar Ellipta/Breo Ellipta (Global) - ICS-LABA, and Incruse Ellipta (Global) - LAMA. Its product pipeline includes VR588, VR475, VR647, VR942, VR179, VR736, VR096, VR876, VR315, VR506, VR632, Seebri Neohaler and VR465. Its dry powder inhalers (DPI) include GyroHaler, Lever-operated, Open-inhale-close and Unit Dose DPI. Its smart nebuliser delivery systems provide targeted inhalation therapy for applications where precise and targeted delivery of a drug to the lungs is needed. Its smart nebulizer delivery systems include AKITA JET and FOX.
