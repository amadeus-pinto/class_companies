Bellicum Pharmaceuticals, Inc. is a clinical-stage biopharmaceutical company. The Company is focused on discovering and developing cellular immunotherapies for various forms of cancer, including hematological cancers and solid tumors, as well as orphan inherited blood disorders. The Company uses its chemical induction of dimerization (CID) technology platform to engineer and then control components of the immune system. The Company is developing next-generation product candidates in the areas of cellular immunotherapy, including hematopoietic stem cell transplantation (HSCT), chimeric antigen receptors (CAR) T cells therapy and T-cell receptor (TCR) cell therapies. The Company's product candidates include BPX-501, BPX-601 and BPX-701. The Company's CID-based technologies include CaspaCIDe and GoCAR-T. It is evaluating BPX-501 in various Phase I/II clinical trials in adults and pediatric patients with leukemias, lymphomas and genetic blood diseases in the United States and Europe.
