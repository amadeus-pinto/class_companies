Procera Networks, Inc. is a provider of subscriber experience assurance (SEA) solutions designed for network operators around the world. The Company's PacketLogic solutions enable network operators to gain insights, make decisions, and take actions to ensure a quality experience for Internet connected devices. Network operators deploy its technology to gain insights on their subscribers and networks, make decisions on service packaging and then deliver those solutions to subscribers connected to their network. Its network operator customers include service provider customers and enterprises. Its service provider customers include mobile service providers and broadband service providers, which include cable multiple system operators (MSOs), telecommunications companies, Wi-Fi network operators, and Internet Service Providers (ISPs). Its enterprise customers include educational institutions, commercial enterprises and government and municipal agencies.
