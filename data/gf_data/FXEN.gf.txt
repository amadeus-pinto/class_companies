FX Energy, Inc. is an independent oil and gas exploration and production company with production, development, and exploration activities in Poland. The Company also has oil production and oilfield service activities in the United States. Its two operating segments include exploration and production (E&P) segment in Poland and the United States, and the oilfield services segment in the United States. It holds oil and gas exploration rights in Poland in various separately designated project areas encompassing approximately 1.8 million gross acres. It holds interests in approximately 1.3 million net acres throughout Poland. Its activities in Poland are conducted in various project areas: Fences, Edge and Warsaw South. Its drilling activities are focused primarily on the core Fences area. The Fences concession area encompasses 853,000 gross acres (3,450 square kilometers) in western Poland's Permian Basin.
