River Valley Bancorp (River Valley) is a bank holding company. The Company, through its subsidiaries, engages in banking services. The activities of the holding Company is to own the stock of River Valley Financial Bank (River Valley Financial or the Bank). The Bank conducts operations from its 14 full-service office locations in southeastern Indiana and northern Kentucky. The Bank's primary market areas are Jefferson, Floyd and Clark Counties in southeastern Indiana and adjacent Carroll and Trimble Counties in Kentucky. The Bank provides loans, such as mortgage loans for the purchase, construction, or refinancing of one-to-four family residential real property. The Bank also offers multi-family mortgage loans, nonresidential real estate loans, land loans, construction loans, non-mortgage commercial loans and consumer loans.
