Crown Media Holdings, Inc. owns and operates two pay television networks Hallmark Channel and Hallmark Movies and Mysteries. The Company operates in Network segment. Through its subsidiary, Crown Media United States, the Company owns, operates and distributes the Networks. The Company's Networks offer a range of entertainment programming for adults and families, including original series, lifestyle programming, television series, movies, miniseries, theatricals, romances, literary classics, television specials and contemporary stories. The Company's Hallmark Channel features television series, such as Golden Girls, The Middle and Frasier, as well as original series, such as Good Witch and When Calls the Heart. It also features lifestyle programming, such as Home and Family. The Company's Hallmark Movies and Mysteries focuses on broadcasting programming of the mystery genre, including original movies, movie franchises and series.
