Cover-All Technologies Inc., is an insurance solutions company. The Company provides business-focused solutions to the property and casualty insurance industry. The Company provides technology solutions and services designed to enable customers to introduce new products and expand distribution channels to their customers. The Company&#8217;s software products and services focus on the functions required to underwrite, rate, quote, issue, print, bill and support lifecycle of insurance policies. The Company&#8217;s products platforms include: Cover-All Policy, Cover-All Business Intelligence, and Cover-All Claims. Cover-All Policy, is a web-based, data-centric software platform pre-built for commercial insurance products. Cover-All Business Intelligence, provides access to information with respect to pricing, risk selection and service. Cover-All Claims, software solution designed to provide support for Claims processing using technology.
