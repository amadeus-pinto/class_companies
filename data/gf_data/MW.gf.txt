The Men's Wearhouse, Inc. is a holding-company. The Company operates as a specialty retailer of men's suits and a provider of tuxedo rental product in the United States and Canada. It operates in two segments: retail, which offers its products and services through its over four retail merchandising brands and Internet Websites. The Company&#8217;s corporate apparel segment provides corporate clothing uniforms and work wear to workforces. It operates approximately 1,760 retail stores, with over 1,635 stores in the United States and Puerto Rico, as well as over 120 stores in Canada. The Company operates its retail stores in the United States, the District of Columbia and Puerto Rico, under the brand names of Men's Wearhouse (over 700 stores), Men's Wearhouse and Tux (over 210 stores), Jos. A. Bank (over 640 stores) and K&G (over 90 stores). Its Canadian stores are operated under the brand name of Moores (over 120 stores) in approximately 10 provinces.
