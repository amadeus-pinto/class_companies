Emmis Communications Corp., a diversified media company, engages in radio broadcasting activities in the United States. The company operates in three segments: Radio, Publishing, and Corporate &amp; Emerging Technologies. It operates 19 FM and 4 AM radio stations in New York, Los Angeles, St. Louis, Austin, Indianapolis, and Terre Haute, Indiana. The company also publishes various city and regional magazines, which include Texas Monthly, Los Angeles, Atlanta, Indianapolis Monthly, Cincinnati, and Orange Coast. In addition, it develops and licenses TagStation, a cloud-based software platform that allows a broadcaster to manage album art, meta data, and enhanced advertising on its various broadcasts; and develops NextRadio, a smartphone application that marries over-the-air FM radio broadcasts with visual and interactive features on smartphones; and engages in dynamic pricing business. Emmis Communications Corp. was founded in 1981 and is based in Indianapolis, Indiana.
