Gaia, Inc. operates a digital video subscription service and on-line community worldwide. It provides a digital subscription video streaming service that allows subscribers to have unlimited access to a library of inspiring films, personal growth related content, cutting edge documentaries, interviews, yoga classes, transformation related content, and other under the Gaia brand. The company was formerly known as Gaiam, Inc. and changes its name to Gaia, Inc. in July 2016. Gaia, Inc. was founded in 1988 and is headquartered in Louisville, Colorado.
