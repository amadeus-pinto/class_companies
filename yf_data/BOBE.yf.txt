Bob Evans Farms, Inc. produces and distributes food products for grocery retailers in the United States. It offers pork sausage and bacon products, as well as complementary refrigerated and frozen convenience foods, such as mashed potatoes, Oven Bake side dishes, macaroni, cheese, and handheld breakfast items. The company offers its products under the under the Bob Evans, Owens, and Country Creek brand names. It also manufactures and sells products for restaurants and food sellers. Bob Evans Farms, Inc. was founded in 1948 and is based in New Albany, Ohio.
