Hudson Global, Inc. provides professional-level recruitment and related talent solutions for small to large-sized corporations and government agencies worldwide. The company offers permanent recruitment services; and contracting services, such as project management, interim management, and professional contract staffing services. It also offers permanent and contracting outsourced recruitment solutions, such as complete recruitment outsourcing, project-based outsourcing, contingent workforce, and recruitment consulting services to multinational companies; and talent management solutions, including talent assessment, interview training, executive coaching, employee development, and outplacement. The company was formerly known as Hudson Highland Group, Inc. and changed its name to Hudson Global, Inc. in April 2012. Hudson Global, Inc. was founded in 2003 and is based in New York, New York.
