State Bank Financial Corporation operates as the bank holding company for State Bank and Trust Company that provides various community banking services to individuals and businesses. It offers a range of deposit products and services, such as checking, commercial operating, savings and money market, individual retirement, and time deposit accounts, as well as short-term to longer-term certificates of deposits. The company also provides residential and commercial construction and development, commercial real estate, farmland and agricultural production, residential mortgage, home equity, and consumer loans, as well as loans to small and medium-sized businesses. As of December 31, 2016, it operated 31 full-service banking offices in the counties of Georgia: Bibb, Chatham, Clarke, Cobb, Columbia, Dooly, Fulton, Gwinnett, Hall, Houston, Jones, Liberty, McDuffie, Richmond, and Tattnall; and 8 mortgage production offices. State Bank Financial Corporation was founded in 2005 and is headquartered in Atlanta, Georgia.
