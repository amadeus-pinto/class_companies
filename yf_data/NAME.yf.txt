Rightside Group, Ltd. provides domain name registration and related value-added services to resellers and domain name registrants in the United States, Ireland, Canada, Australia, and the Cayman Islands. The companys services enable businesses and consumers to find, establish, and maintain their digital address-the starting point for connecting with their online audience. It offers domain name registration services under the Name.com brand name. The company also develops proprietary tools and services to identify, acquire, monetize, and sell domain names for its own portfolio of names, as well as for customers domain names. Rightside Group, Ltd. was incorporated in 2013 and is headquartered in Kirkland, Washington.
