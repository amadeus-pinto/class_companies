Zoe&#x27;s Kitchen, Inc., through its subsidiaries, develops and operates a chain of fast-casual restaurants. It operates a range of restaurant formats, including in-line, end-cap, and free-standing restaurants. As of February 23, 2017, the company operated 207 owned and franchised restaurants in 20 states of the United States. Zoe&#x27;s Kitchen, Inc. was founded in 1995 and is based in Plano, Texas.
