Fidelity &amp; Guaranty Life, through its subsidiaries, provides annuities and life insurance products in the United States. It offers fixed indexed annuities, fixed rate annuities, single premium immediate annuities, and indexed universal life insurance policies. The company distributes its products to various age groups in the middle-income market through independent agents, managing general agents, and specialty brokerage firms, as well as to selected institutional markets. Fidelity &amp; Guaranty Life was formerly known as Harbinger F&amp;G, LLC and changed its name to Fidelity &amp; Guaranty Life in August 2013. The company was founded in 1959 and is headquartered in Des Moines, Iowa. Fidelity &amp; Guaranty Life operates as a subsidiary of FS Holdco II Ltd.
