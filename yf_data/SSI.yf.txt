Stage Stores, Inc. operates specialty department stores primarily in small and mid-sized towns and communities in the United States. Its merchandise portfolio comprises moderately priced brand name and private label apparel, accessories, cosmetics, footwear, and home goods. The company also offers merchandise direct-to-consumer through its e-commerce Website and send program. As of March 30, 2017, it operated 798 specialty department stores in 38 states and a direct-to-consumer channel under the BEALLS, GOODY&#x27;S, PALAIS ROYAL, PEEBLES, and STAGE nameplates. The company was founded in 1988 and is based in Houston, Texas.
