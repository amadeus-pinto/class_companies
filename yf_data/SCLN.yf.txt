SciClone Pharmaceuticals, Inc., a specialty pharmaceutical company, provides therapies for oncology, infectious diseases, and cardiovascular disorders in the PeopleÂ’s Republic of China, the United States, and Hong Kong. Its lead product is ZADAXIN, which is used for the treatment of hepatitis B and hepatitis C viruses, and certain cancers, as well as for use as an immune system enhancer. In addition, the company focuses on developing ZADAXIN for the treatment of sepsis. SciClone Pharmaceuticals, Inc. markets and sells ZADAXIN through distribution arrangements, local importers, and distributors. It also markets and distributes products, such as DC Bead, Holoxan, Mesna, Endoxan, Methotrexate, Estracyt, Farlutal, Neucardin, Angiomax, and Cleviprex. The company has a strategic partnership with The Medicines Company to promote Angiomax (bivalirudin) and Cleviprex (clevidipine). SciClone Pharmaceuticals, Inc. was founded in 1989 and is headquartered in Foster City, California.
