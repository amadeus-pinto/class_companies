State National Companies, Inc., through its subsidiaries, provides property and casualty insurance in the United States. It operates in two segments, Program Services and Lender Services. The Program Services segment acts as an issuing carrier for foreign and domestic insurance and reinsurance companies. This segment writes a range of insurance products, including general liability insurance, commercial liability insurance, commercial multi-peril insurance, property insurance, and workers compensation insurance. The Lender Services segment provides collateral protection insurance, which insures personal automobiles and other vehicles held as collateral for loans made by credit unions, banks, and specialty finance companies, as well as offers ancillary insurance products. The company was founded in 1973 and is headquartered in Bedford, Texas.
