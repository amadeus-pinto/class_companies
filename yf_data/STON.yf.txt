StoneMor Partners L.P., together with its subsidiaries, owns and operates cemeteries in the United States. It operates through two segments, Cemetery Operations and Funeral Homes. It also provides receptacles for cremated remains, including urns, and the inurnment of cremated remains in niches or scattering gardens; and funeral home services, such as consultation, the removal and preparation of remains, and the use of funeral home facilities for visitation and prayer services. As of February 29, 2016, the company owned and operated 307 cemeteries and 105 funeral homes in 28 states and Puerto Rico. StoneMor GP LLC serves as the general partner of StoneMor Partners L.P. The company was founded in 1999 and is headquartered in Levittown, Pennsylvania.
