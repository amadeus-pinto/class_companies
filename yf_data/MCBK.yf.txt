Madison County Financial, Inc., through its subsidiary, Madison County Bank, provides banking and financial services to individual and corporate customers in Nebraska, the United States. It offers deposits, such as interest-bearing and non-interest bearing checking accounts, money market savings accounts, and certificates and time deposits; and agricultural real estate loans, one-to four-family residential real estate loans, commercial and multi-family real estate loans, agricultural and commercial non-real estate loans, and automobile or recreation vehicle loans. The company was formerly known as Madison County Holding Company and changed its name to Madison County Financial, Inc. in October 2012. Madison County Financial, Inc. was founded in 1888 and is headquartered in Madison, Nebraska.
