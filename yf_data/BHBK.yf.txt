Blue Hills Bancorp, Inc. operates as the bank holding company for Blue Hills Bank that provides financial services to individuals, families, small to mid-size businesses, government, and non-profit organizations in Massachusetts. It accepts passbook and statement savings, money market, commercial and regular checking, as well as certificates of deposit and IRAs. The company also offers one- to four family residential mortgage, commercial real estate, commercial business, construction, and consumer loans, as well as home equity loans and lines of credit. In addition, it invests in securities; and provides cash management services. The company operates through a network of 11 full-service branch offices located in Boston, Dedham, Hyde Park, Milton, Nantucket, Norwood, West Roxbury, and Westwood, Massachusetts. Blue Hills Bancorp, Inc. was founded in 2008 and is headquartered in Norwood, Massachusetts.
