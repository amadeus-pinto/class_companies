Truett-Hurst, Inc. produces, markets, and sells wines primarily in the United States. The company operates through Wholesale and Direct to Consumer segments. It produces various varietals of wine products, including Pinot Noir, Chardonnay, Sauvignon Blanc, Zinfandel, Petite Sirah, Merlot, and Cabernet Sauvignon. The company offers its products primarily under the VML, Healdsburg Ranches, Colby Red, and Bradford Mountain brands directly through its tasting rooms, wine clubs, and winery Websites. Truett-Hurst, Inc. was founded in 2007 and is based in Healdsburg, California.
